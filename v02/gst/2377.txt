{#अनमित्र#}¦ Bahuvr. I. m. f. n. ({#-त्रः-त्रा-त्रम्#}) Free from enemies,

<div n="P">II. n. ({#-त्रम्#}) The being free from or without enemies.
<div n="P">III. m. ({#-त्रः#}) A proper name of <sup>1</sup> A prince of the solar
race, a descendant of Sagara, son of Nighna and brother
of Raghu, the fifty-second king of Ayodhyā. <sup>2</sup> A son of
the king Kroṣṭu or Kroṣtṛ by Gāndhāri and father of
Śini or, according to others, a grandson of Vṛṣṇi, son
of Sumitra by Mādrī and brother to Śini; or, again, a
grandson of Dhṛṣṭa, son of Sumitra &c. E. {#अ#} priv. and
{#अमित्र#}.
