{#अन्योन्याभाव#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-वः#}) (In the Vaiśeṣika philosophy.)
Mutual negation, one of the four divisions of {#अभाव#} q. v.;
viz. that kind of negation or mutual exclusion which is held
to exist between notions that have no property in common;
indifference considered as a negation of heterogeneous notions;
as if one says: ‘shape’ is not ‘jar’ ({#‘अन्योन्याभावत्वं तादात्म्यसं-#}
{#बन्धावच्छिन्नप्रतियोगिताकाभावत्वम्’#}). This notion is different
therefore from that of {#पृथक्त्व#} q. v. E. {#अन्योन्य#} and {#अभाव#}.
