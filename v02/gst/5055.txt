{#अपाकर्तोस्#}¦ Tatpur. (ved.) The same as {#अपाकर्तुम्#} of the
classical dialect, i. e. to drive away, to remove. E. The
genitive of an obsolete noun {#अपाकर्तु,#} the accus. of which
is alone used, as in all similar cases, in the sense of our
infinitive in the classical dialect; the native etym. expresses
this thus: {#कृ#} with {#आ#} and {#अप,#} kṛt aff. {#तोसुन्,#} to indicate
that the udātta accent is on {#अप#} i. e. on the first syllable.
