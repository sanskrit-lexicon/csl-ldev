{#अन्यतोवात#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-तः#}) (In Medicine.) A disease of the
whole eye ({#सर्वाक्षिरोग#}); symptoms and etymology are con-
veyed by the following verse: {#यस्यावटूकर्णशिरोहनुस्थो म-#}
{#न्यागतो वाप्यनिलोऽन्यतो वा । कुर्याद्रुजोऽति भ्रुवि लोचने वा#}
{#तमन्यतोवातमुदाहरन्ति#} ‘when the {%air%} of the back of the
neck, of ears, head, jaw, the tendons which form the nape
of the neck or {%elsewhere%} produces excessive pain in the eye-
brow and eye, they call this (disease of the eye) {#अन्यतो-#}
{#वात#}’. The word occurs also in its uncompounded state,
e. g. {#हताधिमन्थोऽनिलपर्यायश्च शुष्काक्षिपाकोऽन्यत एव#}
{#वातः#}. E. {#अन्यतस्#} and {#वात#}.
