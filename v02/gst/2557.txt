{#अनाधृष्टि#}¦ Bahuvr. m. ({#-ष्टिः#}) <sup>1</sup> The proper name of <sup>a.</sup> a prince,
the son of Śura by Bhojyā, or according to others by

Nirvṛttiśatru; <sup>b.</sup> a son of Ugrasena and chief of the Yā-

drāśva. E. {#अ#} priv. and {#आधृष्टि,#} ‘suffering no defeat, in-
vincible’.
