<L>475<pc>014-a<k1>अग्नीषोमप्रणयन<k2>अग्नीषोमप्रणयन
{#अग्नीषोमप्रणयन#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) Carrying forth the fire and
the Soma; a ceremony in the Jyotiṣṭoma sacrifice which
takes place after the Vaisarjana homa (q. v.) and consists
in taking the fire from the Prāchīnavaṇśa (q. v.) Manḍapa
and carrying it to the Āgnīdhra (q. v.) Manḍapa, and in
the same time taking the Soma from the Prāchīnavaṇśa
and carrying it to the Havirdhāna Manḍapa, a building
erected in the Saumikī Vedi (q. v.). See also {#अग्निप्रणयन#}.
E. {#अग्नीषोम#} and {#प्रणयन#}.
