{#अनिलव्याधि#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-धिः#}) (In Medicine.) Derangement
of the Rasa wind (see {#अनिल#} 1. f.), produced, according to
the native theory, by the continued use of dry, cold or light
articles of food, taken in unusually small quantities &c.
and causing, according to some authorities, eighty varieties
of nervous diseases. See also {#अनिलामय#} and {#वातव्याधि#}.
E. {#अनिल#} and {#व्याधि#}.
