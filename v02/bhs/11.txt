<div n="lb">({@1@}) n. of the fifth and highest class of the Śuddhāvāsakāyika
<div n="lb">gods (see {@deva@}), and ({@2@}) sg., n. of the region where they

<div n="lb">in the lists of classes of gods: Mvy 3106; Dharmas 128;
<div n="lb">LV 47.1; 150.11; 227.2; 266.8; 342.18; Mv i.266.3, 7;

<div n="lb">of the class, LV 44.13.
