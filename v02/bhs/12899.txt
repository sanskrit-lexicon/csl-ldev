<div n="lb">of a superior person (a cakravartin or a Buddha), of
<div n="lb">which there are standardly 32 (anomalously 28 in Gv
<div n="lb">353.7, aṣṭaviṃśatibhir mahāpuruṣalakṣaṇair upeto; no

<div n="lb">ii.17.10 ff. and iii.143.5 ff. (here followed by lengthy treat-
<div n="lb">ments of each item); MN ii.136.6 ff.; in BHS, LV 105.11 ff.

<div n="lb">(here referred to as LVa) and 429.3 ff. (LVb); Mvy 235 ff.;
<div n="lb">Dharmas (here abbreviated Dh) 83; Gv 399.20 ff., with
<div n="lb">valuable brief explanations of each term; Bbh 375.9 ff.,
