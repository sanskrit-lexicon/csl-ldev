<div n="lb">Mvy 8783, defined by Tib. smad ḥchal, which is reported
<div n="lb">to mean {%prostitution, dissoluteness%} (and Jap. {%one who%}
<div n="lb">{%frequents prostitutes%}); MSV iv.68.11. But Chin. {%one who%}
<div n="lb">{%is defective in the lower parts%}; and the surrounding terms in
<div n="lb">Mvy and MSV (khañja, khela, kāṇa, etc.) are all adjectives
<div n="lb">referring to physical deformities, thus supporting the Chin.
<div n="lb">The Tib. word smad means {%lower%}, and may be used of the
<div n="lb">lower limbs; ḥchol (ḥchal) ba may mean {%to be confused,%}
<div n="lb">{%deranged%}.
