of water for bath;<sup>1</sup> the dharma of the Yoga destroys sins;<sup>2</sup>
800 times in the head;<sup>3</sup> Dhāraṇa Agneyī a special form of
samādhi practised by Sati, the daughter of Dakṣa.<sup>4</sup>
