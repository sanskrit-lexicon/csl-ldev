{%Dāruka%} (I)¦ — the charioteer of Kṛṣṇa;<sup>1</sup> went with
Kṛṣṇa to Śālva's station, and drove the chariot when Kṛṣṇa
gained Lakṣmaṇā by svayamvara;<sup>2</sup> took Kṛṣṇa to Mithilā
and the Videha country.<sup>3</sup> His son was the charioteer of
Pradyumna.<sup>4</sup> Stood before Kṛṣṇa on the eve of His return-
ing from the earth. Kṛṣṇa asked him to inform the Dvārakā
people of the position of the Yādavas at Prabhāsa and advise
them to go to Indraprastha with Arjuna. Returning to the
city, Dāruka informed Vasudeva and others accordingly.<sup>5</sup>
