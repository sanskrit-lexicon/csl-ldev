{%Aśmaka%}¦ — a Kṣetraja son of Kalmāṣapāda (Mitrasaha-
{%Vi.%}) born of Madayantī, queen of Saudāsa, to Vasiṣṭha, with
the king's assent. The period of pregnancy lasted for seven
years when Vasiṣṭha bit the queen's stomach with a stone.
Father of Mūlaka.
