{%Sudaridra%}¦ — an old poor Brahman of Pāñcala deśa who
had the four cakravākas (in previous birth sons of
Kauśika) as his sons, all learned Brahmans. The sons left
for yoga giving a verse (on their previous births) to their
old father to show it to the king and get money and land.
Thus he did and got much wealth including many villages.
