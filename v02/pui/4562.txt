{%Caṇḍavega%}¦ — the Lord of the Gandharvas, who had 360
followers. These entered Puramjana's city and harassed
the people. The superintendent of the city defended it.
Allegorically 360 stands for the year, while the Gandharvas
represent the day time and their women the night.
