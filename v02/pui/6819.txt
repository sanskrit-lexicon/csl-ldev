{%Nagnajit%} (I)¦ — a King of Kosala, had a daughter Satyā;
established a convention that he who would curb the ferocity
of seven bulls in his possession would be the proper husband
for his daughter; all tried in vain; Kṛṣṇa went to Ayodhyā
and offered to pass the test; seeing his feat Nagnajit gave
[Page2-193+ 31]
him his daughter with a large dowry;<sup>1</sup> went to Syamanta-
pañcaka for the solar eclipse.<sup>2</sup>
