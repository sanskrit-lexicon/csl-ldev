{%Karṇa%} (I)¦ — born of Kuntī as a virgin and abandoned
by her; brought up by Adhiratha; became a king and father
of Vṛṣasena.<sup>1</sup> Pāṇḍavas defeated his army. Arjuna was
saved from Karṇa's arrows by Kṛṣṇa. Joined Duryodhana
in insulting Vidura, and was his evil adviser.<sup>2</sup> Resented
Sāmba's action in carrying away Lakṣmaṇā but vanquished
by Sāmba. In charge of gifts in Yudhiṣtira's {%Rājasūya.%}
Joined Duryodhana's army and succeeded Droṇa as com-
mander for two days, but was killed by Arjuna.<sup>3</sup> Failed to
hit the mark in the {%svayamvara%} of Lakṣmaṇā.<sup>4</sup> Identified
with Cakravarman, the son of Bala.<sup>5</sup> Was called son of Sūta
as he was brought up by Adhiratha the Sūta.<sup>6</sup>
