{%Śālmali(dvīpa)%} (I)¦ — one of the seven continents;
<div n="lb"/>Vapuṣmān the first king. Twice Plakṣa in size, surrounded
<div n="lb"/>by suroda (sea of sura): distinguished for its tree Śālmali:
<div n="lb"/>the abode of Garuḍa: Soma is worshipped here. Its king was
<div n="lb"/>Yajñabāhu, son of Priyavrata who divided the kingdom
<div n="lb"/>among his seven sons;<sup>1</sup> after their names — Śvetam, Haritam,
<div n="lb"/>Vaidyutam, Mānasam, Jimūtam, Rohitam, Suprabham, and
<div n="lb"/>Śobhanam; the four varṇas are Kapila, Aruṇa, Pīta and
<div n="lb"/>Kṛṣṇa; famous for seven hills and seven rivers: frequented
<div n="lb"/>by Garuḍa birds.<sup>2</sup>
