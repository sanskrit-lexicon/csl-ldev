{%Śālmali(dvīpa)%} (I)¦ — one of the seven continents;
Vapuṣmān the first king. Twice Plakṣa in size, surrounded
by suroda (sea of sura): distinguished for its tree Śālmali:
the abode of Garuḍa: Soma is worshipped here. Its king was
Yajñabāhu, son of Priyavrata who divided the kingdom
among his seven sons;<sup>1</sup> after their names — Śvetam, Haritam,
Vaidyutam, Mānasam, Jimūtam, Rohitam, Suprabham, and
Śobhanam; the four varṇas are Kapila, Aruṇa, Pīta and
Kṛṣṇa; famous for seven hills and seven rivers: frequented
by Garuḍa birds.<sup>2</sup>
