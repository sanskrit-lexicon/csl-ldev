{%Bṛhanmanas%} (II)¦ — a son of Bṛhadbhānu; had two wives
Yaśodevī and Satyā, both daughters of Śaibya, the Cedi
king; the first gave birth to Jayadratha and the latter to
Vijaya; Vijaya had a son Bṛhatputra whose son was Bṛhad-
ratha; he had a son Satyakarma who had a son Adhiratha
who was known to be Sūta; hence Karṇa is Sūta's son.
