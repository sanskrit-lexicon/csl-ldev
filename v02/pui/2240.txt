<L>2240<pc>1-280<k1>ऐक्ष्वाकु<k2>ऐक्ष्वाकु
{%Aikṣvākus%}¦ — Born in the family of Bṛhadbala and end-
ing with Sumitra. Twenty-seven kings contemporaries of ten
Śiśunāgas.<sup>1</sup> These are Bṛhadbala, Urukṣaya, Vatsadroha,
Prativyoma, Divākara, Sahadeva, Dhruvāśva, Pratīpāśva,
Supratīpa, Marudeva, Sunakṣatra, Kinnarāśva, Antarikṣa,
Suṣeṇa, Sumitra, Bṛhadrāja, Kṛtamjaya, Raṇejaya, Sañjaya,
Śākya, Suddhaudana, Siddhārtha, Prasenajit, Kṣudraka,
Kulaka, Suratha and Sumitra.<sup>2</sup>
<div n="P"/>Went with Devāpī to Kalāpagrāma: the future founders
of Kṣatra in the 29th Caturyuga.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) Br. III. 74. 104 & 136.</F>
<F>2) M. 271. 4-14.</F>
<F>3) M. 273. 56-7.</F>
