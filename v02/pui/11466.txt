{%Rathītara%} (II)¦ — a pupil of Satyaśṛṇga — also Śākhavaiṇa;
author of three {%Samhitas%} given to four pupils Paila and
others; also author of Nirukta — a Trayārṣeya, with Angiras
and Virūpa;<sup>1</sup> had four disciples, Ketava, Dalaki, Dhama-
śarma and Devaśarma; all of them became murderers of
Brahmanas on the death of their master; they approached
the creator for light who advised them to make a visit to
Vālukesvaram to get redeemed of their sins by bath and
prayer; on the completion of the pilgrimage they reached
Sūryamaṇḍalam.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1)  Br. II. 34. 32. 35. 3; M. 196. 38; Vā. 61. 2.</F>
<F>2) Ib. 60. 65-72.</F>
