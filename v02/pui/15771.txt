{%Sāndīpani%}¦ — (also Sāndīpana); of Kāśī, and a resident
of Avanti; was the preceptor of Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma. Taught
them all branches of learning in 64 days; got back his dead
son recovered from the world of Yama (from the sea {%Vi. P.%})
by his pupils as {%gurudakṣiṇa;%} acted as purohita in the {%yajñas%}
performed by Kṛṣṇa at Kurukṣetra;<sup>1</sup> his concern when
Kṛṣṇa and Kucela were caught once in storm and rain.<sup>2</sup>
