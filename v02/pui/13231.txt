{%Viśvarūpa%} (III)¦ — a son of Rūpavatī, and devoted to
Nārāyaṇa: elected Purohita in the absence of Bṛhaspati
who went to the earth due to a curse by sages: Indra
quarrelled with him, and the latter repaired to spend his life
in penance. Sages cursed Indra for this.
