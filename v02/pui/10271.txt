{%Malayadhvaja%}¦ — the Pāṇḍya who married the daughter
of the Vidarbha King Rājasimha and became the father of
a daughter and seven sons; a Rājaṛṣi; divided his kingdom
among his sons and with his mind set on Kṛṣṇa retired to
Kulācala followed by his queen; after a period of strenuous
{%tapas%} he died and the queen wailed.
<div n="P"/>Bhā. IV. 28. 29-30, 33-34, 36-50.
