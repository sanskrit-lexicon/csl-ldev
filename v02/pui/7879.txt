<L>7879<pc>2-327<k1>पिण्डा<k2>पिण्डा
{%Piṇḍās%}¦ — rice balls given on ceremonial occasions to
Pitṛs: three to be given; like the calf in search of the cow lost
in the stall, {%mantra%} takes them to the Pitṛs; to be given in the
name of the gotra of the person;<sup>1</sup> can be given to cows,
Brahmanas, females, crows, hen or thrown into fire or water;
the middle Piṇḍa can be eaten by the wife which leads to
increase of santānam;<sup>2</sup> Piṇḍadānam on the 12th day after
death is supposed to be the Pātheyam or food for the way
to heaven;<sup>3</sup> seven Piṇḍas for seven generations;<sup>4</sup> giving of,
in the {%tīrthas%} of the Narmadā;<sup>5</sup> at Gayā with tila.<sup>6</sup>
