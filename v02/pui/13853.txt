{%Vyoma%} (II)¦ — a son of Maya and follower of Kamsa,
came to Vraja in the guise of a Gopa, and mixed in a game
with Gopas. Removed a good number of them to a cave near-
by. Finding the numbers reduced, Kṛṣṇa spotted out the
mischievous foe and killed him on the spot.
