{%Upabarhaṇa%}¦ — the name of Nārada in a previous birth
born as a Gandharva. Looking pleasant he spent all time
in women's company. His wrong behaviour was discovered
in the sacrifice of the gods and he was cursed to be born a
śūdra. He took birth as a dāsi's son, and by proper conduct,
became the son of Brahmā.
<div n="P"/>Bhā. VII. 15. 69-73.
