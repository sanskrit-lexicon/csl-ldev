{%Tālajaṃgha%} (I)¦ — the son of Jayadhvaja and father of
one hundred sons (Tālajanghas — Vītihotra being the
elder). The Tālajangha line came to an end by Aurva's
power.<sup>1</sup> Afraid of Paraśurāma he escaped to the Himalayas
and returned after peace was restored. Led an expedition
to Ayodhyā whose king Phalgutantra fled with his wife and
child;<sup>2</sup> defeated Bāhu, but was defeated by his own son,
Sagara.<sup>3</sup>
