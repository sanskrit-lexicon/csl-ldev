{%Rohita%} (I)¦ — a son of Hariścandra; knowing that he
would be sacrificed, went out of his kingdom. Having heard
of the decease of his father, he wanted to return but was
prevented by Indra. At the end of the sixth year, he pur-
chased Sunaśśepa and returned home. Father of Harita.
