<L>5902<pc>2-079<k1>दशार्ह<k2>दशार्ह
{%Daśārhas%}¦ — (also Daśārhakās) a tribe related to the Pāṇ-
ḍavas. They defended Dvārakā when attacked.<sup>1</sup> Praised
Kṛṣṇa's heroism.<sup>2</sup> Felt relieved at Kamsa's death as they
heard of his plan to vanquish them.<sup>3</sup> Followed Balarāma
to Dvārakā and fought their own kinsmen.<sup>4</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. I. 11. 11; 14. 25; III. 1. 29.</F>
<F>2) Ib. IX. 24. 63.</F>
<F>3) Ib. X.
36. 33; 39. 25; 45. 15.</F>
<F>4) Ib. X. 61. 40; XI. 29. 39; 30. 18.</F>
