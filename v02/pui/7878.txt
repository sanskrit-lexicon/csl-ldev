{%Piṇḍaṇirvapana (Piṇḍaḍāna)%}¦ — a ceremonial of the
śrāddha; the mode of offering three to Pitṛs, Pitāmahas and
Prapitāmahas with the respective {%mantras%} to be uttered at
that time;<sup>1</sup> Piṇḍam may also be offered to fire, cows, crows,
fowls, with their respective effects to the giver.<sup>2</sup>

15. 34.</F>
