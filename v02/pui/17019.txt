{%Svaphalka%}¦ — a son of Pṛśni — a righteous and great king;
no disease or famine during his reign; when the Kāśi
kingdom suffered from want of rain continuously for three
years Svaphalka was invited to live there and there were
rains; he then married Kāśirāja's daughter Gāndinī who
gave in gift, every day one cow for a Brahman;<sup>1</sup> his daughter
Narā, was the wife of Bhangakāra.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Vā. 96. 101-105.</F>
<F>2) Ib. 96. 86.</F>
