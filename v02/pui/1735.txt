{%Uttānapāda%} (I)¦ — son of Svāyambhuva Manu and Śata-
rūpā: Brother Priyavrata. Father of Dhruva,<sup>1</sup> a devotee of
Hari. Amśa of Vāsudeva.<sup>2</sup> Had two queens Sunīti and
Suruci. The former's son was Dhruva, and the latter's
Uttama. Suruci and her son were more beloved of the
king. Once when Dhruva ascended his father's lap, Suruci
scolded him and asked him to propitiate god to be born of
her. Heard that his child of five had gone to the forest with
his mother, from Nārada, and regretted his action. Was
assured by the sage that he would return with glory to his
line.<sup>3</sup> Obliged to Hari.<sup>4</sup>
