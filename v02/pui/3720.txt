{%Kauśika%} (VIII)¦ — a sage by tapas of the epoch of
Sāvarṇi;<sup>1</sup> a sage of Kurukṣetra who had seven sons who,
during famine, had their guru's cow killed and after offer-

rebirths they attained final beatitude. These five rebirths
detailed.<sup>2</sup>
