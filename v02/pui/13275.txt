{%Viśvedevas%}¦ — Dharmaputras: (see Viśvāndevas). Ten
sons of Viśvā and Dharma: these are childless gods: wait on
Indra; worshipped for gain of a kingdom.<sup>1</sup> Fought with
Paulomas in Devāsura war: gods of Vaivasvata epoch.<sup>2</sup>
Formed the {%Sadas%} of Marutta's yajñas: blessed Jyāmagha
with a son: {%sūktas%} sacred to;<sup>3</sup> came with other gods to
Dvārakā to ask Kṛṣṇa to go back to Vaikuṇṭha;<sup>4</sup> Kratu,
Dakṣa, Śrava, Satya, Kāla, Kāma, Muni, Purūravas, Ārdra-
vasa, and Rocamāna are their names, live in Bhuvarloka;<sup>5</sup> see
Viśvas, ten in number, were Camasādhvaryavas for Soma's
rājasūya;<sup>6</sup> performed austerities in the Himālayas, and Pitṛs
pleased with them blessed them; Brahmā gave them the first
place of honour in the Śrāddha and the second to devas.<sup>7</sup>
