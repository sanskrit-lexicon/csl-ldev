{%Kumbhāṇḍa%} (I)¦ — a minister of Bāṇa. His daughter
Citralekhā, was a companion of Ūṣā, Bāṇa's daughter;<sup>1</sup> was
attacked by Balarāma in the siege of Śoṇitapura; defeated,
ran away.<sup>2</sup> Sent to Mathurā for Jarāsandha's help. Kṛṣṇa
overcame his illusory warfare by {%Vijñānāstra.%} Defeated by
Sātyaki, he fell unconscious; was taken from the field in a
chariot back to his city.<sup>3</sup>

<F>2) Bhā. X. 63, 8 and 16.</F>
