{%Payovrata%}¦ — (also called {%Sarvayajña, Sarvavrata%}) a
votive offering of the essence of {%tapas,%} in honour of Hari to be
observed for twelve days and in which Hari is represented
in an image or in any other form; originally told about it
by Brahmā, Kaśyapa suggested to his wife Aditi to observe
it; Aditī observed it as instructed by Kaśyapa, when Hari
manifested Himself and promised to be born of her womb;<sup>1</sup>
observed by Śraddhā.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. VIII. 16. 25-60; 17. 1-18.</F>
<F>2) IX. 1. 14.</F>
