{%Guṇas%}¦ — three kinds of persons according to their
nature; hence Guṇatrayam; satva prakṛti, rājasa prakṛti
and tāmasa prakṛti. Their different characteristics. A muni
[Page1-537+ 32]
serves satva and conquers rajas and tamas.<sup>1</sup> If the three
states are normal it is prakṛti, pradhāna or avyakta. If in
a condition of agitation, three deities Brahma, Viṣṇu and
Śiva are the result.<sup>2</sup> Twelve qualities;<sup>3</sup> twenty-six in num-
ber; yoga, sāmkhya, tapas, vidyā, vidhi, kriyā, ṛtam, satyam,
ahimsā, dhyānam, sānti, avidyā, mati, dhṛti, kānti, smṛti,
medha, lajjā, śuddhi, sarasvati, tuṣti, puṣti etc., all in
Brahmā.<sup>4</sup>
