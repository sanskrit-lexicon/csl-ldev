{%Vānaprastha%} (II)¦ — the third āśrama;<sup>1</sup> adopted by
Yayāti after Pūru's coronation; living on fruits and roots and
always in peace, having conquered his mind and anger,
was engaged for 1,000 years in offering oblations to Pitṛs and
Devas and in fire rites and entertaining guests; performed
penance feeding on water alone for 3 years, on air for a year,
in the midst of fire for another year and standing on one leg
for six months; reached heaven.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Vā. 59. 25; 104. 23.</F>
<F>2) M. 35. 1-2, 13-17; 40. 1, 4 and 7.</F>
