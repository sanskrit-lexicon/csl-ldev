{%Citrabhānu%} (II)¦ — a name of the sun; moves in the north
of Śākadvīpa in Śravaṇa and Uttarāṣāḍha;<sup>1</sup> begged of Kārta-
vīryārjuna, the seven islands as {%bhikṣā%} and burnt them all;
father of Varuṇa (Vasiṣṭha) famous as Āpava who cursed
the king; the sun-god in the guise of a Brahmana asked for
[Page1-604+ 31]
food all the immovable property of the king to which he
agreed; Āditya was pleased with him and gave him res-
plendent and useful arrows; he ate all the eastern parts of
the kingdom including the {%tapovana.%}<sup>2</sup>
