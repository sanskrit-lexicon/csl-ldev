{%Gārgya%} (VII)¦ — a pupil of Bhāṣkala; was childless and
hence was ridiculed as impotent by Syāla; was engaged in
penance for Mahādeva for a son by living on iron ore; was
appointed to produce a child on the Yavana queen and the
son was Kālayavana.
