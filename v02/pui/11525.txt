{%Rasātala%}¦ — (also Pātālam) the underworld of rocky
soil. Here live Paṇis, Nāgas, Nivātakavacas and Kāleyas; all
dānavas of great strength and enterprising spirit and afraid
of Hari and Sarama, the messenger of Indra.<sup>1</sup> Visited by
Arjuna in search of the dead child of the Dvārakā
Brahmana;<sup>2</sup> burnt by Pralaya;<sup>3</sup> the Asuras entered it help-
less;<sup>4</sup> Vasu, cursed by the sages to live there, for his decision
against the killing of Paśu in sacrifices;<sup>5</sup> of 10,000 Yojanas
in extent; other talams are Atala, Sutala, Vitala, Gabhastala,
Mahātala, Sritala, and Patāla with the characteristics of
black, white, {%pītam,%} chunam, rocky and gold they are in
groups of earth, water and space.<sup>6</sup>
