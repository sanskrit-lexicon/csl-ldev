<L>13729<pc>3-336<k1>वैराजा<k2>वैराजा<h>I
{%Vairājās%} (I)¦ — the first gods created by Brahmā to
occupy the first worlds by name Samtānakas; among them
seven groups are distinguished, three formless and four with
form; then earth, rains, food, worship of moon for rice; the
formless attain absolution through yoga power;<sup>1</sup> the three
formless groups are Pitṛs engaged in yoga;<sup>2</sup> Ṛbhu and Sanat-
kumāra come under this class; they attained Brahmaloka or
siddhi.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) Br. III. 9. 52-62; Vā. 71. 52.</F>
<F>2) Br. III. 10. 4.</F>
<F>3) Ib. IV. 2. 35, 70 ff.</F>
