{%Daṇḍa%} (II)¦ — (see also daṇḍanīti): the rod of punish-
ment, unknown in Śākadvīpa;<sup>1</sup> in Puṣkaradvīpa;<sup>2</sup> the duty
of a Kṣatriya;<sup>3</sup> one of the upāyas of a king; to be used if the
first three fail; illegal punishments lead the king to hell; to
be used according to time and the considered advice of men
learned in ancient lore.<sup>4</sup>

<F>2) Br. II. 19. 120.</F>
