{%Aṃśumat%} (I)¦ — son of Asamañja(sa)(s), devoted to
grandfather Sagara.<sup>1</sup> Went in search of the consecrated
horse of Sagara, met Kapila and belauded his greatness.<sup>2</sup>
Pleased with him, Kapila sent back the horse, adding that
the Sāgaras would obtain release by the waters of the
Ganges.<sup>3</sup> Succeeded Sagara as king.<sup>4</sup> Was engaged in
penance to get the Gaṅgā for the liberation of his uncles.
Before he could achieve this, he died. His son was Dilīpa.<sup>5</sup>
Anointing him, Aṃśumat retired to the forest.
