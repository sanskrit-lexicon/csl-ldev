{%Aṃśumat%} (I)¦ — son of Asamañja(sa)(s), devoted to
<div n="lb"/>grandfather Sagara.<sup>1</sup> Went in search of the consecrated
<div n="lb"/>horse of Sagara, met Kapila and belauded his greatness.<sup>2</sup>
<div n="lb"/>Pleased with him, Kapila sent back the horse, adding that
<div n="lb"/>the Sāgaras would obtain release by the waters of the
<div n="lb"/>Ganges.<sup>3</sup> Succeeded Sagara as king.<sup>4</sup> Was engaged in
<div n="lb"/>penance to get the Gaṅgā for the liberation of his uncles.
<div n="lb"/>Before he could achieve this, he died. His son was Dilīpa.<sup>5</sup>
<div n="lb"/>Anointing him, Aṃśumat retired to the forest.
