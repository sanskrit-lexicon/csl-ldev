{%Bhaṅgakāra%} (II)¦ — the eldest of the 101 sons of Satrājit
(Śakrajit-{%Vā. P.%}); his wife was Vratavatī (Dvāravatī-{%Vā.%}
{%P.%}); she gave birth to three daughters, Satyabhāmā, Vratinī
and Padmāvatī; these were given in marriage to Kṛṣṇa,
killed by Śatadhanvā for the jewel Syamantaka taken from
him and handed over it to Akrūra.
