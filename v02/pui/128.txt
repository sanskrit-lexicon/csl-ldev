{%vasu%} ray of the sun, attains {%Lauhitam sthānam%} or the {%Lohita%}

{%yojanas.%} Also known as {%Lohita%} and {%Vakra.%}<sup>2</sup> The first of
Planets, originally Vīrabhadra who destroyed Dakṣa's sacri-
fice; son of mother Earth; fought with Soma.<sup>3</sup> Day sacred
to.<sup>4</sup> {%Vratam%} in honour of, conduces to health and prosperity;
the Śūdras observe it; described by Śukra in detail.<sup>5</sup> The
{%tanu%} of Śarva (Agni) by Vikeśi.<sup>6</sup> Born in {%Āṣāḍha.%}<sup>7</sup>
