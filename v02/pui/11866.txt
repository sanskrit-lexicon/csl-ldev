{%Lakṣmaṇā%} (II)¦ — a daughter of Duryodhana married
by Sāmba in a {%svayamvara.%} For this the Kurus like Karṇa
and Duryodhana took him captive after a severe fight and
threw him into prison. Afraid of Balarāma's prowess,
Duryodhana agreed to her marriage with Sāmba and sent a
rich dowry.
