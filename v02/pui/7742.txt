{%Pāṇḍu%} (I)¦ — a Rājaṛṣi: one of the three sons of Vicitra-
vīrya; (Kṛṣṇadvaipāyana — {%Vā. P.%}) born to Vyāsa; married
two wives, Kunti alias Prthā, sister of Vasudeva and Mādri;
father of the five Pāṇḍavas, who were born to Kunti by
favour of Gods, himself being prevented by a curse from
having sexual intercourse; on his death Pṛthā with the
young children underwent a lot of difficulties.
