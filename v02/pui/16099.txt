{%Sudarśana%} (I)¦ — a weapon of Kṛṣṇa, known as Cakra;<sup>1</sup>
reached Kṛṣṇa when Mathurā was besieged by Jarāsandha,
and was used to kill Śatadhanvā;<sup>2</sup> sent to the sun and moon
to ward off Rāhu's attack;<sup>3</sup> presented to Ambarīṣa; fell upon
Durvāsa when he raised a spirit to attack Ambarīṣa who
begged to spare the sage and the Cakra did so;<sup>4</sup> a weapon of
Hari, at Kāśī.<sup>5</sup>
