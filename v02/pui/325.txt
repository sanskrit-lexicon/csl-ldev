{%Aditi%} (I)¦ — a daughter of Dakṣa and a wife of Kaśyapa.<sup>1</sup>
<div n="lb"/>Mother of Vāmana-Hari and other gods.<sup>2</sup> Was much con-
<div n="lb"/>cerned at the evacuation of Amarāvati by gods, and informed
<div n="lb"/>her husband of her grief. Kaśyapa consoled her saying that
<div n="lb"/>it was all Hari's {%māyā%} and everything would be rectified by
<div n="lb"/>His aid. Asked to the means of approaching Hari, Kaśyapa
<div n="lb"/>taught her the {%Payovrata.%} Observed the {%vrata%} for twelve
<div n="lb"/>days as directed. Hari heard her prayers, promised to be
<div n="lb"/>born in her womb to protect her sons, and desired that she
<div n="lb"/>should keep it a secret. She soon waited on Kaśyapa and
<div n="lb"/>became pregnant with Hari. Then Hari was praised by
<div n="lb"/>Brahmā.<sup>3</sup> Birth of the Lord described.<sup>4</sup> Changed before
<div n="lb"/>her into a boy-brahmacārin.<sup>5</sup> Worshipped for food and
<div n="lb"/>other things, while her sons the Ādityas are prayed to for
<div n="lb"/>attainment of heaven.<sup>6</sup> Her desire was fulfilled by Vāmana.
<div n="lb"/>Pleased with his anointing, gods sent their praises to Aditi
<div n="lb"/>on the exploits of Vāmana.<sup>7</sup> Kṛṣna took back the {%Kuṇḍalas%}
<div n="lb"/>which had been taken away by Naraka, and Satyabhāmā
<div n="lb"/>in her Lord's company bowed to Aditi and handed over the
<div n="lb"/>{%Kuṇḍalas.%} At this Aditi embraced her daughter-in-law.<sup>8</sup>
<div n="P"/>Prajāpati caused the {%tejas%} of the {%aṇḍam%} in her {%garbha,%}
<div n="lb"/>and removing it at the request of gods, made it into two
<div n="lb"/>pieces, and finding it weak, placed it on the lap of the Sun-
<div n="lb"/>god (Mārtāṇḍa): given to dharma.<sup>9</sup> A part of Mother Earth.<sup>10</sup>
<div n="P"/>A mother goddess.<sup>11</sup> Worshipped in housebuilding.<sup>12</sup>
<div n="lb"/>Mother of the 12 Ādityas the former Jayadevas.<sup>13</sup> Known
<div n="lb"/>for strength.<sup>14</sup>
