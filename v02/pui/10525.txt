{%Maheśvara%} (I)¦ — (Śiva): cursed the seven sages to be
born in every epoch;<sup>1</sup> got vṛṣa (bull) from Surabhī for his
standard;<sup>2</sup> lived long in his father-in-law's house after
marriage; Meru insulted him as poor and useless when Umā
insisted on her husband to go to his house; he went to
Benares after which it became Avimukta;<sup>3</sup> also Brahmā;<sup>4</sup>
Stambha; Kāpāli on earth for having removed one face of
Brahmā; to get rid of this brahmicide Śiva's service to
Kāmākṣī at Kāñcī; blessed for his samādhi; bathed in Pañ-
casaras and found himself at Kāśi, from there to Kāñcī; Jyo-
tirmayibhikṣa given; the Kapāla dropped out and he was
relieved;<sup>5</sup> blessed Kubera to be the lord of the Yakṣas for
his penance at the confluence of the Narmadā and the
Kāverī.<sup>6</sup> Praise of, by Kāvya.<sup>7</sup>
