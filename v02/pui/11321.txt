{%Yuyudhāna%}¦ — (see Sātyaki) the grandson of Śini, and
son of Satyaka, and father of Jaya.<sup>1</sup> Learnt the {%dhanurveda%}
from Arjuna. Approved of Draupadī's suggestion to release
Aśvatthaman: accompanied Kṛṣṇa to Hāstinapura;<sup>2</sup> took
active part in the Rājasūya of Yudhiṣṭhira: was sent to
Dvārakā for defence;<sup>3</sup> went with the sacrificial horse of
Kṛṣṇa.<sup>4</sup>
