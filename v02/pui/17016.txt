{%Svapna%}¦ — dreams, bad and good; ceremonies must be
performed for bad ones; after a bad dream, one must try to
sleep for sometime; after a good dream one must try to be
awake lest it should be followed by a bad one for the latter
will generally come true. Dream had in the first of the
four divisions of the night will come true in a year; that
had in the second will come true in six months; that had in
the third, will come true in three months and that had early
in the morning will come true in 10 days.
