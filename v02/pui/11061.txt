{%Merusāvarṇi%} (I)¦ — the first of the four sons of Brahmā,
grandson of Dakṣa and son of his daughter Kriyā; engaged
in austerities on the slopes of the Meru; went to Maharloka
and was reborn in the epoch of Cākṣuṣa; the other three
names are Parā, Marīcigarbha, and Sudharmāṇa; all of them
sons of Rohita Prajāpati;<sup>1</sup> the eleventh Manu of the future.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Br. IV. 1. 23, 53.</F>
<F>2) M. 9. 36.</F>
