{%Upendra%}¦ — a manifestation of Hari born of Aditi and
Kaśyapa. He was known as Vāmana because of his short
stature.<sup>1</sup> Anointed by gods as the Lord of all worlds.
Helped Indra his elder brother in the administration of his
kingdoms.<sup>2</sup> Knew the {%yoga%} power of Hari<sup>3</sup> and was
invoked by gopas for the protection of the baby Kṛṣṇa.<sup>4</sup>
Had a son Bṛhatśloka through Kīrti.<sup>5</sup> Also known as Uru-
krama. A son of Diti.<sup>6</sup> Kṛṣṇa, crowned by Indra as the
Indra of cows, urged by the speech of Gava; perhaps the
cows of heaven like the Kāmadhenu; at that time the cattle
delighted the earth with milk.<sup>7</sup>
