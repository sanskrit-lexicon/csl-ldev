{%Bṛhadrathas%}¦ — the kings of Magadha with Girivraja as
capital; 32 in number, and ruled altogether for 1000 years;
these are Somādhi, Śrutaśrava, Apratīpi, Niramitra, Surakṣa,
Bṛhatkarma, Senājit, Samprayāta, Śrutamjaya, Vibhu, Śuci,
Kṣema, Anuvrata, Sunetra, Nirvṛti, Trinetra, Dyumatsena.
Mahīnetra, Acala and Ripuñjaya; Pulaka killed this king
and installed his son, Bālaka on the throne.
