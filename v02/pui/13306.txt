<div n="P"/>Propitiated by Dakṣa after his reconciliation with Śiva,

Brahmā.<sup>3</sup> presented Pṛthu with Sudarśana and addressed

In Svāyambhuva epoch was born of Ākūtī, in Svārociṣa,
born of Tuṣitā as Ajita, in Uttama epoch, born of Satyā as
Satya, in Tāmasa born of Hariṇī as Hari, in Vaivasvata, born
of Kaśyapa and Aditī with the name Vaikuṇṭha.<sup>7</sup> Overlord
of Ādityas; gave peacock and fowl to Kumāra.<sup>8</sup> Killed
Daityas in Tārakāmaya and especially Kālanemi. Aided
Indra against Asuras. Introduced yajña and other rites,
divisions of time, three castes, three Vedas, three guṇas and so
on.<sup>9</sup> Got Indra redeemed of the sin of hitting his Purohita Viś-
varūpa, by distributing it among the women, trees and earth.
When Durvāsa cursed Indra for refusing to accept Devī's
garland, the Asuras destroyed the Devas and entered heaven.
Then Hari suggested churning of the ocean and distributed
as Mohinī nectar to Devas making them invincible.<sup>10</sup> Gave
his sister Devī in marriage to Śiva with wedding presents.<sup>11</sup>
