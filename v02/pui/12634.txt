{%Vālakhilyas%}¦ — a class of seers, 60,000 in number, born
of Kratu:<sup>1</sup> advised Citraratha, who fell to the ground to
gather Kauśika's bones to throw them into the Sarasvatī
and get redemption;<sup>2</sup> They go in front of the Sun from his
rise to his setting, singing his glory;<sup>3</sup> live on air; sages by {%tapas;%}
authors of certain {%Samhitas;%} live in Brahmaloka;<sup>4</sup> Ṛṣis by
{%tapas.%}<sup>5</sup> Of the category of Sāvarna;<sup>6</sup> were born out of kuśa
grass and endowed with all powers in {%Vāruṇi yajña;%}<sup>7</sup> Puṇyā
and Sumatī are younger sisters of.<sup>8</sup>
