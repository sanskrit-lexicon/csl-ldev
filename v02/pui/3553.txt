enlisted by Jarāsandha against the Yadus. Their princes were
stationed on the North during the siege of Gomanta.<sup>1</sup> But

festivities of Rukmiṇī.<sup>2</sup> Heard of Kṛṣṇa going to Mithilā,

Bhīmasena in his {%digvijaya;%}<sup>4</sup> rose against Śiśupāla.<sup>5</sup> Took
part in Yudhiṣṭhira's {%Rājasūya.%}<sup>6</sup> In the Mahābhārata war,
five princes of Kekaya joined the Pāṇḍavas against the
