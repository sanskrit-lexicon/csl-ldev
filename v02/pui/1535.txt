{%Indradyumna%}¦ — the son of Tejasa (Taijasa-{%Vā. P.%}). A
Drāviḍa and a Pāṇḍyan king. Devoted to Hari. While
engaged in {%tapas%} Agastya came to his hermitage. Finding
him not extending a welcome, the angry sage cursed him to
become an elephant. Indradyumna considered that to be
the will of the Lord. He was born as the Lord of the ele-
phants, and had reminiscences of his past life;<sup>1</sup> an account
of, in the {%Kūrma Pūrāṇa.%}<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. VIII. 4. 7-12; Br. II. 14. 64; Vā. 33. 54; Vi. II. 1. 36.</F>
<F>2) M. 53. 47-8.</F>
