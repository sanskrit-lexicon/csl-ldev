{%Taptakumbha%}¦ — (Taptakuṇḍa-{%Vi. P.%}); a hell, into
which fall disloyal government servants, dealers in horses,
gaolers, and women, those who have sexual intercourse with
sisters, preceptor's wife, incestuous offenders, murderers of
Kṣatriyas and Vaiśyas and slayers of royal emissary.
