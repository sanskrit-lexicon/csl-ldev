{@Mucukundopākhyāna(ṃ)@}¦ (“the episode relating to
Mucukunda”). § 641{%g%} (Rājadh.). King {%Mucukunda%}, having
subjugated the whole earth, repaired to {%Kubera%} in order to
test his strength. {%Kubera%} created a large force of Rā., who
annihilated the forces of {%Mucukunda. Mucukunda%} rebuked
his priest {%Vasiṣṭha%}, who then by penances caused those Rā.
to be slain. {%Kubera's%} haughty address and {%Mucukunda's%}
manly answer.
