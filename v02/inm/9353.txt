with only a single chariot subjugated all the kings of the
East ({%prācyāḥ%}) backed by 10,000 chariots; and in the same
way those of the South. {%Dhṛtarāṣṭra's%} sentiments suddenly
became poisoned against the {%Pāṇḍavas%}, and from anxiety he
could not sleep (I, {@139@}).—§ 212: Kaṇikavākya (q.v.).
