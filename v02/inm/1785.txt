<div n="lb">of Brahmán”). § 270 (Lokapālas.): In the {%Kṛta%} age, {%Āditya%}
<div n="lb">(= the Sun) having seen the {%sabhā%} of {%Pitāmaha%} (i.e. {%Brahmán%}),

<div n="lb">is not supported by columns; it is eternal, etc. There
