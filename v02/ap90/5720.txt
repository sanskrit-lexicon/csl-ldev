{#आ#}¦ {@1@} Used as a particle or <lbinfo n="inter+jection"/>
interjection showing ({%a%}) assent; ‘yes’,
‘verily’. ({%b%}) Compassion ({#अनुकंपा#})
‘Ah’. ({%c%}) Pain or regret (usually
written {#आस्#} or {#आः#} <ab>q. v.</ab>), ‘alas’. ({%a%})
Recollection ({#स्मरण#}) ‘Ah’, ‘Oh’;
{#आ एवं किलासीत्#} <ls>U. 6.</ls> ({%e%}) But (used
as a disjunctive conjunction). ({%f%})
And (used as a cumulative <lbinfo n="con+junction"/>
conjunction). ({%g%}) Sometimes used as an
expletive; {#आ एवं मन्यसे#}. In all these
senses {#आ#} is treated as a Pragṛhya
vowel (does not form any Saṃdhi
with a following vowel) <ls>P. I. 1. 14.</ls>
{@--2@} (As a prefix to verbs and nouns)
({%a%}) it expresses the senses of
near, near to, towards, from all
sides, all around (see the several
verbs). ({%b%}) With verbs of motion,
taking, carrying &c. it shows the
reverse of the action; as {#गम्#} to go,
{#आगम्#} to come; {#दा#} to give, {#आदा#} to
take; {#नी#} to carry, {#आनी#} to bring.
{@--3@} (As a separable preposition with
<ab>abl.</ab>) it shows either ({%a%}) the limit
inceptive ({#अभिविधि#}), from, ever since,
away from, out of, off, from among;
{#आमूलात् श्रोतुमिच्छामि#} <ls>Ś. 1</ls>; {#बहुभ्य#}
{#आ#} from out of many; {#आ जन्मनः#}
<ls>Ś. 5. 25</ls> ever since (her) birth;
{#आ मनोः#} <ls>U. 6. 18.</ls> ({%b%}) Or, it <lbinfo n="ex+presses"/>
expresses the limit exclusive or <lbinfo n="con+clusive"/>
conclusive ({#मर्यादा#}), till, until, upto, as
far as, unto; {#आङ् मर्यादाभिविध्योः#}  <lbinfo n="ls:P. II.+1"/>
<ls>P. II. 1. 13</ls>; see {#अभिविधि#}; {#आ#} <lbinfo n="paritozAdvidu#zAM"/>
{#परितोषाद्विदुषां#} <ls>Ś. 1. 2</ls> till the learned are <lbinfo n="satis+fied"/>
satisfied; {#आ कैलासात्#} <ls>Me. 11</ls> upto or as
far as Kailāsa; {#ओदकांतात्#} <ls>Ś. 4</ls>;  <lbinfo n="ls:U. 1.+ 37"/>
<ls>U. 1. 37</ls>; <ls>V. 2. 2.</ls> In this sense {#आ#} <lbinfo n="some+times"/>
sometimes governs the <ab>acc.</ab>; {#शतमा जातीः#}
upto a hundred births. ({%c%}) In both
these senses {#आ#} frequently enters

into compound, forming either <lbinfo n="Avya+yībhāva"/>
Avyayībhāva <ab>comp.</ab> or compound <lbinfo n="adjec+tives"/>
adjectives; {#आबालं#} (or {#आ बालेभ्यः#}) {#हरिभक्तिः#}
commencing with or including <lbinfo n="child+ren"/>
children; {#आमुक्ति#} (or {#आमुक्तेः#}) {#संसारः#} <ls>Sk.</ls>
till final emancipation; {#आमेखलं#}  <lbinfo n="ls:Ku.+ 1. 5"/>
<ls>Ku. 1. 5</ls> as far as.; {#आमरणं#} <ls>Pt. 1</ls> till
death; {#आगोपालं ननृतुः#} <ls>K. 70</ls> down to
the cowherd; {#आगोपाला द्विजातयः#}
including the cowherds. Sometimes
the compound so formed stands
as the first member of other <lbinfo n="com+pounds"/>
compounds, {#सोऽ#} <lbinfo n="hamAjanmaSudDAnAmAPalodaya#karmaRAM"/>
{#हमाजन्मशुद्धानामाफलोदयकर्मणां ।#} <lbinfo n="AsamudrakzitISAnAmAnAkaraTavartma#nAM"/>
{#आसमुद्रक्षितीशानामानाकरथवर्त्मनां#} <ls>R. 1. 5</ls>; {#आगंडविलंबि#} <ls>Ś. 6. 17</ls>;
{#आकर्णलंबि#} <ls>M. 5. 10.</ls> ({%d%}) Used
with <ab>loc.</ab> it has the sense of ‘in’,
‘at’ (mostly <ab>Ved.</ab>); {#गावो न यवसेष्वा#}
<ls>Rv. 1. 91. 13.</ls> {@--4@} With adjectives
(or sometimes with nouns) {#आ#} has
a diminutive force; {#आपांडुर#} a little
white, whitish; {#आलक्ष्य#} <ls>Ś. 7. 17</ls>
slightly visible; {#आकंपः#} gentle
shaking; so {#आनील, आरक्त#}. {@--5@} (As
a separable adverb) {#आ#} chiefly <lbinfo n="oc+curs"/>
occurs in the Vedas and means near,
near to, or towards, there to, further;
and also, even; in many cases it
emphasizes the word which <lbinfo n="pre+cedes"/>
precedes it, and when placed after <lbinfo n="pre+positions"/>
prepositions it strengthens their sense.
