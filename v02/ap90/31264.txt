{#हनु(नू)मत्#}¦ {%<ab>m.</ab>%} <ab>N.</ab> of a powerful
monkey-chief. [He was the son of
Añjanā by the god Wind or Marut and
hence called Māruti. He is represented
as a monkey of extraordinary strength
and prowess which he manifested on
several critical occasions on behalf of
Rāma whom he regarded as the idol of
his heart. When Sītā was carried off by
Rāvaṇa, he crossed the sea and brought
news about her to his lord. He played
a very important part in the great war
at Laṅkā.].
