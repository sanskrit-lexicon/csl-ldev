{#कामरूप#}¦ An important kingdom said to have extended
from the banks of the Karatoyā or Sadānīrā to
the extremities of Assam. It must have extended
up to the Himālaya on the north and the borders of
China on the east, as its king is said to have <lbinfo n="assist+ed"/>
assisted Duryodhana with an army of Kirātas and
Cīnas. The ancient capital of this kingdom was
{#प्राग्ज्योतिष#} on the other side of the Lauhitya or the
river Brahmaputra; <ab>cf.</ab> <ls>R. 4. 81.</ls>
