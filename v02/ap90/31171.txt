{#स्वस्ति#}¦ {%<ab>ind.</ab>%} A particle meaning
‘may it be well with (one)’, <lbinfo n="‘fare+well"/>
‘farewell,’ ‘hail’, ‘adieu’ (with <ab>dat.</ab>);
{#स्वस्ति भवते#} <ls>Ś. 2</ls>; {#स्वस्त्यस्तु ते#}  <lbinfo n="ls:R. 5.+ 17"/>
<ls>R. 5. 17</ls>; it is also used in expressing
one's approbation; (often used at
the beginning of letters). {@--Comp.@}
{#--अयनं#} {1} a means of securing
prosperity. {2} the averting of evil
by the recitation of {%mantras%} or <lbinfo n="per+formance"/>
performance of expiatory rites. {3} the
benediction of a Brāhmaṇa after
presentation of offerings; {#प्रास्थानिकं#}
{#स्वस्त्ययनं प्रयुज्य#} <ls>R. 2. 70.</ls> {#--दः, --भावः#}
an epithet of Śiva. {#--मुखः#} {1} a <lbinfo n="let+ter"/>
letter. {2} a Brāhmaṇa. --. a bard;
panegyrist. {#--वाचनं, --वाचनकं#}, <lbinfo n="vAca#nikaM"/>
{#--वाचनिकं#} {1} a religious rite preparatory
to a sacrifice or any religious or
solemn observance. {2} a <lbinfo n="compli+mentary"/>
complimentary or congratulatory present of
flowers &c. to any one attended
with good wishes and blessings.
{#--वाच्यं#} congratulation, invoking
blessings.
