{#ऋषिः#}¦ [<ab>cf.</ab> <ls>Uṇ. 4. 119</ls>] {@1@} An
inspired poet or sage, a singer of
sacred hymns, ({%<ab>e. g.</ab>%} {#कुत्स, वसिष्ठ#},
{#अत्रि, अगस्त्य#} &c.). (These Ṛṣis form
a class of beings distinct from
gods, men, Asuras &c. They
are the authors or {%seers%} of the
Vedic hymns; {#ऋषयो मंत्रद्रष्टारो#} <lbinfo n="vasi#zWAdayaH"/>
{#वसिष्ठादयः#}; or, according to Yāska, {#यस्य#}
{#वाक्यं स ऋषिः#}, {%<ab>i. e.</ab>%} they are the <lbinfo n="per+sons"/>
persons to whom the Vedic hymns
were revealed. In every Sūkta the
{#ऋषि#} is mentioned along with the
{#देवता, छंदस्#} and {#विनियोग#}. The later
works mention seven Ṛṣis or
{%Saptarṣayas%}, whose names, <lbinfo n="accord+ing"/>
according to <ls>Śat. Br.</ls>, are {#गौतम, भरद्वाज#}, <lbinfo n="vi#SvAmitra"/>
{#विश्वामित्र, जमदग्नि, वसिष्ठ, कश्यप#} and {#अत्रि#};
according to Mahābhārata, {#मरीचि#},
{#अत्रि, अंगिरस्, पुलह, क्रतु, पुलस्त्य#} and
{#वसिष्ठ#}; Manu calls these sages <lbinfo n="Prajā+patis"/>
Prajāpatis or progenitors of mankind, and
gives ten names, three more being
added to the latter list, {%<ab>i. e.</ab>%} {#दक्ष#} or 
[Page0351-a+ 30]
{#प्रचेतस्, भृगु#} and {#नारद#}. In astronomy
the seven Ṛṣis form the <lbinfo n="constella+tion"/>
constellation of “the Great Bear”). {@--2@} A <lbinfo n="sancti+fied"/>
sanctified sage, saint, an ascetic, anchorite;
there are usually three classes of
these saints; {#देवर्षि, ब्रह्मर्षि#} or {#राजर्षि#}; <lbinfo n="some+times"/>
sometimes four more are added; {#महर्षि#}, <lbinfo n="pa#ramarzi"/>
{#परमर्षि, श्रुतर्षि#} and {#कांडर्षि#}. {@--3@} A ray of
light. {@--4@} An imaginary circle.
{@--Comp.@} {#--कुल्या#} {1} a sacred river.
{2} <ab>N.</ab> of {#महानदी#}. {#--कृत्#} {%<ab>a.</ab>%} making
one's appearance. {#--गिरिः#} <ab>N.</ab> of a

{#--जांगलिका#} the plant {#ऋक्षगंधा#}. {#--तर्पणं#} <lbinfo n="liba+tion"/>
libation offered to the Ṛṣis. {#--पंचमी#}
<ab>N.</ab> of a festival or ceremony on the
fifth day in the first half of <lbinfo n="Bhā+drapada"/>
Bhādrapada (observed by women).
{#--प्रोक्ता#} the plant {#माषपर्णी#}. {#--बंधु#} {%<ab>a.</ab>%}
