<L>13983<pc>0515-c<k1>जीवत्<k2>जीवत्
{#जीवत्#}¦ {%<ab>a.</ab>%} ({#न्ती#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%}) Living, alive.
{@--Comp.@} {#--तोका#} a woman whose
children are living. {#--पतिः#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%}, {#--पत्नी#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%}
a woman whose husband is living.
{#--पितृकः#} one whose father is alive.
{#--मुक्त#} {%<ab>a.</ab>%} ‘liberated while living’,
a man who, being purified by a true
knowledge of the Supreme spirit, is
freed from the future birth and
all ceremonial rites while yet
living. {#--मुक्तिः#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%} final liberation
in the present state of life. {#--मृत#}
{%<ab>a.</ab>%} ‘dead while alive,’ one who, though
alive, is as good as dead and useless
to the world (said of a mad man or
one whose character is lost).
