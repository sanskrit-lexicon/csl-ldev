{#अभिवादः, --वादनं#}¦ {@1@} Reverential <lbinfo n="salu+tation"/>
salutation, respectful obeisance, <lbinfo n="saluta+tion"/>
salutation of a superior or elder by an
inferior or junior, or of a teacher
by his disciple. It consists in (1)
rising from one's seat ({#प्रत्युत्त्थान#}),
(2) seizing the feet ({#पादोपसंग्रह#}), and
(3) repeating the form of <lbinfo n="saluta+tion"/>
salutation ({#अभिवाद#}) which includes the
name or title of the person <lbinfo n="ad+dressed"/>
addressed, followed by the <lbinfo n="men+tion"/>
mention of the person's own name.
For the different ways of <lbinfo n="perform+ing"/>
performing obeisance and the merit arising
therefrom see <ls>Ms. 2. 120-126</ls>.
{@--2@} Abuse, insulting or scurrilous
speech (for {#अतिवाद#}).
