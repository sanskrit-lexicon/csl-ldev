{#स्विद्#}¦ {%<ab>ind.</ab>%} A particle of <lbinfo n="inter+rogation"/>
interrogation or inquiry, often <lbinfo n="imply+ing"/>
implying ‘doubt’, or ‘surprise’, and
translateable by ‘what’, ‘hey’.
‘hallo’, ‘can it be that’.
It is added to interrogative
pronouns in this sense or to give
an indefinite sense; {#कास्विदवगुंठनवती#}
{#नातिपरिस्फुटशरीरलावण्या#} <ls>Ś. 5. 13</ls>;
<ls>Me. 14.</ls> It is sometimes used <lbinfo n="dis+junctively"/>
disjunctively in the sense of ‘either,’
‘or’, with {#नु, उत, वा#} &c.; see  <lbinfo n="ls:Ki.+ 8. 35"/>
<ls>Ki. 8. 35</ls>, <ls n="Ki.">12. 15</ls>, <ls n="Ki.">13. 8</ls>, <ls n="Ki.">14. 60</ls>; {#आहो#}
also.
