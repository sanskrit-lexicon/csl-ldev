[He was the son of Marīci, the son of
Brahmā. He bears a very important
share in the work of creation. <lbinfo n="Accord+ing"/>
According to Mahābhārata and other accounts,
he married Aditi and 12 other <lbinfo n="daugh+ters"/>
daughters of Dakṣa, and begot on Aditi
the twelve Ādityas. By his other
twelve wives he had a numerous and
very diversified progeny: serpents,
reptiles, birds, demons, nymphs of

the father of gods, demons, men,
