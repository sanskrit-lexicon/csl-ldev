(Usually {#गोष्ठं#} only) {@1@} A cowpen,
cowhouse, cow-station. {@--2@} A station
of cowherds. {#--ष्ठः#} An assembly or
meeting. {@--3@} An epithet of Śiva.
{#--ष्ठं#} A purificatory {#श्राद्ध#} ceremony.
{#°पतिः#} a chief herdsman. {#°वेदिका#} a
mound or altar in a cowpen. {#°श्वः#}
a dog in a cowpen which barks at
every one; applied figuratively to
a slanderous person, one who stays
idly at home and slanders his
neighbours. {#गोष्ठेपंडितः#} ‘wise in a
cowpen,’ a braggart, vain boaster.
{#गोष्ठेक्ष्वोडिन्#} {%<ab>m.</ab>%} a boasting coward;
also {#गोष्ठपटु, गोष्ठेप्रगल्भः, --शूरः#} &c.
