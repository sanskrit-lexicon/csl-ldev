A fallacious {%hetu%}; one of the
five principal divisions of  <lbinfo n="hetvA#BAsa"/>
{#हेत्वाभास#} or fallacies. It is of three

kinds:--(1) {#आश्रयासिद्ध#} where the <lbinfo n="ex+istence"/>
existence of any such locality ({#आश्रय#})

‘{#गगनारविंदं सुरभि अरविंदत्वात्#}’; (2)  <lbinfo n="svarUpAsi#dDa"/>

{#व्याप्यतासिद्ध#} where the alleged <lbinfo n="inva+riableness"/>
invariableness of concomitance is not

{#साध्य#}); as {#पर्वतो वह्निमान् कांचनमयधूमात्#}.
