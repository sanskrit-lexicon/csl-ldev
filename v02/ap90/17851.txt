<L>17851<pc>0670-b<k1>परि<k2>परि
{#परि#}¦ {%<ab>ind.</ab>%} (Sometimes changed
to {#परी#}, as {#परिवाह#} or {#परीवाह, परिहास#} or
{#परीहास#}) {@1@} As a prefix to verbs and
nouns derived from them, it means
({%a%}) round, round about, about. ({%b%})
in addition to, further. ({%c%}) opposite
to, against. ({%d%}) much, excessively.
{@--2@} As a separable preposition it
means ({%a%}) towards, in the direction
of, to, opposite to; (with an <ab>acc.</ab>);
{#वृक्षं परि विद्योतते विद्युत्#} ({%b%}) <lbinfo n="succes+sessively"/>
successessively, severally (with an <ab>acc.</ab>);
{#वृक्ष वृक्षं परि सिंचति#} ‘he waters tree
after tree.’ ({%c%}) to the share or lot 
[Page0670-c+ 57]
of (showing {#भाग#} or participation)
(with <ab>acc.</ab>); {#यदत्र मां परि स्यात्#} ‘what
may fall to my lot’; or {#लक्ष्मीर्हरिं परि#}.
<ls>Sk.</ls> ({%d%}) from, out of. ({%e%}) except,
outside of, with the exception of
(with <ab>abl.</ab>); {#परि त्रिगर्तेभ्यो वृष्टो देवः#}
or {#पर्यनंतात्त्रयस्तापाः#} <ls>Vop.</ls> ({%f%}) after
the lapse of. ({%g%}) in consequence of.
({%h%}) beyond, more than. ({%i%}) <lbinfo n="accord+ing"/>
according to, in accordance with. ({%j%})
above, over. {@--3@} As an adverbial <lbinfo n="pre+fix"/>
prefix to nouns not directly connected
with verbs, it means ‘very,’; ‘very
much,’ ‘excessively’; as in {#पर्यश्रु#}
‘bursting into tears’; so {#परिचतुर्दशन्#}, <lbinfo n="pa#ridOrvalya"/>
{#परिदौर्वल्य#}. {@--4@} At the beginning of <lbinfo n="ad+verbial"/>
adverbial compounds {#परि#} means ({%a%})
without, except, outside, with the
exception of; as in {#परित्रिगर्तं वृष्टो देवः#}
<ls>P. II. 1. 12</ls>; <ls n="P.">VI. 2. 33</ls>. According
to <ls>P. II. 1. 10</ls> {#परि#} may be used at
the end of adverbial <ab>comp.</ab> after {#अक्ष#},
{#शलाका#}, and a numeral to denote ‘loss
or defeat in a game by an unlucky or
adverse cast of dice’ ({#द्यूतव्यवहारे#} <lbinfo n="parAja#ye"/>
{#पराजये एवायं समासः#}); {%<ab>e. g.</ab>%} {#अक्षपरि#}, <lbinfo n="SalAkApa#ri"/>
{#शलाकापरि, एकपरि#}; <ab>cf.</ab> {#अक्षपरि#}. ({%b%}) round
about, all round, surrounded
by; as in {#पर्यग्नि#} ‘in the midst of
flames’. {@--5@} At the end of an <lbinfo n="ad+jectival"/>
adjectival <ab>comp.</ab> {#परि#} has the sense
of ‘exhausted by,’ or ‘feeling <lbinfo n="repug+nance"/>
repugnance, for’; as in {#पर्यध्ययनः#} = <lbinfo n="pariglA#no"/>
{#परिग्लानोऽध्ययनाय#}.
