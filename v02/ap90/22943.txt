{#मात्र#}¦ {%<ab>a.</ab>%} ({#त्रा, --त्री#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%}) [{#मा-त्रन्#}] An
affix added to nouns in the sense
of ‘measuring as much as’, ‘as 
[Page0858-b+ 57]
high or long, or broad as,’ <lbinfo n="‘reach+ing"/>
‘reaching as far as;’ as in {#ऊरुमात्री भित्तिः#}
{#पंचदशयोजनमात्रमध्वानमतिचक्राम#} <ls>K.</ls>;
(in this sense the word may as
well be considered to be {#मात्रा#} at the
end of <ab>comp.</ab> <ab>q. v.</ab> below). {#--त्रं#} {@1@}
A measure, whether of length,
breadth, height, size, space, <lbinfo n="dis+tance"/>
distance or number; usually at the
end of <ab>comp.</ab> {%<ab>i. e.</ab>%} {#अंगुलिमात्रं#} a <lbinfo n="‘fin+ger"/>
‘finger's breadth’; {#किंचिन्मात्रं गत्वा#} ‘to
some distance’; {#क्रोशमात्रे#} ‘at the
distance of a Krośa’, {#रेखामात्रमपि#}
‘even the breadth of a line, as much
as a line’; <ls>R. 1. 17</ls>; so {#क्षणमात्र, नि#}
{#मिषमात्रं#} ‘the space of an instant;’
{#शतमात्र#} ‘a hundred in number’; so
{#गजमात्र#} ‘as high or big as an <lbinfo n="ele+phant"/>
elephant’; {#तालम त्र, यवमात्र#} &c. {@--2@} The
full measure of anything, the whole
or entire class of things, totality;
{#जीवमात्र#} or {#प्राणिमात्रं#} ‘the entire
class of living beings’; {#मनुष्यमात्रो#} <lbinfo n="ma#rtyaH"/>
{#मर्त्यः#} ‘every man is mortal’;  <lbinfo n="vastumAtrapa#kzako"/>
{#वस्तुमात्रपक्षकोऽनुप पंहारी (हेतुः)#} <ls>Tarka K.</ls>;
{#मानुषमात्रदुष्करं कर्मानुष्ठितं#} <ls>Dk.</ls> {@--3@} The
simple measure of anything, the
one thing and no more, often <lbinfo n="trans+lateable"/>
translateable by ‘mere,’ ‘only’, ‘even’;
{#जातिमात्रेण#} <ls>H. 1. 58</ls> ‘by mere caste’;
{#टिट्टिभमात्रेण समुद्रो व्याकुलीकृतः#} <ls n="H.">2. 149.</ls>
‘by a mere wag-tail’, {#वाचामात्रेण#} <lbinfo n="jA#pyase"/>
{#जाप्यसे#} <ls>Ś. 2</ls> ‘merely by words’; so
{#अर्थमात्रं, संमानमात्रं#} <ls>Pt. 1. 83</ls>; used
with past participles {#मात्र#} may be
translated by ‘as soon as’, ‘no
sooner than’, ‘just’; {#विद्धमात्रः#}  <lbinfo n="ls:R. 5.+ 51"/>
<ls>R. 5. 51</ls> ‘as soon as pierced’, ‘when just
pierced’; {#भुक्तामात्रे#} ‘just after eating’;
{#प्रविष्टमात्र एव तत्रमवति#} <ls>Ś. 3.</ls> &c.
