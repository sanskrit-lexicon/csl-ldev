<L>8810<pc>0329-a<k1>उपरिष्टात्<k2>उपरिष्टात्
{#उपरिष्टात्#}¦ {%<ab>ind.</ab>%} [<ab>cf.</ab> <ls>P. V. 3. 31</ls>]
{@1@} As an adverb it means ({%a%}) Over,
above, from above, on high;  <lbinfo n="parahi#takaraRe"/>
{#परहितकरणे नोपरिष्टान्न चाधः#} <ls>Bh. 3. 131</ls>;
<ls>Y. 1. 106.</ls> ({%b%}) Further or later on,
afterwards; {#कल्याणावतंसा हि कल्याण#}
{#संपदुपरिष्टाद्भवति#} <ls>Māl. 6</ls>; {#इदमुपरिष्टात्#}
{#व्याख्यातं#} in the sequel. ({%c%}) Behind
(<ab>opp.</ab> {#पुरस्तात्#}). {@--2@} (As a <lbinfo n="preposi+tion"/>
preposition) it means ({%a%}) Over, upon
(with <ab>gen.</ab>, rarely <ab>acc.</ab>); <ls>Śi. 11. 3.</ls> ({%b%})
Down upon. ({%c%}) Behind (with <ab>gen.</ab>).
{@--Comp.@} {#--ज्योतिष्मती#} <ab>N.</ab> of a
Vedic metre consisting of 43 or 44
syllables. {#--ज्योतिः#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%} a verse of
the Triṣṭubh metre, the last line
of which consists of eight syllables.
{#--बृहती#} <ab>N.</ab> of a Vedic metre <lbinfo n="hav+ing"/>
having four Pādas, the first of which
contains 12 syllables, each of the
three others having only eight.
