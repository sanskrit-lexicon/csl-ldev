{#आशिस्#}¦ {%<ab>f.</ab>%} ({#°शीः, °शीर्भ्यां#} &c.) [ <lbinfo n="A#SAs"/>
{#आशास्-क्विप्, अत इत्वम्#}] {@1@} A blessing, <lbinfo n="bene+diction"/>
benediction. (It is thus defined:
--{#वात्सल्याद्यत्र मान्येन कनिष्ठस्याभिधीयते ।#} <lbinfo n="izwA#vaDArakaM"/>
{#इष्टावधारकं वाक्यमाशीः सा परिकीतिंता ॥#}). {#आशिस्#}
is sometimes distinguished from {#वर#},
the former being taken to be merely
an expression of one's good wishes
which may or may not be realized;
while a {#वर#} is a boon which is more
permanent in character and surer of
fulfilment; <ab>cf.</ab> {#वरः खल्वेषनाशीः#} <ls>Ś. 4</ls>;
{#आशिषो गुरुजनवितीर्णा वरतामापद्यंते#}  <lbinfo n="ls:K.+ 291"/>
<ls>K. 291</ls>; {#अमोघाः प्रतिगृह्णंतावर्घ्यानुपदमाशिषः#}
<ls>R. 1. 44</ls>, <ls n="R.">11. 6</ls>; <ls>Ku. 5. 76</ls>, <ls n="Ku.">7. 47.</ls>
{@--2@} Act of bestowing a blessing <lbinfo n="up+on"/>
upon others. {@--3@} A prayer, wish, desire;
<ls>Ku. 5. 76</ls>, <ls>Bg. 4. 21</ls>, <ls n="Bg.">6. 10.</ls> {@--4@} A
serpent's fang (<ab>cf.</ab> {#आशी#}). {@--5@} One of
the eight chief medicaments ({#वृद्धि#}).
{@--Comp.@} {#--वादः, --वचनं#} ( <lbinfo n="ASI#rvAdaH"/>
{#आशीर्वादः#} &c.) a blessing, benediction,
expression of a prayer or wish;
{#आशीर्वचनसंयुक्तां नित्यं यस्मात् प्रकुर्वते#}
<ls>S. D. 6</ls>; <ls>Ms. 2. 33.</ls> {#--विषः#} ({#आशीर्विषः#})
‘having poison in its fangs,’ a snake.
