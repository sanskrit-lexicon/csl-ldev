{#अंध्र#}¦ <ab>N.</ab> of a people and their country. It is said to be
the same as the modern Telangaṇa, and the
mouths of the Godāvarī were in the possession of
the Andhras. But the limits were probably <lbinfo n="con+fined"/>
confined to the Ghāts on the west, and the rivers
Godāvarī and Kṛṣṇā on the north and south. It
bordered on Kaliṅga; (see <ls>Dk. 7</ls>th Ullāsa,) and
its capital {#अंध्रनगर#} is probably the old town of
Veṅgi or Vegi.
