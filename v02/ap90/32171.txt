{#सह्म#}¦ <ab>N.</ab> of a country which lay to the west of Vaṅga.
Its capital {#ताम्रलिप्त#} (also called {#तामलिप्त, दामलिप्त#}, <lbinfo n="tAma#liptI"/>
{#तामलिप्ती#} and {#तमालिनी#}) is identified with the modern
Tumlook on the right bank of the Cossye, which
is the same as the {#कपिशा#} of Kālidāsa. In ancient
times the town was situated nearer to the sea,.
and was a place of considerable maritime trade.
The Suhmas are sometimes called Rāḍhas, the
people of Western Bengal.
