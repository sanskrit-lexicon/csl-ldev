{#द्रविड#}¦ <ab>N.</ab> of a country to the south of the wild tract
between the Kṛṣṇā and the Polar. In its larger
sense it included the whole of the Coromandel
coast to the south of the Godāvarī. But in its
strict sense it must not have extended beyond the
Kāverī. Its capital was Kāñcī, the same as
Conjeveram situated on the Vegavatī river 42
miles south-west of Madras.
