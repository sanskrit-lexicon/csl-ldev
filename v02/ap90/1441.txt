{#अनिरुद्ध#}¦ {%<ab>a.</ab>%} Unobstructed, free,
uncontrolled, self-willed, unruly, <lbinfo n="un+governable"/>
ungovernable. {#--द्धः#} {@1@} A spy, secret <lbinfo n="emis+sary"/>
emissary. {@--2@} <ab>N.</ab> of a son of Pradyumna.
[Aniruddha was the son of Kāma and
grandson of Kṛṣṇa. Uṣā, the daughter
of a demon named Bāṇa, fell in love with
him and had him brought by magic <lbinfo n="influ+ence"/>
influence to her apartments in her father's city
of Śoṇitapura. Bāṇa sent some guards to
seize him, but the brave youth slew his
assailants with only an iron club. At last,
however, he was secured by means of <lbinfo n="mag+ic"/>
magic powers. On discovering where <lbinfo n="Ani+ruddha"/>
Aniruddha had been carried, Kṛṣṇa, <lbinfo n="Bala+rāma"/>
Balarāma and Kāma went to rescue him and
a great battle was fought. Bāṇa, though
aided by Śiva and Skanda, was <lbinfo n="van+quished"/>
vanquished, but his life was spared at the
intercession of Śiva, and Aniruddha was
carried home to Dvārakā with Uṣā as
his wife. He had also another wife <lbinfo n="Ro+chanā"/>
Rochanā, grand-daughter of king Rukmin
of Vidarbha, who bore him a son named
Vajra.]. {@--3@} Also <ab>N.</ab> of Viṣṇu; and
of Śiva; of an Arhat, a <lbinfo n="contem+porary"/>
contemporary of Buddha. {#--द्धं#} A cord
or rope (for fastening cattle).
