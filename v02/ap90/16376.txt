{#नरकः, --कं#}¦ Hell, infernal regions;
(corresponding to the realm of
Pluto; there are said to be 21 <lbinfo n="dif+ferent"/>
different parts of these regions where
different kinds of tortures are <lbinfo n="in+flicted"/>
inflicted upon sinners). {#--कः#} <ab>N.</ab> of a
demon, king of Prāgjyotiṣa.
[According to one account he carried off
Aditi's ear-rings and Kṛṣṇa at the <lbinfo n="re+quest"/>
request of the gods killed him in a single
combat and recovered the jewels. <lbinfo n="Ac+cording"/>
According to another account, Naraka <lbinfo n="as+sumed"/>
assumed the form of an elephant and <lbinfo n="car+ried"/>
carried off the daughter of Viśvakarman and
outraged her. He also seized the daughters
of Gandharvas, gods, men and the
nymphs themselves, and collected more
than 16000 damsels in his harem. These,
it is related, were transferred by <lbinfo n="Kṛsh+ṇa"/>
Kṛshṇa to his own harem after he had slain
Naraka. The demon was born of earth,
and hence called ‘Bhauma.’]. {@--Comp.@}
