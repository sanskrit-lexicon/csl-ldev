{#विदेह#}¦ <ab>N.</ab> of a country lying to the north-east of <lbinfo n="Maga+dha"/>
Magadha. Its capital Mithilā is the same as Janakapur
in Nepal north of Madhuvāṇī. Videha must have
covered, in ancient times, besides a portion of
Nepal, all such places as Sītāmāri, Sītākuṇḍa, or the
northern part of the old district of Trihut and the
north-western portion of Champaran.
