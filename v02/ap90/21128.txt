{#प्रातिशाख्यं#}¦ A grammatical <lbinfo n="trea+tise"/>
treatise laying down rules for the
phonetic changes which words in
any Śākhā of the Vedas undergo,
and teaching the mode of <lbinfo n="pronounc+ing"/>
pronouncing the accents &c. (There exist four
Prātiśākhyas, one for the Śākala
branch of Ṛgveda, one for each of
the two branches of the Yajurveda,
and one for the Atharvaveda).
