{#वाह्लीक, वाहीक#}¦ A general name for the tribes <lbinfo n="inhabit+ing"/>
inhabiting the Punjab. Their country is the modern
Bactria or Balkh. In the Bhārata they are said to
have inhabited the country watered by the Indus
and the five rivers of the Punjab outside ‘holy’
India. The country was noted for its breed of horses
and asa-fœtida.
