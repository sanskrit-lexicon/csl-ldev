{#प्र#}¦ {%<ab>ind.</ab>%} {@1@} As a prefix to verbs it
means ‘forward’, ‘forth’, ‘in
front’, ‘onward’, ‘before’, ‘away’,
as in {#प्रगम्, प्रस्या, प्रचर्, प्रय#} &c. {@--2@}
With adjectives it means ‘very’,
‘excessively’, ‘very much’ &c:  <lbinfo n="prakf#zwa"/>
{#प्रकृष्ट, प्रमत्त#} &c., see further on. {@--3@} With
nouns whether derived from verbs
or not, it is used in the following
senses according to <ls>G. M.</ls>:-- ({%a%})
beginning, commencement; ({#प्रमाणं#},
{#प्रस्थानं, प्रह्ण#}); ({%b%}) length; ( <lbinfo n="pravAlaBUzi#ka"/>
{#प्रवालभूषिक#}); ({%c%}) power ({#प्रभु#}); ({%d%}) <lbinfo n="inten+sity"/>
intensity, excess; ({#प्रवाद, प्रकर्ष, प्रच्छाय#}, <lbinfo n="pra#guRa"/>
{#प्रगुण#}); ({%e%}) source or origin; ({#प्रभव, प्रपौत्र#});
({%f%}) completion, perfectness, <lbinfo n="satis+faction"/>
satisfaction ({#प्रभुक्तमन्नं#}); ({%g%}) destitution,
separation, being without; ({#प्रोषिता#},
{#प्रपर्णवृक्षः#}); ({%h%}) apart; ({#प्रज्ञु#}); ({%i%})
excellence; ({#प्राचार्यः#}): ({%j%}) purity
({#प्रसन्नं जलं#}); ({%k%}) wish ({#प्रार्थना#}); ({%l%})
cessation ({#प्रशम#}); ({%<ab>m.</ab>%}) adoration,
respect; ({#प्रांजलिः#} who respectfully
folds his hands together); ({%<ab>n.</ab>%}) <lbinfo n="pro+minence"/>

it is often used as a separable <lbinfo n="ad+verb"/>
