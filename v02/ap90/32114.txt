{#कुरुक्षेत्र#}¦ <ab>N.</ab> of an extensive region or plain near Delhi;
the scene of the great war between the Pāṇḍavas
and Kauravas. It is the tract near the holy lake
called by the same name lying to the south of
Thāneshvar, and extended from the south of the
Sarasvatī to the north of the Dṛṣadvatī. It is
sometimes called {#समंतपंचक#} the tract of the ‘five
pools’ of blood of the Kṣatriyas slain by
Paraśurāma.
