Asura, son of Kaśyapa and Diti

{#प्रोचुस्तत्र निवासिनः ॥#} He was slain by
Śiva when he attempted to carry off the
Pārijāta tree from heaven; whence Śiva
is called {%Andhakaripu%}, {%-ari%}, {%-dvish%} &c. <lbinfo n="Ac+cording"/>
