{@1@} The second deity of the sacred
Triad, entrusted with the <lbinfo n="preserva+tion"/>
preservation of the world, which duty he
is represented to have duly <lbinfo n="dis+charged"/>
discharged by his various incarnations;
(for their descriptions see the <lbinfo n="sever+al"/>
several avatāras <ab>s. v.</ab> and also under
{#अवतार#}); the word is thus popularly
derived: --{#यस्माद्विश्वमिदं सर्वं तस्य शक्त्या#}
{#महात्मनः । तस्मादेवोच्यते विष्णुर्वीशधातोः#} <lbinfo n="praveSa#nAt"/>
{#प्रवेशनात् ॥#}. {@--2@} <ab>N.</ab> of Agni. {@--3@} A pious man.
{@--4@} <ab>N.</ab> of a law-giver, author of a
Smṛti called {#विष्णुस्मृति#}. {@--5@} <ab>N.</ab> of one
of the Vasus. {@--6@} The lunar mansion
called Śravaṇa (presided over by 
[Page1000-b+ 55]
Viṣṇu). {@--Comp.@} {#--कांची#} <ab>N.</ab> of a
town. {#--क्रमः#} the step or stride of <lbinfo n="Vi+shṇu"/>
Viṣṇu. {#--गुप्तः#} <ab>N.</ab> of Cāṇakya. {#--तैलं#}
a kind of medicinal oil. {#--दैवत्या#} <ab>N.</ab>
of the eleventh and twelfth days
of each fortnight (of a lunar
month). {#--पदं#} {1} the sky, <lbinfo n="atmos+phere"/>
atmosphere. {2} the sea of milk. {3} the
foot of Viṣṇu (worshipped at
Gayā). {4} a lotus. {#--पदी#} an <lbinfo n="epi+thet"/>
epithet of the Ganges. {#--पुराणं#} <ab>N.</ab> of
one of the most celebrated of the
eighteen Purāṇas. {#--प्रीतिः#} {%<ab>f.</ab>%} land
granted rent-free to Brāhmaṇas to
maintain Viṣṇu's worship. {#--माया#}
<ab>N.</ab> of Durgā. {#--रथः#} an epithet of
Garuḍa. {#--रातः#} <ab>N.</ab> of king <lbinfo n="Parī+kshit"/>
Parīkshit. {#--लिंगी#} a quail. {#--लोकः#} <lbinfo n="Vi+shṇu"/>
Vishṇu's world. {#--वल्लभा#} {1} an <lbinfo n="epi+thet"/>
epithet of Lakṣmī. {2} the holy basil.
{#--वाहनः, --वाह्यः#} epithets of Garuḍa.
