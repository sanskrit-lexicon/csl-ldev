{@Marutta Āvi-kṣita@}¦ (‘descendant of Avikṣit’) {@Kāma-pri@}
(‘descendant of Kāmapra’) is the name of a king who was
anointed by {@Saṃvarta@} according to the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa.<sup>1</sup>
In the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> account of the same king he is
called {@Āyogava.@}
<F>1) viii. 21, 12.</F>
<F>2) xiii. 5, 4, 6. {%Cf.%} also Śāṅkhāyana
Śrauta Sūtra, xvi. 9, 14. 16; Maitrāyaṇī
Upaniṣad, i. 4.</F>
