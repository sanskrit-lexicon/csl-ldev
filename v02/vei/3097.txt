{@Vaiṣṭha-pureya,@}¦ ‘descendant of Viṣṭhapura,’ is the name of
a teacher in the first two Vaṃśas (lists of teachers) of the
Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad in the Mādhyaṃdina recension (ii. 5,
20; iv. 5, 25). He was a pupil of {@Śāṇḍilya@} and {@Rauhiṇāyana.@}
