{@Devṛ@}¦ is a rare word denoting the wife's ‘brother-in-law’
<div n="lb"/>(that is, the husband's brother). He is included with the
<div n="lb"/>sisters of the husband among those over whom the wife of the
<div n="lb"/>husband — his elder brother — rules;<sup>1</sup> at the same time the wife
<div n="lb"/>is to be devoted to him,<sup>2</sup> and friendly to him.<sup>3</sup> After the death
[Page1-379+ 50]
<div n="lb"/>of the husband the Devṛ could perform the duty of begetting
<div n="lb"/>a son for him.<sup>4</sup> No word occurs for the wife's brother
<div n="lb"/>corresponding to Devṛ.
