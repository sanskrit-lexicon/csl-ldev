<L>84<pc>1-025<k1>अपस्कम्भ<k2>अपस्कम्भ
{@Apa-skambha.@}¦ — The word occurs only in one passage of the
<div n="lb"/>Atharvaveda, where the tip of it is mentioned as poisoned.
<div n="lb"/>Roth<sup>1</sup> suggests that the fastening of the arrow-point to the
<div n="lb"/>shaft is meant. Whitney<sup>2</sup> inclines to this version, but suggests
<div n="lb"/>corruption of text. Zimmer<sup>3</sup> follows Roth. Ludwig<sup>4</sup> renders
<div n="lb"/>the word by ‘barb.’ Bloomfield<sup>5</sup> thinks it means ‘tearing
<div n="lb"/>(arrow),’ a sense deduced from the etymology.
