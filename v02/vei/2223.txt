2. {@Brāhmaṇa,@}¦ ‘religious explanation,’<sup>1</sup> is the title of a class
of books which as such are only mentioned in the Nirukta<sup>2</sup> and
the Taittirīya Āraṇyaka,<sup>3</sup> and then in the Sūtras, where the
names of the Brāhmaṇas occur, showing that literary works
were in existence.

45, 8; vi. 25, 1, etc.; Taittirīya Saṃhitā,
iii. 1, 9, 5; 5, 2, 1; Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa,
iii. 2, 4, 1, etc. In the Kauṣītaki
Brāhmaṇa and the Śāṅkhāyana Āraṇ-
yaka, i. and ii., the use is constant.</F>
<F>2) ii. 16; xiii. 7.</F>
<F>3) ii. 10.</F>
