{@Proṣṭha,@}¦ denoting perhaps a ‘bench,’ is found in the Rigveda<sup>1</sup>
in the adjective {%proṣṭhaśaya,%} ‘lying on a bench,’ used of women,
and uncompounded in the Taittīriya Brāhmaṇa.<sup>2</sup> In the first
passage it is distinguished from {@Talpa@} and {@Vahya,@} but what
the exact difference was there is not sufficient evidence to
show.
