‘cowstall’ as the ‘grazing ground of cows,’ as Geldner<sup>1</sup> shows

Mahīdhara on the Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā.<sup>3</sup> This sense suits
adequately all the passages of the Rigveda<sup>4</sup> where it occurs,
and it greatly improves the interpretation of a hymn of the
Atharvaveda,<sup>5</sup> besides being acceptable elsewhere.<sup>6</sup> See also {@Go.@}
