{@Aupoditi,@}¦ ‘descendant of Upodita,’ is the patronymic applied
in the Taittirīya Saṃhitā<sup>1</sup> to {@Tumiñja,@} and in the Baudhāyana
Śrauta Sūtra<sup>2</sup> to {@Gaupālāyana,@} son of {@Vyāghrapad,@} Sthapati
(‘general’) of the Kurus. In the form of Aupoditeya, a metro-
nymic from Upoditā, the name is found in the Śatapatha
Brāhmaṇa,<sup>3</sup> where the Kāṇva text calls him Tumiñja Aupo-
diteya Vaiyāghrapadya.
