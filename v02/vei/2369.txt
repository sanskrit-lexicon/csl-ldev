‘particles of light’ or ‘shining motes’ that fill the air, as
opposed to rays of light ({%raśmi%}). This meaning adequately-
suits the passages in the early Vedic literature<sup>2</sup> where the

the Upaniṣads,<sup>3</sup> as well as the older sense.<sup>4</sup>
