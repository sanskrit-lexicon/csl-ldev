<L>244<pc>1-059<k1>आमिक्षा<k2>आमिक्षा
{@Āmikṣā@}¦ designates a mess of clotted curds. It is not known
to the Rigveda, but occurs in all the later Saṃhitās,<sup>1</sup> Brāh-
maṇas,<sup>2</sup> etc., and is associated with the Vaiśya in the Taittirīya
Āraṇyaka.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) Av. x. 9, 13; Taittirīya Saṃhitā,
ii. 5, 5, 4; iii. 3, 9, 2; vi. 2, 5, 3;
Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā, ii. 1, 9; Vāja-
saneyi Saṃhitā, xix. 21; 23, etc.</F>
<F>2) Satapatha Brāhmaṇa, i. 8, 1, 7. 9;
iii. 3, 3, 2, etc.; Taittirīya Āraṇyaka,
ii. 8, 8; Jaiminīya Brāhmaṇa, ii. 438
({%Journal of the American Oriental Society,%}
19, 99, 101); Chāndogya Upaniṣad,
viii. 8, 5, etc.</F>
<F>3) {%Loc. cit. Cf.%} Mānava Srauta Sūtra,
ii. 2, 40.</F>
