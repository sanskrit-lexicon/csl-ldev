{@Tārṣṭāgha,@}¦ a species of tree, is mentioned in the Kauśika
Sūtra,<sup>1</sup> while the adjective formed from it, {%tārṣṭāghī,%} ‘derived
from the Tārṣṭāgha tree,’ is found in the Atharvaveda.<sup>2</sup>
Weber<sup>3</sup> thinks that the {%sarṣapa,%} or mustard plant, is meant.
