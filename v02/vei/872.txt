<L>872<pc>1-226<k1>गार्गीपुत्र<k2>गार्गीपुत्र
{@Gārgī-putra,@}¦ ‘son of Gargī,’ occurs as the name of three
teachers in the last Vaṃśa (list of teachers) in the Mādhyaṃdina
recension of the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad (vi. 4, 30). The
earliest of these three was the pupil of {@Bāḍeyīputra@} and the
teacher of the second Gārgīputra. The latter was the teacher
of {@Pārāśarīkauṇḍinīputra,@} the teacher of the third Gārgīputra.
