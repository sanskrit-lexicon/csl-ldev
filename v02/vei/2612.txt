{@Rajana Koṇeya,@}¦ or {@Kauṇeya,@} is the name of a teacher
mentioned in the Yajurveda Saṃhitās.<sup>1</sup> It is said in the
Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā<sup>2</sup> that {@Kratujit Jānaki@} successfully sacrificed
for him when he desired to obtain eyesight. He is also
mentioned in the Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa,<sup>3</sup> where the name of
his son, {@Ugradeva Rājani,@} also occurs.<sup>4</sup>
