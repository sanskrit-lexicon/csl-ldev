{@Nava-nīta,@}¦ ‘fresh butter,’ is mentioned frequently in the
later Saṃhitās<sup>1</sup> and the Brāhmaṇas.<sup>2</sup> According to the
Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>3</sup> this is the kind of butter which is fitted
for anointing an embryo ({%garbha%}), while the gods receive
{@Ājya,@} men fragrant ghee ({@Ghṛta@}), and the fathers {@Āyuta.@}
Elsewhere<sup>4</sup> it is contrasted with Ghṛta and {@Sarpis.@}
