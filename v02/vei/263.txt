<div n="lb"/>months, years,;<sup>3</sup> ‘half-months, months, Ārtavas, seasons’;<sup>4</sup>

<div n="lb"/>‘seasons, Ārtavas, months, half-months, days and nights, day’;<sup>5</sup>
<div n="lb"/>and in the Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā ‘months, seasons, Ārtavas, the
<div n="lb"/>year,’<sup>6</sup> or simply with the seasons.<sup>7</sup>
