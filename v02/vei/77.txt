<L>77<pc>1-023<k1>अन्तेवासिन्<k2>अन्तेवासिन्
{@Ante-vāsin,@}¦ ‘dwelling near,’ is the epithet of the {@Brahma-@}
{@cārin@} who lives in the house of his teacher. The expression
does not occur before the late Brāhmaṇa period.<sup>1</sup> Secrecy is
often enjoined on others than Antevāsins.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Satapatha Brāhmaṇa, v. 1, 5, 17;
Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad, vi. 3, 7;
Chāndogya Upaniṣad, iii. 11, 5; iv.
10, 1; Taittirīya Upaniṣad, i. 3, 3;
11, 1.</F>
<F>2) Aitareya Āraṇyaka, iii. 2, 6; Sāṅkh-
āyana Āraṇyaka, viii, 11.</F>
