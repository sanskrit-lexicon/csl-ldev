{@Nalada,@}¦ ‘nard’ ({%Nardastachys Jatamansi%}) is a plant mentioned
in the Atharvaveda,<sup>1</sup> in the Aitareya<sup>2</sup> and the Śāṅkhāyana<sup>3</sup>
Āraṇyakas (where it is mentioned as used for a garland), as well as
in the Sūtras. In the Atharvaveda<sup>4</sup> the feminine form of the word,
Naladī, occurs as the name of an Apsaras, or celestial nymph.
<F>1) vi. 102, 3.</F>
<F>2) iii. 2, 4.</F>
<F>3) xi. 4.</F>
<F>4) iv. 37, 3.
