<L>852<pc>1-222<k1>गर्मुत्<k2>गर्मुत्
{@Garmut@}¦ is the name of a kind of wild bean mentioned in the
Taittirīya Saṃhitā.<sup>1</sup> The Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā<sup>2</sup> has the form
Ganmut, which is probably a false reading. The adjective
{%gārmuta,%} ‘made from the Garmut bean,’ is found in the
Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā.<sup>3</sup>
