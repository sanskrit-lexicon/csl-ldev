{@Himavant,@}¦ ‘snowy,’ appears as an epithet of mountains in
<div n="lb"/>the Atharvaveda.<sup>1</sup> It is also used both there<sup>2</sup> and in the
<div n="lb"/>Rigveda,<sup>3</sup> as well as later,<sup>4</sup> as a noun. There seems no reason
<div n="lb"/>to deny that in all the passages the word refers vaguely to the
<div n="lb"/>mountains now called Himālaya, though it is possible that the
<div n="lb"/>name may include mountains not strictly in that system, like
<div n="lb"/>the Suleiman hills.<sup>5</sup> See also {@Mūjavant@} and {@Trikakubh.@}

<F>2) vi. 95, 3. See also iv. 9, 9; v. 4,
<div n="lb"/>2. 8; 25. 7; vi. 24, 1 (where reference

<div n="lb"/>xix. 39, 1.</F>
<F>3) x. 121, 4.</F>
<F>4) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, v. 5, 11, 1;
