<L>197<pc>1-048<k1>असितमृग<k2>असितमृग
{@Asita-mṛga@}¦ is the designation in the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup>
of a family of the {@Kaśyapas@} who were excluded from a sacrifice
by {@Janamejaya,@} but who took away the conduct of the offering
from the {@Bhūtavīras,@} whom the king employed. In the
Jaiminīya Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> and the Ṣaḍviṃsa Brāhmaṇa<sup>3</sup> the Asita-
mṛgas are called ‘sons of the Kaśyapas,’ and one is mentioned
as {@Kusurubindu@}<sup>4</sup> {@Auddālaki.@}
<F>1) vii. 27. {%Cf.%} Eggeling, {%Sacred Books%}
{%of the East,%} 43, 345.</F>
<F>2) i. 75.</F>
<F>3) i. 4.</F>
<F>4) Variously read as Asurbinda,
Kusurbinda, Kusurubinda.</F>
