1. {@Yava@}¦ in the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> appears to be a generic term for
any sort of ‘grain,’ and not merely ‘barley.’ The latter sense
is probably found in the Atharvaveda,<sup>2</sup> and is regular later.
The barley harvest came after spring,<sup>3</sup> in the summer.<sup>4</sup> That
barley was cultivated in the period of the Rigveda<sup>5</sup> is not
certain, but on the whole very probable.<sup>6</sup>
