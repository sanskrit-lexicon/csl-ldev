<L>741<pc>1-191<k1>कौण्डिन्यायन<k2>कौण्डिन्यायन
{@Kauṇḍinyāyana@}¦ is mentioned in the first Vaṃśa (list of
teachers) of the Mādhyaṃdina recension of the Bṛhadāraṇyaka
Upaniṣad<sup>1</sup> as a pupil of Kauṇḍinya, pupil of Kauṇḍinya and
Āgniveśya; in the second<sup>2</sup> as a pupil of the two Kauṇḍinyas,
pupil of Kauṇḍinya and Āgniveśya. Neither Vaṃśa is of much
value.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) ii. 5, 20.</F>
<F>2) iv. 5, 26.</F>
<F>3) Eggeling, {%Sacred Books of the East,%}
12, xxxiv.</F>
