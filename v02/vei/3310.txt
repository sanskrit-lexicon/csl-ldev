{@Śutudrī,@}¦ twice mentioned in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> is the name of
the most easterly river of the Panjab, the modern Sutlej, the
Zaradros of Ptolemy and Arrian.<sup>2</sup> In the post-Vedic period

very considerably within historical times.<sup>3</sup>
