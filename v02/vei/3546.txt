<div n="lb"/>a collection of verses for chanting, often mentioned in
<div n="lb"/>the Brāhmaṇas.<sup>1</sup> The Sāman itself is repeatedly referred
<div n="lb"/>to in the Rigveda,<sup>2</sup> and the triad Ṛc, Yajus, and Sāman
<div n="lb"/>is common from the Atharvaveda onwards.<sup>3</sup> These texts
<div n="lb"/>know also the Sāma-ga, the ‘Sāman-chanter,’<sup>4</sup> who occurs
<div n="lb"/>later.<sup>5</sup>
