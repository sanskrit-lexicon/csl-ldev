{@Kauravya@}¦ (‘belonging to the {@Kurus’@}). A man of the Kuru
people, is described in the Atharvaveda as enjoying prosperity
with his wife under the rule of King {@Parikṣit.@}<sup>1</sup> Mention is also
made of the Kauravya king {@Balhika Prātipīya@} in the Śatapatha
Brāhmaṇa,<sup>2</sup> and in the later legend {@Ārṣṭiṣeṇa@} and {@Devāpi@} are
alleged to have been Kauravyas.<sup>3</sup>
