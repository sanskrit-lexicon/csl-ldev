<L>1967<pc>2-028<k1>प्रउग<k2>प्रउग
{@Praūga@}¦ is apparently equivalent to {%pra-yuga,%} denoting the
fore part of the pole of the cart, the part in front of the yoke.
It is mentioned in the Yajurveda Saṃhitās<sup>1</sup> and the Śatapatha
Brāhmaṇa,<sup>2</sup> where it is said to be the part of the pole behind
the {@Kastambhī,@} or prop on which the pole rests.
<F>1) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, v. 4, 11, 1, 2;
Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā, xxi. 4.</F>
<F>2) Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa, i. 1, 2, 9;
iii. 5, 3, 4, etc.
<div n="P"/>{%Cf.%} Zimmer, {%Altindisches Leben,%} 248;
Eggeling, {%Sacred Books of the East,%} 12,
14, n. 1.</F>
