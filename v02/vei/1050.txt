<L>1050<pc>1-284<k1>जामदग्निय<k2>जामदग्निय
{@Jāmadagniya@}¦ is the patronymic of two ‘descendants of
Jamadagni’ in the Taittirīya Saṃhitā.<sup>1</sup> It appears from the
Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>2</sup> that the {@Aurvas@} are meant, and that
Jamadagni's descendants were ever prosperous.
<F>1) vii. 1, 9, 1.</F>
<F>2) xxi. 10, 6.
