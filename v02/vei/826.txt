<div n="lb"/>Weber<sup>1</sup> thinks, the {%Phaselus radiatus.%} It is mentioned with
<div n="lb"/>other grains of all sorts in the Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā,<sup>2</sup> and as
<div n="lb"/>being crushed with the {@Dṛṣad@} in the Atharvaveda.<sup>3</sup> It occurs
<div n="lb"/>also in the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad,<sup>4</sup> where Śaṅkara glosses
<div n="lb"/>it with {%niṣpāva.%}
