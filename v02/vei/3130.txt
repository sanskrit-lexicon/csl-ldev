{@Śakuni,@}¦ ‘bird,’ is used practically like {@Śakuna,@} but with a
much clearer reference to divination. It was smaller than the
{@Śyena@} or {@Suparṇa,@}<sup>1</sup> gave signs,<sup>2</sup> and foretold ill-luck.<sup>3</sup> When
it is mentioned<sup>4</sup> in the list of sacrificial victims at the Aśva-

later the falcon is so called, but the ‘raven’ may be intended;
the commentator on the Taittirīya Saṃhitā thinks that it is
the ‘crow.’ It is mentioned several times elsewhere.<sup>5</sup>
