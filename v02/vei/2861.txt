{@Vāja-śravasa,@}¦ ‘descendant of {@Vājaśravas,@}’ is the patro-
nymic of {@Kuśri@} in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa.<sup>1</sup> It is also the
patronymic of the father of {@Naciketas@} in the Taittirīya

understood by Sāyaṇa as a participle in the sense of ‘desiring.’

been sages.<sup>3</sup> They were {@Gotamas.@}<sup>4</sup>
