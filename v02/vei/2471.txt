<L>2471<pc>2-155<k1>मार्गार<k2>मार्गार
{@Mārgāra@}¦ is the name of one of the victims at the Paruṣa-
medha (‘human sacrifice’) in the Yajurveda.<sup>1</sup> The sense of the
word is apparently ‘hunter,’ or possibly ‘fisherman,’<sup>2</sup> as a
patronymic from {%mṛgāri,%} ‘enemy of wild beasts.’
<F>1) Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, xxx. 16; Tait-
tirīya Brāhmaṇa, iii. 4, 12, 1.</F>
<F>2) {%Cf.%} Sāyaṇa on Taittirīya Brāh-
maṇa, {%loc. cit.%}</F>
