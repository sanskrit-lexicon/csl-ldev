<L>126<pc>1-034<k1>अरत्नि<k2>अरत्नि
{@Aratni.@}¦ — This word, which primarily means ‘elbow,’ occurs
frequently from the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> onwards as denoting a measure of
length (‘ell’ or ‘cubit’), the distance from the elbow to the tip
of the hand. The exact length nowhere appears from the early
texts.
<F>1) Rv. viii. 80, 8; Av. xix. 57, 6;
Aitareya Brāhmaṇa, viii. 5; Śatapatha
Brāhmaṇa, vi. 3, 1, 33, etc. For
Rv. viii. 80, 8, see also Āji.</F>
