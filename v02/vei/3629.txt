<L>3629<pc>2-460<k1>सुवास्तु<k2>सुवास्तु
{@Su-vāstu@}¦ (‘having fair dwellings’) is the name of a river in the
Rigveda.<sup>1</sup> It is clearly the Soastos of Arrian<sup>2</sup> and the modern
Swāt, a tributary of the {@Kubhā@} (Kabul river) which is itself an
affluent of the Indus.
<F>1) viii. 19, 37; Nirukta, iv. 15.</F>
<F>2) {%Indica,%} iv. 11.
