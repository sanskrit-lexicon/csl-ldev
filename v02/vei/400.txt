<L>400<pc>1-103<k1>उशनस्काव्य<k2>उशनस्काव्य
{@Uśanas Kāvya@}¦ is an ancient seer, already a half-mythical
figure in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> where he is often mentioned, especially
as associated with Kutsa and Indra. Later on<sup>2</sup> he becomes
the Purohita of the Asuras in their contests with the gods.
A variant of his name is Kavi Uśanas.<sup>3</sup> He appears in the
Brāhmaṇas as a teacher also.<sup>4</sup>
