<L>2615<pc>2-198<k1>रजस्<k2>रजस्<h>1
1. {@Rajas@}¦ denotes the region of the atmosphere between
heaven and earth in the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> and later.<sup>2</sup> The atmosphere,
like the sky ({@Div@}), is divided into three regions,<sup>3</sup> but more
normally into two, the ‘earthly’ ({%pārthiva%})<sup>4</sup> and the ‘heavenly’
({%divya%} or {%divaḥ%}).<sup>5</sup> In some passages<sup>6</sup> the word refers in the
plural to the dusty fields on earth.
