1. {@Pra-vara@}¦ denotes properly the ‘summons’ addressed to
Agni at the beginning of the sacrifice to perform his functions.
But as Agni was then invoked by the names of the ancestors of
the Purohita,<sup>1</sup> the term Pravara denotes the series of ancestors
invoked.<sup>2</sup>
