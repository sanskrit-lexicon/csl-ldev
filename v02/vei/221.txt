<div n="lb"/>which came from Mount {@Trikakubh@}<sup>2</sup> in the Himālaya, and was
<div n="lb"/>used to anoint the eyes.<sup>3</sup> The region of the {@Yamunā@}<sup>4</sup> is also

<div n="lb"/>diseases.<sup>5</sup> A female ointment-maker is mentioned in the list of
<div n="lb"/>victims of the {@Puruṣamedha@} (‘human sacrifice’).<sup>6</sup>
