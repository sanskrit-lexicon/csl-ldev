{@Yama@}¦ denotes ‘twins,’ the birth of which is frequently
alluded to in Vedic literature.<sup>1</sup> Twins of different sex seem to
be indicated by the expression {%yamau mithunau.%}<sup>2</sup> There are
traces of the belief — widely spread among negro and other
races — that twins are uncanny and of evil omen,<sup>3</sup> but there are
also vestiges of the opposite opinion, that twins are lucky.<sup>4</sup>
