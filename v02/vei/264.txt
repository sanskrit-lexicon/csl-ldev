<div n="lb"/>({%jyā%}) was attached.<sup>1</sup> The string was not normally kept
<div n="lb"/>fastened to both ends of the bow, but when an arrow was to
<div n="lb"/>be shot it was strung taut.<sup>2</sup> On the other hand, the legend
