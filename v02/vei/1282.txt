{@Dātra@}¦ (‘cutter’), denoting a ‘sickle,’ is mentioned in the
Rigveda.<sup>1</sup> Cows ‘with sickle-shaped marks on their ears’
({%dātra-karṇyaḥ%}) are referred to in the Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā.<sup>2</sup>
Otherwise the expression is only found later, occurring in the
Sūtra and epic literature.<sup>3</sup> See also {@Sṛṇi.@}
