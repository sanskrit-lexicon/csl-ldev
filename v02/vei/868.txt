{@Gāthina.@}¦ — The sons of Viśvāmitra are described in the
Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup> as Gāthinas, or descendants of Gāthin,
who, according to tradition, was their grandfather;<sup>2</sup> and
{@Viśvāmitra@} himself is styled Gāthina in the Sarvānukramaṇī.

{%et seq.;%} Pargiter, {%Journal of the Royal%}
