{@Niṣṭya@}¦ means in the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> and later<sup>2</sup> an outsider or
stranger. Hence the constellation usually known as Svāti
(see {@Nakṣatra@}) is named Niṣṭyā in the Taittirīya Brāhmaṇa,<sup>3</sup>
because it occupies a position markedly away from the ecliptic.
