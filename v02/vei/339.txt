<L>339<pc>1-084<k1>उत्तरमद्र<k2>उत्तरमद्र
{@Uttara Madra@}¦ is the name of a tribe mentioned with the
{@Uttara Kurus@} in the Aitareya Brāhmaṇa<sup>1</sup> as living beyond the
Himālaya. Zimmer<sup>2</sup> points out that in the Vaṃśa Brāhmaṇa<sup>3</sup>
{@Kāmboja Aupamanyava@} is a pupil of {@Madragāra,@} and thence
[Page1-085+ 40]
infers that Kambojas and Madras were not far distant in space.
This conclusion is perfectly reasonable in view of the probable
position of the Kāmbojas.<sup>4</sup>
