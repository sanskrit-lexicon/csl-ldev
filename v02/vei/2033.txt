{@Pra-vāta,@}¦ ‘a windy spot,’ is mentioned in the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> as
the place where the {@Vibhītaka@} nuts, used as dice ({@Akṣa@}) grow.
In the Taittirīya Saṃhitā<sup>2</sup> reference is made to the exposure of
decaying matter in such a place.
<F>1) x. 34, 1; Nirukta, ix. 8. Geldner,
{%Rigveda, Glossar,%} 119, thinks the refer-
ence here is to nuts being blown down
in a storm of wind.</F>
<F>2) vi. 4, 7, 2.</F>
