<L>592<pc>1-151<k1>कार्शकेयीपुत्र<k2>कार्शकेयीपुत्र
{@Kārśakeyī-putra@}¦ (‘son of Kārśakeyī’) is the name of a man
mentioned in the last Vaṃśa (list of teachers) of the Bṛhadā-
raṇyaka Upaniṣad. In the Kāṇva (vi. 5, 2) recension he is a
pupil of {@Prācīnayogīputra;@} in the Mādhyaṃdina (vi. 4, 33)
recension his teacher's name is {@Prāśnīputra Āsurivāsin.@}
