<L>3118<pc>2-342<k1>व्रातपती<k2>व्रातपती
{@Vrāta-patī,@}¦ ‘lord of troops,’ is an epithet included in the
names of Rudra in the Yajurveda Saṃhitās,<sup>1</sup> along with the
epithet Gaṇa-pati, ‘lord of groups.’ The exact sense is quite
uncertain, but the term may allude to the chief of a band of
robbers, as Zimmer<sup>2</sup> thinks.
<F>1) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, iv. 5, 4, 1;
Kāṭhaka Saṃhitā, xvii. 13; Maitrāyaṇī
Saṃhitā, ii. 9, 4; Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā,
xvi. 25.</F>
<F>2) {%Altindisches Leben,%} 179.</F>
