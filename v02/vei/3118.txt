{@Vrāta-patī,@}¦ ‘lord of troops,’ is an epithet included in the
<div n="lb"/>names of Rudra in the Yajurveda Saṃhitās,<sup>1</sup> along with the
<div n="lb"/>epithet Gaṇa-pati, ‘lord of groups.’ The exact sense is quite
<div n="lb"/>uncertain, but the term may allude to the chief of a band of
<div n="lb"/>robbers, as Zimmer<sup>2</sup> thinks.
