<L>357<pc>1-090<k1>उपक्वस<k2>उपक्वस
{@Upa-kvasa@}¦ is the name in the Atharvaveda (vi. 50, 2) of a
noxious insect injurious to seed. Sāyaṇa, however, reads the
word as a plural adjective ({%a-pakvasaḥ = a-dagdāḥ%}), but the
Paippalāda recension supports the form {%upakvasaḥ.%}
