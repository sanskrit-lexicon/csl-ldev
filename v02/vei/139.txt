{@Argala-Kāhoḍi@}¦ is mentioned in the Kāṭhaka Samhitā (xxv. 7)
as a teacher. The name may, however, be Aryala, as suggested
in the St. Petersburg Dictionary, {%s.v.,%} and as read by
v. Schroeder in his edition. The Kapiṣṭhala (xxxix. 5) has
Ayala. See below.
