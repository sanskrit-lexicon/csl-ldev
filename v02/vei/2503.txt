{@Muhūrta@}¦ denotes a division of time, one-thirtieth of a day,
or an hour of forty-eight minutes, in the Brāhmaṇas.<sup>1</sup> In the
Rigveda<sup>2</sup> the sense of ‘moment’ only is found. {%Cf.%} {@Ahan.@}
<F>1) Taittirīya Brāhmaṇa, iii. 10, 1, 1
(for the names); 9, 7; 12, 9, 6; Śata-
patha Brāhmaṇa, x. 4, 2, 18. 25. 27;
3, 20; xii. 3, 2, 5; x. 4, 4, 4, etc.</F>
<F>2) iii. 33, 5; 53, 8. The sense of
‘moment’ is also common in the
Brāhmaṇas.
