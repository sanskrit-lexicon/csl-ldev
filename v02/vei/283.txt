<L>283<pc>1-069<k1>अश्वतरआश्वि<k2>अश्वतरआश्वि
{@Aśvatara Āśvi,@}¦ or {@Āśvatarāśvi.@} — These two expressions are
used<sup>1</sup> as patronymics of {@Buḍila,@} denoting, according to Sāyaṇa,<sup>2</sup>
that he was son of Aśva, and descendant of Aśvatara.
<F>1) The first is found in Aitareya
Brāhmaṇa, vi. 30; the second in Sata-
patha Brāhmaṇa, iv. 6, 1, 9; x. 6, 1, 1;
Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad, iv. 15, 8;
Chāndogya Upaniṣad, v. 11, 1; 16, 1.</F>
<F>2) On Aitareya Brāhmaṇa, {%loc. cit.%}</F>
