<L>1997<pc>2-033<k1>प्रतिहर्तृ<k2>प्रतिहर्तृ
{@Prati-hartṛ@}¦ is the name of the assistant of the Udgātṛ in the
list of the sixteen priests ({@Ṛtvij@}). It is found in the later
Saṃhitās<sup>1</sup> and the Brāhmaṇas,<sup>2</sup> but not in the Rigveda.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, iii. 3, 2, 1.</F>
<F>2) Taittirīya Brāhmaṇa, i. 8, 2, 3;
Aitareya Brāhmaṇa, vii. 1; Śatapatha
Brāhmaṇa, iv. 3, 4, 22; xii. 1, 1, 8;
Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa, xxv. 15, 3;
Chāndogya Upaniṣad, i. 10, 11; 11, 8.</F>
<F>3) {%Cf.%} Ludwig, Translation of the
Rigveda, 3, 227.</F>
