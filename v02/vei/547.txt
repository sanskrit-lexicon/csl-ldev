<L>547<pc>1-144<k1>कशीका<k2>कशीका
{@Kaśīkā@}¦ is name of an animal mentioned once in the
Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> and interpreted as ‘weasel’ by the commentator
Sāyaṇa. Fick<sup>2</sup> suggests that the meaning is ‘pole-cat.’
Geldner<sup>3</sup> takes it as ‘female ichneumon.’
