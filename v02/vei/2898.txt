{@Vārkali,@}¦ ‘descendant of Vṛkalā,’ is the metronymic of a
teacher in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa.<sup>1</sup> The name in the form
of Vārkalin has been seen in the Aitareya Āraṇyaka,<sup>2</sup> but
wrongly.
<F>1) xii. 3, 2, 6.</F>
<F>2) iii. 2, 2, and Keith's note; Śāṅkh-
āyana Āraṇyaka, viii. 2. {%Cf.%} Weber,
{%Indian Literature,%} 33, 123, who thinks
Vārkali is equivalent to Vāṣkali.</F>
