<L>1857<pc>1-537<k1>पुनर्वसु<k2>पुनर्वसु
{@Punar-vasu@}¦ (‘bringing goods again’), used in the dual,
denotes the fifth in the series of the Vedic {@Nakṣatras,@} or
‘Lunar Mansions.’ Roth<sup>1</sup> takes the word to have this sense
in its only occurrence in the Rigveda,<sup>2</sup> but this must be regarded
as decidedly doubtful. The term is, however, found in the
ordinary lists of the Nakṣatras in the later Saṃhitās and the
Brāhmaṇas.<sup>3</sup>
