{@Ūrṇā-vatī.@}¦ — In the hymn of the Rigveda<sup>1</sup> which celebrates
<div n="lb"/>the rivers Ludwig<sup>2</sup> finds a reference to an affluent of the Indus

<div n="lb"/>structure of the hymn into confusion. Pischel<sup>5</sup> makes the
<div n="lb"/>word an epithet of the Indus, ‘rich in sheep.’
