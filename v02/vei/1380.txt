{@Daiyāṃpāti,@}¦ ‘descendant of Dayāṃpāta,’ is the name of a
teacher of the east, who was instructed by {@Śāṇḍilyāyana,@}
according to the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa (ix. 5, 1, 14), in the lore
of the construction of the fire-altar. The same patronymic is
given, in the form of Dayyāṃpāti, to {@Plakṣa,@} the contemporary
of {@Atyaṃhas@} in the Taittirīya Brāhmaṇa (iii. 10, 9, 3-5).
