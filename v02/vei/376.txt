<L>376<pc>1-095<k1>उपस्तरण<k2>उपस्तरण
{@Upa-staraṇa@}¦ denotes in the description of the couch ({@Par-@}
{@yaṅka@}) in the Kauṣītaki Upaniṣad<sup>1</sup> a ‘coverlet,’ and has this
sense, used metaphorically, in the Rigveda<sup>2</sup> also. In the
Atharvaveda<sup>3</sup> it seems to have the same meaning. Whitney,<sup>4</sup>
however, renders it ‘couch,’ though he translates<sup>5</sup> the parallel
word {@Āstaraṇa@} in another passage<sup>6</sup> by ‘cushion.’
