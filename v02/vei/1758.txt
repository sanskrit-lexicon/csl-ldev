{@Pāṅktra@}¦ is the name of an animal mentioned in the list of
victims at the Aśvamedha, or ‘horse-sacrifice,’ in the Yajurveda
Saṃhitās.<sup>1</sup> The ‘field-rat’ seems to be meant.
<F>1) Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā, iii. 14, 7; Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, xxiv. 26. {%Cf.%} Zimmer,
{%Altindisches Leben,%} 85.</F>
