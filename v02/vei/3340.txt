{@Sailāli,@}¦ ‘descendant of Śilālin,’ is the name of a ritual teacher
in the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa.<sup>1</sup> A Śailāli Brāhmaṇa is mentioned
in the Āpastamba Śrauta Sūtra,<sup>2</sup> and the school of the Śailālins
often occurs in the Śūtras.<sup>3</sup>
<F>1) xiii. 5, 3, 3.</F>
<F>2) vi. 4, 7.</F>
<F>3) Anupada Sūtra, iv. 5, etc.
{%Cf.%} Weber, {%Indische Studien,%} 1, 156;
{%Indian Literature,%} 197. who compares
the Naṭa Sūtra attributed to Śilālin by
Pāṇni, iv. 2, 110, 111.</F>
