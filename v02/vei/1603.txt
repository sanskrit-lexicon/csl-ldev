apron of cloth, worn by both men and women, but especially
by the latter. It is mentioned in the Atharvaveda<sup>1</sup> and later.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) viii. 2, 16; xiv. 2, 50. {%Cf. nīvi-%}
{%bhārya,%} ‘to be borne in the apron,’
viii. 6, 20.</F>
<F>2) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, vi. 1, 1, 3;
Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, iv. 10; Satapatha
Brāhmaṇa, i. 3, 3, 6; iii. 2, 1, 15, etc.
