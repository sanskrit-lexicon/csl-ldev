{@Dhana,@}¦ ‘prize,’ is often found in the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> probably the
prize in racing rather than the ‘booty’ in battle. It also
denotes<sup>2</sup> the ‘stake’ at dicing. In some passages it possibly
means the ‘contest’ itself.<sup>3</sup> More generally it denotes ‘wealth’
or ‘gift.’<sup>4</sup> But it sometimes expresses ‘booty,’<sup>5</sup> probably from
the notion of ‘wealth’ rather than of ‘prize.’
