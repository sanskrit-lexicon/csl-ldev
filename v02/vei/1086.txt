<L>1086<pc>1-292<k1>ज्याह्रोड<k2>ज्याह्रोड
{@Jyā-hroḍa@}¦ occurs in the description of the arms of the
{@Vrātya@} in the Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa,<sup>1</sup> and is also mentioned
in the Sūtras.<sup>2</sup> The sense is somewhat obscure, for one Sūtra
describes it as a ‘bow not meant for use’ ({%ayogyaṃ dhanus%}),<sup>3</sup>
while the other speaks of it as a ‘bow without an arrow’
({%dhanuṣka aniṣu%}).<sup>4</sup> Some sort of a bow, therefore, seems to be
meant.
<F>1) xvii. 1, 14 (spelt {%-hṇoḍa%} in text,
{%-hnoḍa%} in comm.).</F>
<F>2) Kātyāyana Śrauta Sūtra, xxii. 4,
11 (spelt {%-hroḍa%}); Lāṭyāyana Śrauta
Sūtra, viii. 6, 8 (spelt {%-hnoḍa:%} the editor
notes that the Drāhyāyaṇa Sūtra has
the same reading).</F>
<F>3) Kātyāyana, {%loc. cit.%}</F>
<F>4) Lāṭyāyana, {%loc. cit.%}
