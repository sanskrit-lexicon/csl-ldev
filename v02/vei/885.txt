{@Gṛdhra,@}¦ ‘the vulture,’ is often mentioned from the Rigveda<sup>1</sup>
downwards.<sup>2</sup> The swiftness of its flight<sup>3</sup> and its fondness for
devouring carrion are especially noticed.<sup>4</sup> More generally the
word is used to designate any bird of prey, the eagle ({@Śyena@})
being classed as the chief of the Gṛdhras.<sup>5</sup>
