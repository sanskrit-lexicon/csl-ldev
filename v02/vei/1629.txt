{@Nyaṅku@}¦ is the name of an animal in the list of victims at the
<div n="lb"/>Aśvamedha, or ‘horse sacrifice,’ in the Yajurveda Saṃhitās.<sup>1</sup>
<div n="lb"/>It seems clear that a kind a ‘gazelle’ is meant, but the com-
<div n="lb"/>mentary on the Taittirīya Saṃhitā<sup>1</sup> suggests ‘bear’ ({%ṛkṣa%}) as
<div n="lb"/>a variant rendering.

<div n="lb"/>Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā, iii. 14, 9; Vāja-
<div n="lb"/>saneyi Saṃhitā, xxiv. 27. 32. {%Cf.%}

<div n="lb"/>metre {%nyaṅku-sāriṇī,%} ‘with a gazelle's

<div n="lb"/>5; Nidāna Sūtra, i. 2.</F>
