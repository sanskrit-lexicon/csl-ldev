<L>353<pc>1-089<k1>उद्र<k2>उद्र
{@Udra@}¦ is the name of an animal occurring only in the list of
sacrificial victims at the Aśvamedha given in the Saṃhitās of
the Yajurveda.<sup>1</sup> According to Mahīdhara<sup>2</sup> it was a crab; but
as the commentary on the Taittirīya Saṃhitā<sup>3</sup> calls it a water-
cat, there can be no doubt that it was an otter.
<F>1) Taittirīya Saṃhitā, v. 5, 20, 1;
Maitrāyaṇī Saṃhitā, iii. 14, 18; Vāja-
saneyi Saṃhitā, xxiv. 37.</F>
<F>2) On Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā, {%loc. cit.%}</F>
