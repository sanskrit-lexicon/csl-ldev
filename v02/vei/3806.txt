{@Hima,@}¦ denoting ‘cold,’ ‘cold weather,’ is quite common in
the Rigveda,<sup>1</sup> but less frequent later.<sup>2</sup> As ‘snow’ the word
appears as a masculine in the Taittirīya Brāhmaṇa,<sup>3</sup> and often
later as a neuter.<sup>4</sup> {%Cf.%} {@Hemanta.@}
<F>1) i. 116, 8; 119, 6; viii. 32, 26, etc.</F>
<F>2) Av. vii. 18, 2; xiii. 1, 46; xix. 49, 5
(night as mother of coolness), etc.</F>
<F>3) iii. 12, 7, 2.</F>
<F>4) Saḍviṃśa Brāhmaṇa, vi. 9, etc.
