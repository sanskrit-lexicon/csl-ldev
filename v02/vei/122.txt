<L>122<pc>1-033<k1>अयोगू<k2>अयोगू
{@Ayogū@}¦ is a word of quite doubtful meaning, found in the list
of victims in the Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā.<sup>1</sup> It may, like the late
{%āyogava,%} denote a member of a mixed caste (theoretically a
descendant of a Śūdra by a Vaiśya wife).<sup>2</sup> Weber<sup>3</sup> rendered it
as ‘unchaste woman.’<sup>4</sup> Zimmer thinks it denotes a brotherless
maiden who is exposed to the dangers of prostitution ({%cf.%}
{%āyogava%}).
