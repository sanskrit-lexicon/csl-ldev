3. {%Ugrasena%}¦ (No. 1, L. 20):
He is mentioned as a ruler of Pālakka during the reign of
Samudragupta. Ugra meaning ‘powerful mighty or terrible’, is
another name of Rudra or Śiva.<sup>8</sup> Sena is merely a surname.
