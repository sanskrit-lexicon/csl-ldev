{#तमस्#}¦ n. ({#-मः#}) 1. Third of the qualities incident to the state of humani-
ty, the Tama guna, or property of darkness, whence proceed folly,
ignorance, mental blindness, worldly delusion, &c. 2. Darkness,
gloom. 3. Sin. 4. Sorrow, grief. mn. ({#-माः-मः#}) Rahu or the personi-
fied ascending node: see {#राहु#}. E. {#तम्#} to be disturbed, and {#असुन्#}
Unadi affix; that property by which the mind is troubled, the
world perplexed, &c.; also {#तमस#} and {#तम#}.
