{#विष्कम्भ#}¦ m. ({#-म्भः#}) 1. The first of the twenty-seven astronomical periods,
called Yogās or Yoga star of the first lunar mansion. 2. Obstacle,
hindrance, impediment, resistance. 3. Spreading, extension. 4.
Act, doing any thing. 5. A posture of the devotees called Yogīs.
6. One of the performers at a dramatic entertainment, either an
actor who in the intervals of the scenes explains to the audience
the progress of the story, or one or two of the personages of the
drama, who do the same in a soliloquy or dialogue. 7. A tree. 8.
The bolt or bar of a door. 9. A post, the roof-tree of a house. 10.
The diameter of a circle. E. {#वि#} before {#ष्कभि#} to stop or hinder, aff.
{#घञ्;#} or {#वि + स्कन्भ्-अच्;#} also with {#कन्#} added, {#विष्कम्भक ।#}
