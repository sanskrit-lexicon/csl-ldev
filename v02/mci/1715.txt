{@Śatakumbhā@}¦ f.: Name of a river.
<div n="P">Listed by Saṃjaya among the rivers of
the Bhāratavarṣa; its water used by people
for drinking 6. 10. 18, 13; all the rivers listed
by Saṃjaya are described as mothers of the
universe and very strong 6. 10. 35 (for cita-
tion see {@Atikṛṣṇā@}); also listed by Mārka-
ṇḍeya among rivers which are famous as
mothers of the sacrificial hearths ({%dhiṣṇyā-%}
{%nāṃ mātaro yāḥ prakīrtitāḥ%}) i. e. on the
banks of which sacrifices were performed 3.
212. 21 (for Nī. see {@Kapilā@}); by visiting it
one is glorified in heaven ({%abhigamya nara-%}
{%śreṣṭha svargaloke mahīyate%}) 3. 82. 9.
