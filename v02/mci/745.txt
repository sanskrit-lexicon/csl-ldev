<div n="P">The original Nītiśāstra composed by
Lokapitāmaha (Brahmadeva) was succes-
sively abridged by Śiva, Indra, Bṛhaspati,
and Kāvya; Bṛhaspati apparently received
the abridged form known as Bāhudantaka
from Indra; he then further abridged it to
three thousand {%adhyāyas;%} (this was further
abridged by Kāvya, the teacher of the Yoga
({%yogācārya%}) into one thousand {%adhyāyas-%}
the name of this last abridgement not given
12. 59. 91). [See {@Paitāmahaśāstra, Bāhu-@}
{@dantaka, Vaiśālākṣa@}].
