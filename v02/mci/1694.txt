{@Vedī<sup>2</sup>@}¦ f.: Name of a sacred place having
<div n="lb">the form of an altar.
[Page449-b+ 39]
<div n="lb">{@A.@} Origin: Viśvakarman offered a
<div n="lb">sacrifice in the forest of Svayaṃbhū (Brah-
<div n="lb">man); at this sacrifice Svayaṃbhū gave the
<div n="lb">entire earth as {%dakṣiṇā%} to Kaśyapa; angered
<div n="lb">at being gifted to a mortal, the earth entered
<div n="lb">Rasātala; when propitiated by Kaśyapa, the
<div n="lb">earth emerged from water and appeared in the
<div n="lb">form of an altar ({%punar unmajjya salitād%}
<div n="lb">{%vedīrūpā sthitā babhau//saiṣā prakāśate%}
<div n="lb">{%rājan vedīsaṃsthānalakṣaṇā%}) 3. 114. 17-23.
<div n="lb">{@B.@} Characteristic: If touched by a mortal
<div n="lb">the Vedī would enter the ocean ({%spṛṣṭā hi%}
<div n="lb">{%martyena tataḥ samudram eṣā vedī praviśati%}
<div n="lb">{% … %}) 3. 114. 24; hence described as ‘going
<div n="lb">to the ocean’ ({%sāgaragā%}) 3. 114. 26. {@C.@}
<div n="lb">Holiness: By ascending it one is endowed
<div n="lb">with virility ({%āruhyātra mahārāja vīryavān%}
<div n="lb">{%vai bhaviṣyasi%}) 3. 114. 23. {@D.@} Epic event:
<div n="lb">As advised by Lomaśa, Yudhiṣṭhira ascended
<div n="lb">the Vedī; before he did it, Lomaśa pronounced
<div n="lb">on him benediction ({%svastyayana%}); Yudhi-
<div n="lb">ṣṭhira was also to utter the following truth
<div n="lb">({%satyavākya%}) while ascending the altar;
<div n="lb">{%agnir mitro yonir āpo 'tha devyo viṣṇo retas%}
<div n="lb">{%tvam amṛtasya nābhiḥ%} 3. 114. 24-26.
