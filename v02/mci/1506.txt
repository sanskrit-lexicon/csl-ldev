{@Maladā@}¦ f.: Name of a river.
<div n="P">One should proceed to this river from the
Vimalatīrtha 3. 80. 103, 105; described as
famous in the three worlds ({%triṣu lokeṣu%}
{%viśrutām%}) 3. 80. 105; one should bathe in it
in the evening twilight according to the pre-
scribed rites and offer a {%caru%} according to
one's mite in the fire having seven flames;
such an offering to manes is considered to be
inexhaustible ({%paścimāyāṃ tu saṃdhyāyām%}
{%upaspṛśya yathāvidhi//caruṃ narendra%}
{%saptārcer yathāśakti nivedayet/pitṝṇām%}
{%akṣayaṃ dānaṃ pravadanti manīṣiṇaḥ//%})
3. 80. 105-106; offering such a {%caru%} is more
meritorious than gifting a hundred thousand
cows, than a hundred Rājasūya sacrifices, and
a thousand Aśvamedha sacrifices 3. 80. 107.
