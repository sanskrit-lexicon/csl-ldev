{%sarvagāḥ sarvadhāriṇaḥ%}) 12. 315. 53. {@C.@}
Activity: It blows in the seventh of the
seven paths of winds which blow on the
earth and in the atmosphere ({%pṛthivyām%}
{%antarikṣe ca yatra saṃvānti vāyavaḥ%}) 12.
315. 31 (Nī. on Bom. Ed. 12. 328. 31:

lives of living beings at the time of their
death; both Mṛtyu (Death) and Vaiva-

to immortality who, with their intellect
calm and steady in {%ātman,%} understand (the
world) correctly and who take delight in
the practice of meditation; having met Parā-
vaha ten thousand sons of Dakṣa Prajāpati
