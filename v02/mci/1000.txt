{@Alakanandā@}¦ f.: Name of a river.
<div n="P">Arjuna told the Gandharva Aṅgāraparṇa
that when the holy river Gaṅgā flows through
the sky the river has only one bank; there,
among the gods, it obtains the status of
Alakanandā ({%iyaṃ bhūtvā caikavaprā śucir%}
{%ākāśagā punaḥ/deveṣu gaṅgā gandharva%}
[Page290-a+ 37]
{%prāpnoty alakanandatām%}) 1. 158. 18 (Nī.
on Bom. Ed. 1. 170 21: {%ekam ākāśarūpaṃ%}
{%vapraṃ taṭaṃ yasyāḥ sā%}).
