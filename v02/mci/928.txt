<L>928<pc>265-a<k1>म्र्गोत्तम<k2>म्र्गोत्तम
{@Mrgottama@}¦ nt.: Name of the constella-
tion Mṛgaśiras; also called {@Tārāmṛga@} (m.)
(3. 262. 19) and {@Somadaivata@} (13. 63. 7).
{@A.@} Religious rites: (1) {%dāna:%} Nārada
told Devakī (13. 63. 2-4) that those who
gave as a gift a milch cow with a calf under
the constellation Somadaivata ({%nakṣatre%}
{%somadaivate%}) went to the excellent heaven
[Page265-b+ 39]
from the world of mortals ({%gacchanti mānu-%}
{%ṣāl lokāt svargalokam anuttamam%}) 13.
63. 7 (Nī. on Bom. Ed. 13. 64. 7: {%saumya-%}
{%nakṣatre mṛgaśirasi%}); (2) {%śrāddha:%} Yama
told Śaśabindu (13. 89. 1) that one desirous
of vigour should offer a {%śrāddha%} under the
constellation Mṛgottama ({%ojaskāmo mṛgot-%}
{%tame%}) 13. 89. 3 (Unknown commentator:
{%mṛgottame mṛgaśirasi,%} cf. Editor's note on
the stanza Cr. Ed. Vol. 17, p. 1090). {@B.@}
Simile: Rāma ran after the deer as did
Rudra after the Tārāmṛga ({%anvadhāvan%}
{%mṛgaṃ rāmo rudras tārāmṛgaṃ yathā%})
3. 262. 19 (Nī. on Bom. Ed. 3. 278. 20:
{%tārāmṛgaṃ tārārūpaṃ mṛgam … tad etan%}
{%mṛgaśīrṣaṃ nāma nakṣatram%}).
