{@Vidiśā@}¦ f.: Name of a river.
<div n="P">One of the rivers who, in bodily form
(2. 9. 21), wait on Varuṇa in his Sabhā 2.
9. 18, 22; listed by Saṃjaya among the rivers
of the Bhāratavarṣa; people used its water
for drinking 6. 10. 27, 13; all the rivers listed
here are described as mothers of the universe
and very strong 6. 10. 35 (for citation see
{@Atikṛṣṇā@}).
