{@Kāñcanākṣī@}¦ f.: One of the seven names
of Sarasvatī.
<div n="P">Described as the best of rivers ({%saritāṃ%}
{%śreṣṭhā%}) 9. 37. 18; the sages once assembled
at the Naimiṣa forest to perform a {%satra;%}
when they thought of Sarasvatī, the river came
to the Naimiṣa forest for the sages and was
called Kāñcanākṣī; the river was revered by
them ({%naimiṣe kāñcanākṣī tu munīnāṃ%}
{%satrayājinām/āgatā sarītāṃ śreṣṭhā tatra%}
{%bhārata pūjitā%}) 9. 37. 15-18; the seven
names of Sarasvatī are given in 9. 37. 4.
