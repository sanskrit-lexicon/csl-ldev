{@Vardhamānadvāra, Vardhamānapuradvāra@}¦
 nt.: Name of the gate of Hāstina-
<div n="lb">pura facing the town Vardhamāna.
<div n="P">Epic events: (1) Kuntī, after the
<div n="lb">death of Pāṇḍu, came in the company of the
<div n="lb">ascetics and her sons to Kurujāṅgala and
<div n="lb">from there she reached the Vardhamāna-
<div n="lb">puradvāra before entering Hāstinapura
<div n="lb">({%sā … saṃprāptā kurujāṅgalam/vardha-%}
<div n="lb">{%mānapuradvāraṃ āsasāda yaśasvinī//%}) 1.
<div n="lb">117. 8 (Nī. on Bom. Ed. 3. 1. 10: {%vardha-%}
<div n="lb">{%mānapuraṃ nāma grāmaviśeṣaḥ/tadabhi-%}
<div n="lb">{%mukhaṃ dvāram;%} however, on 1. 126. 9
<div n="lb">he simply says: {%vardhamānapuradvāraṃ%}
[Page561-b+ 39]
<div n="lb">{%mukhyadvāram%}); (2) The Pāṇḍavas when
<div n="lb">they left Hāstinapura for their exile, came
<div n="lb">out of the Vardhamānapura gate and
<div n="lb">went to the north ({%niryayur gajasāhvayāt/%}
<div n="lb">{%vardhamānapuradvāreṇābhiniṣkramya te ta-%}
<div n="lb">{%dā/udaṅmukhāḥ … prayayuḥ//%}) 3. 1. 8-9;
<div n="lb">(3) When Dhṛtarāṣṭra decided to repair
<div n="lb">to the forest he left Hāstinapura coming
<div n="lb">out of the Vardhamānadvāra ({%sa vardha-%}
<div n="lb">{%mānadvāreṇa niryayau gajasāhvayāt%}) 15. 22.
<div n="lb">3; (Vardhamānadvāra, according to S. K.
<div n="lb">Belvalkar, means ‘an entrance or exit other
<div n="lb">than the southern’, cf. {%dakṣiṇādvārahīnaṃ%}
<div n="lb">{%tu vardhamānam udāhṛtam%} from the Bṛhat-
<div n="lb">saṃhitā of Varāhamihira 53. 33. 36 cited by
<div n="lb">him. Dr. Belvalkar further observes: “It is
<div n="lb">also the name of a specific gate of Hastinā-
<div n="lb">pura”, his note on the stanza in the Cr. Ed.
<div n="lb">Vol. 19, p. 153. The Bṛhatsaṃhitā passage,
<div n="lb">however, seems to mean that a house which
<div n="lb">does not have a gate to the south is called
<div n="lb">Vardhamāna. The editions of the Bṛhat-
<div n="lb">saṃhitā consulted by me read in 53. 33 as
<div n="lb">{%tadvac ca%} (or {%tasmiṃś ca%}) {%vardhamāne%}
<div n="lb">{%dvāraṃ tu na dakṣiṇaṃ kāryam%}).
