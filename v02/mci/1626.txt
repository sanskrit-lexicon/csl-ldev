<div n="P">So called because Vṛṣākapi (Nārāyaṇa),
after assuming the form of a boar and after
lifting up the lost earth ({%imāṃ hi dharaṇīṃ%}
{%pūrvaṃ naṣṭām … govinda ujjahārāśu%}
