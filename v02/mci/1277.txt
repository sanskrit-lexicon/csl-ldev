<div n="P">Listed by Saṃjaya among the rivers of
the Bhāratavarṣa; its water used by people
for drinking 6. 10. 30, 13; all the rivers
listed by Saṃjaya are said to be mothers
of the universe and very strong 6. 10. 35

(for citation see {@Atikṛṣṇā@}); also listed by
Mārkaṇḍeya among the rivers which are
famous as mothers of sacrificial hearths, i. e.
on the banks of which sacrifices were per-
formed ({%etā nadyas tu dhiṣṇyānāṃ mātaro%}
{%yāḥ prakīrtitāḥ%}) 3. 212. 23-24 (for Nī.
see {@Kapilā@}).
