{%mād rākṣasendreṇa)%} 3. 258. 1-2; (2) Mārīca

{%bhāṣya praviveśāśramaṃ punaḥ)%} 3. 262. 39;

hastened to his {%āśrama (rāmo 'bhyapatad%}
{%āśramam)%} 3. 263. 15; (5) When Rāma

the {%āśrama%} were empty, and a herd of
jackals had occupied it ({%tato dṛṣṭvāśra-%}

3. 263. 22; (6) When Rāma and Lakṣ-
maṇa knew about the abduction of Sītā,
