<div n="P">A village sacred to Viṣṇu Nārāyaṇa;
Hari is always present there; by approach-
ing Viṣṇu there one got the fruit of Aśva-
medha sacrifice and went to the world of
Viṣṇu ({%sthānaṃ nārāyaṇasya tu/sadā%}
{%saṃnihito yatra harir vasati bhārata/śāla-%}
{%grāma iti khyāto viṣṇor adbhutakarmaṇaḥ/%}
{%abhigamya … viṣṇum … /aśvamedham avā-%}
{%pnoti viṣṇulokaṃ ca gacchati//%}) 3. 82. 106-
107; there is a well there which freed the
visitor of all sins; four oceans are present
in it; by bathing in the well one did not
meet with calamity ({%tatrodapāno dharmajña%}
{%sarvapāpapramocanaḥ/samudrās tatra%}
{%catvāraḥ kūpe saṃnihitāḥ sadā/tatopa-%}
{%spṛśya rājendra na durgatim avāpnuyāt//%})
3. 82. 108.
