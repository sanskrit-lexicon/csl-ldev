<div n="P">Described as frequented by gods, sages
and manes ({%devarṣipitṛsevita%}) 3. 80. 60; one
should go from Puṣkara to the Jambūmārga
and from there to the Taṇḍulikāśrama 3. 80.
59, 62; one who visits this place gets the fruit
of an Aśvamedha and secures for himself
the Viṣṇuloka 3. 80. 60; if one lives there
for five nights and eats only the sixth meal
(in the evening of the third day) ({%ṣaṣṭhakā-%}
{%lakṣamī naraḥ%}) he will not meet with adver-
sities and will obtain the best perfection
({%siddhiṃ prāpnoti cottamām%}) 3. 80. 61; if
one leads there a well disciplined life with
a composed mind ({%saṃyataḥ susamāhitaḥ%})
he would achieve perfection in a day ({%aho-%}
{%rātreṇa caikena siddhiṃ samadhigacchati%})
13. 26. 48; finds mention in the Daivata-
Ṛṣi-Vaṃśa 13. 151. 19, 2.
