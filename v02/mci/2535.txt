{@Vairāma@}¦ m. (pl.): Name of a people.
{@A.@} Location: They were born in the
bay (?) of the (western) ocean, near the
river Sindhu ({%samudraniṣkuṭe jātāḥ pari-%}
{%sindhu ca mānavāḥ%}) 2. 47. 9 (Nī., however,
on Bom. Ed. 2. 51. 11: {%samudrasamī-%}
{%pasthaniṣkuṭe gṛhodyāne%}). {@B.@} Characte-
ristics: They lived on grains which grew
of their own accord or which they culti-
vated using river water ({%indrakṛṣṭair varta-%}
{%yanti dhānyair nadīmukhaiś ca ye%}) 2. 47. 9
(For Nīlakaṇṭha's comment see {@Pārada@}
p. 783). {@C.@} Epic event: They brought
tribute for Yudhiṣṭhira's Rājasūya many
kinds of articles and jewels of various types
({%vīvidhaṃ balim ādāya ratnāni vividhāni%}
{%ca%}), but they were stopped at the gate
({%te vairāmāḥ … / … dvāri tiṣṭhanti vāritāḥ%})
2. 47. 10-11 (the details of the tribute are
given in 2. 47. 11 for which see {@Pārada@}).
