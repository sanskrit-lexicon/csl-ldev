<L>711<pc>183-b<k1>त्रिसुपर्ण<k2>त्रिसुपर्ण
{@Trisuparṇa@}¦ m., nt.: Name of a Vedic
text; in the Mahābhārata the word occurs as
an adj. of a Brāhmaṇa who has studied this
text 13. 90. 20.
<div n="P">According to Nī. the term applies to
the three {%mantras%} from the Ṛgveda (10. 114.
3-5 which contain the word {%suparṇa%}) or to
those in the Taitt. Ār. 10. 48-50: {%trisuparṇaṃ%}
{%catuṣkapardā yuvatiḥ supeṣā iti bahvṛcānāṃ%}
{%mantratrayaṃ vā brahmam etu mām ityādi%}
{%taittirīyaprasiddhaṃ vā%} on Bom. Ed. 13.
90. 26; the unknown commentator simply
[Page184-a+ 40]
says: {%trisuparṇaḥ bahvṛcāṃ vedabhāga-%}
{%vīśeṣādhyāyī%}—see Critical Notes on 13. 90.
20 (p. 1091. 1); so also Kullūka on Manu
3. 185: {%trisuparṇo bahvṛcāṃ vedabhāgaḥ.%}
