38-39; sinful, they roamed about over the
earth living like dogs, crows, {%balas%} (?)
and vultures; they did not exist in the Kṛta-
yuga, but were found since the Tretā 12.
200. 41-42. {@C.@} Epic events: (1) Kṛṣṇa
told Karṇa before the war that if he were
to join the Pāṇḍavas, Cūcupas and many
other people would lead his procession
({%purogamāś ca te santu%}) 5. 138. 25; (2)
On the second day of the war, Duryodhana,
helped by Cūcupas and other warriors,
protected the army of Śakuni 6. 47. 16:
(3) On the sixth day, they were posted at
the right wing of the Krauñcavyūha of
the Kauravas 6. 71. 14, 20.
