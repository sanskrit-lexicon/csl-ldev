RUDRAKOṬI¦. A holy place in North India. Once a large
number of hermits gathered in this place to worship
Śiva. Greatly pleased at this Śiva appeared before them
in the form of many phalluses. From that day onwards
the place Rudrakoṭi became a holy place. (M.B. Vana
Parva, Chapter 82, Stanza 118).
