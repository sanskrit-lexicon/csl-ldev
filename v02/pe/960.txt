BHADRĀ II¦. The beautiful daughter of King Kakṣīvān.
<div n="lb"/>She was married to King Vyūṣitāśva of the Pūru dynasty.
<div n="lb"/>When Bhadrā, in unbearable grief lamented the death
<div n="lb"/>of her husband his soul appeared on the skies and
<div n="lb"/>blessed her. Accordingly she got pregnant by the corpse
<div n="lb"/>of her husband and delivered six sons. (Ādi Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 120, Verse 38).
