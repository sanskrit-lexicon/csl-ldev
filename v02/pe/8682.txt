VYŪHA¦. (Disposition of an army). Disposition of an
<div n="lb"/>army of four parts, (infantry, cavalry, elephants
<div n="lb"/>and chariots) in the battlefield, the arrangement
<div n="lb"/>of it, in various forms. It is said that during the period
<div n="lb"/>of Mahābhārata, there were various forms of disposition
<div n="lb"/>of the army. Some of them are given below:
[Page889-a+ 65]
<div n="NI"/>i) Ardhacandravyūha. (like a Crescent moon) (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 56)
<div n="NI"/>ii) Garuḍavyūha. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 56).
<div n="NI"/>iii) Krauñcavyūha. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 60).
<div n="NI"/>iv) Cakravyūha. (wheel-shaped) (M.B. Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 34).
<div n="NI"/>v) Makaravyūha. (shark-like) (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 69).
<div n="NI"/>vi) Maṇḍalavyūha. (circular) (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 81).
<div n="NI"/>vii) Maṇḍalārdhavyūha. (Semi circular) (M.B. Droṇa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 20).
<div n="NI"/>viii) Vajravyūha. (Diamond-shaped) (M.B. Bhīṣma
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 81).
<div n="NI"/>ix) Śakaṭavyūha. (cart-shaped) (M.B. Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 7).
<div n="NI"/>x) Śyenavyūha. (Vulture-like) (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 69).
<div n="NI"/>xi) Sarvatobhadravyūha. (fortified all-round) (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 99).
<div n="NI"/>xii) Suparṇavyūha. (kite-shaped) (M.B. Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 20).
<div n="NI"/>xiii) Sūcīmukhavyūha. (Like the needle-eye) (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 77).
