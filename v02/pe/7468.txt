TAKṢAKA II¦. The elder of the two sons of Lakṣmaṇa
of his wife Ūrmilā. The other was Chatraketu. Follow-
ing the instructions of Śrī Rāma, Lakṣmaṇa went to the
east sea coast, defeated the forest tribe there. He then
constructed a city there named Agati and made Takṣaka
the king there. He then went to the west sea-coast
and destroying the barbarous tribe there constructed a
city there called Candramatī and made Chatraketu
(? candraketu) the king. (Uttara Rāmāyaṇa).
