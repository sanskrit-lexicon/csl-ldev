PAVAMĀNA I¦. An Agni (fire). The eldest son of
Brahmā, Agni, got of his wife Svāhā three sons, Pāvaka,
Pavamāna and Śuci. These three got fortyfive children
and they are called Agnis. Thus, father, three sons and
their fortyfive sons constitute the 49 Agnis. (Chapter 10,
Aṃśa 1, Viṣṇu Purāṇa).
