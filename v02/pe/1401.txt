<div n="lb"/>hair, fibre or feathers at the end. A symbol used by
<div n="lb"/>kings and brahmins. Cāmara of the king should have a

<div n="lb"/>the swan, the pea cock, the Balākā bird etc. But, the
<div n="lb"/>wings of different birds shall not be intermixed. Circu-
<div n="lb"/>lar in shape, the Cāmara should have on its handle
<div n="lb"/>3, 4, 5, 6, 7, or 8 sandhis (joints, knots). (Agni-
<div n="lb"/>purāṇa, Chapter 245).
