SAÑJĪVANAMAṆI¦. The jewel in the head of serpents.
There is a tradition among the poets that this jewel is
the basis of the serpent's life. There is a story in Mahā
bhārata, Aśvamedhika Parva, Chapter 30, Verse 42,
stating how Babhruvāhana brought Arjuna to life by
Sañjīvanamaṇi. (For details see under Babhruvāhana).
