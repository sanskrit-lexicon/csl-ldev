VATSANĀBHA¦. A hermit. Dharma, once took the form
of a she-buffalo and saved Vatsanābha from heavy rain.
After this he thought that he was an ungrateful man and
so he decided to forsake his body. But Dharma dissuad-
ed him from this attempt. (M.B. Anuśāsana Parva,
Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭha, Chapter 12).
