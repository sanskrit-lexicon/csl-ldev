<div n="lb"/>Ulūka. Once in a battle, Arjuna defeated this King.
<div n="lb"/>Thenceforward he was friendly with the Pāṇḍavas.
<div n="lb"/>Bṛhadanta offered Arjuna a good deal of costly pre-
<div n="lb"/>sents. In the battle of Bhārata, this king fought on
<div n="lb"/>the side of the Pāṇḍavas and was killed in the battle.
<div n="lb"/>(M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 27, Ādi Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>185, Udyoga Parva, Chapter 4; Droṇa Parva, Chapter
