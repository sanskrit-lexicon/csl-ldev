PŪRVACITTI¦. A celebrated celestial maiden. She
belongs to the group of six celebrated nymphs named
Urvaśī, Pūrvacitti, Sahajanyā, Ghṛtācī, Viśvācī and
Menakā. (Śloka 68, Chapter 74, Ādi Parva). Pūrva-
citti was present for the Janmotsava of Arjuna. (Chapter
122, Ādi Parva). Once when Arjuna went to svarga
Pūrvacitti and others danced in his honour. (Chapter
43, Vana Parva).
