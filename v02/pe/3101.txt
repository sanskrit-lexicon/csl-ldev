KĀLAKĀMUKHA¦ (KĀLAKĀMMUKA). A Rākṣasa.
<div n="lb"/>He was the brother of Prahasta, Rāvaṇa's Minister. (For
<div n="lb"/>Genealogy, see under Prahasta). During Śrī Rāma's
<div n="lb"/>life in the forest Kālakāmukha was also among Khara,
<div n="lb"/>Dūṣaṇa and others. The other eleven members of that
<div n="lb"/>group were—Śyenagāmī, Pṛthugrīva, Yajñaśatru, Vihaṅ-
<div n="lb"/>gama, Durjaya, Karavīrākṣa, Paruṣa, Meghamālī,
<div n="lb"/>Mahāmālī, Sarpāsya and Rudhirāśana. (Vālmīki Rāmā-
<div n="lb"/>yaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, 26th Sarga).
