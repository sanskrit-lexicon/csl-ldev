BṚBUTAKṢA¦. A king of the period of the Vedas. He was
liberal and interested in architecture and all kinds of
construction work. In the Ṛg Veda and Sāṃkhyāyana
Śrauta Sūtra mention is made that this king offered
plenty of presents to the hermit Bharadvāja.
