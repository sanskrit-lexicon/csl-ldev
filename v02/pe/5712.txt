RAUDRĀŚVA II¦. A hermit who was the disciple of the
hermit Kātyāyana. Once Mahiṣāsura came in the guise
of a beautiful woman to the hermitage of Raudrāśva to
hinder his penance. The hermit cursed Mahiṣāsura,
“You will die because of a woman.” (Kālikā Purāṇa,
Chapter 62).
