ŚAṄKHACŪḌA II¦. A slave of Kubera. While Kṛṣṇa
and Balabhadra were enjoying pleasures with naked
Gopastrīs at Vṛndāvana Śaṅkhacūḍa, attracted by the
Gopī women, went there. He abducted the women and
in the fight that ensued was killed by Kṛṣṇa, who gave
to Balabhadra the precious stone taken from his
(Śaṅkhacūḍa's) head. (Bhāgavata, 10th Skandha).
