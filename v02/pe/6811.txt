STHŪLĀKṢA I¦. A giant. He was one of those giants who
fought with Śrī Rāma while Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa were
living in the forest in Pañcavaṭī. It is mentioned in
Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Sarga 26, that
when Khara, Dūṣaṇa and Triśiras were killed, Mahā-
kapāla, Sthūlākṣa and Pramāthī confronted Śrī Rāma
and were killed.
