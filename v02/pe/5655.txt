RĀMAṬHA¦ (S). II. People of a low caste (mlecchas)
who lived in South India during Purāṇic times.
Nakula, subjugated this caste, and from that day
onwards they became devoted to the Pāṇḍavas. They
were invited to the Rājasūya conducted by Yudhiṣṭhira.
(Vana Parva, Chapter 51, Verse 25).
