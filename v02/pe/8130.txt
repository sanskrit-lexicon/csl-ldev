VĀTA¦. One of the Saptarṣis (seven hermits) of the
Manvantara (Age of the Manu) of Manu Svārociṣa.
In this age of the Manu the Devendra was Vipaścit.
The Saptarṣis of that Age of the Manu were Ūrja,
Stamba, Prāṇa, Vāta, Vṛṣabha, Niraya and Parīvān.
(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 3, Chapter 1).
