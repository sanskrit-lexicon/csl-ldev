DYUTIMĀN IV¦. A hermit of the family of Bhṛgu. A
daughter named Lakṣmī and sons named Dhātā and
Vidhātā were born to Bhṛgu by his wife Khyāti. Dhātā
and Vidhātā married the daughters of Meru named
Āyati and Niyati. Prāṇa and Mṛkaṇḍu were born to
the couple. Mārkaṇḍeya was born from Mṛkaṇḍu and
Vedaśiras from Mārkaṇḍeya. A son named Dyutimān
was born to Prāṇa and Rājapāla was born to Dyuti-
mān. The family of Bhṛgu owes its expansion to Rāja-
pāla. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 10).
