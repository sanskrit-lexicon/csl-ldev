DHṚTARĀṢṬRĪ¦. A daughter born from Tāmrā the
<div n="lb"/>wife of Kaśyapa Prajāpati. Tāmrā had five daughters:
<div n="lb"/>Krauñcī, Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī. Of these,
<div n="lb"/>from Krauñcī, the owls, the Bhāsas from Bhāsī, the
<div n="lb"/>eagle and vulture from Śyenī, the swans and the ruddy
<div n="lb"/>goose from Dhṛtarāṣṭrī were born in the world. (This
<div n="lb"/>occurs in Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Sarga 14).
