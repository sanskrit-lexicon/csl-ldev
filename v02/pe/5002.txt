PĀṆIKHĀTA¦ A holy place on the border of Kuru-
kṣetra. If one bathes in the sacred pond there and
performs worship of the Manes one will get the benefit
of conducting the three yajñas, Agniṣṭoma, Atirātra
and Rājasūya. (Śloka 89, Chapter 13, Vana Parva).
