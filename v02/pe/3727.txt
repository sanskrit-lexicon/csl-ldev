KṢEMA¦. A King in Ancient India. Mention is made in
Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Stanza 65,
that this King was the rebirth of the Asura Krodha-
vaśa. In the Bhārata-battle this King took the side of
the Pāṇḍavas and was killed by Droṇa. (M.B. Droṇa
Parva, Chapter 21, Stanza 53).
