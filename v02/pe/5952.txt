ŚĀKADVĪPA¦. One of the Saptadvīpas (seven islands).
Sañjaya once gave Dhṛtarāṣṭra a description of this
island. (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 11). Jambūdvīpa,
Plakṣadvīpa, Śālmalidvīpa, Kuśadvīpa, Krauñcadvīpa,
Śākadvīpa and Puṣkaradvīpa are the Saptadvīpas.
(Devī Bhāgavata, 8th Skandha).
