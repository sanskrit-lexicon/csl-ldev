VĪRADYUMNA¦. An ancient King in India. He had a
son named Bhūridyumna. Once this son was lost in the
great forest. Vīradyumna wandered everywhere in the
forest in search of his son. On the way he met a hermit
called Tanu. He talked elaborately to the King about
the hopes of human mind. (M.B. Śānti Parva, Chapters
127 and 128).
