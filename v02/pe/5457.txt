PULINDA¦(S) I. The people of the country of Pulinda.
Information available regarding them from the Mahā-
bhārata is given below:
<div n="NI"/>(1) Pulindas were originally Kṣatriyas. But they be-
came Śūdras by a curse of the brahmins. (Chapter 33,
Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(2) Pulindas who belong to the mleccha tribe became
rulers in Kaliyuga. (Chapter 186, Vana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(3) Pulindas were born from the foam of the celestial
cow Nandinī of sage Vasiṣṭha when she got enraged.
(Chapter 165, Āśramavāsika Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Bhīma fought against the Pulindas and destroyed
all their big cities. (Chapter 26, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(5) In the great Mahābhārata battle Pulindas fought
on the side of Duryodhana. (Chapter 158, Udyoga
Parva).
