<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}

<div n="lb"/>glorifying him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 8, Verse
<div n="lb"/>16).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) While Sudyumna was the King, to do justice

<div n="lb"/>off. (For detailed story see under Likhita).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Because he had executed the duties of the King
