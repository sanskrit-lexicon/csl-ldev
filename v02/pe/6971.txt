SUDYUMNA II¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A King who was born as a
woman and then became a man and then became a
woman, all in the same birth. (For detailed story see
under Ilā I).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) This royal hermit stays in the court of Yama
glorifying him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 8, Verse
16).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) While Sudyumna was the King, to do justice
properly, both hands of the hermit Likhita were cut
off. (For detailed story see under Likhita).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Because he had executed the duties of the King
properly and righteously, Sudyumna attained heaven.
(M.B. Śānti Parva, Chapter 28, Verse 45).
