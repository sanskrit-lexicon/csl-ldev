DAṆḌADHARA I¦. A Kṣatriya King of Magadha.
<div n="lb"/>The following information about this King is available
<div n="lb"/>from the Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>1) Daṇḍadhara was born as the rebirth of a giant
<div n="lb"/>known as Krodhavardhana. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>67, Stanza 46).
<div n="NI"/>2) Bhīmasena, during his conquest of the countries,
<div n="lb"/>overcame King Daṇḍadhara and his brother Daṇḍa.
[Page201-a+ 64]
<div n="lb"/>(M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 30, Stanza 17).
<div n="NI"/>3) In the battle between the Pāṇḍavas and the Kaura-
<div n="lb"/>vas, Daṇḍadhara fought from the back of an elephant
<div n="lb"/>against the Pāṇḍavas. When Daṇḍadhara began to
<div n="lb"/>exterminate the army of the Pāṇḍavas, Śrī Kṛṣṇa per-
<div n="lb"/>suaded Arjuna to fight against Daṇḍadhara, who was
<div n="lb"/>killed in the fight. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 8,
<div n="lb"/>Stanzas 1 to 13).
