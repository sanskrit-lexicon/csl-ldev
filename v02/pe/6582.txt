SIDDHI II¦. A goddess. Kuntī the mother of the
Pāṇḍavas was the human rebirth of this goddess. (M.B.
Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Verse 120). This goddess
Siddhi had walked in front of the army of Subrahmaṇya,
in the great battle between the Devas and the asuras.
To attain the object in any matter, the blessing of this
goddess is essential. (M.B. Śalya Parva, Chapter 46,
Verse 64).
