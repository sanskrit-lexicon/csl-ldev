VAIVASVATA¦ MANU. The seventh Manu. There is a
description of Manu Vaivasvata under Manvantara.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in the following
order:—Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Vivasvān-Vaivasvata
Manu.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%The incarnation of Matsya and Vaivasvata Manu.%} See
under Avatāra, Section “Matsya.”
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Wife and children.%} The wife of Vaivasvata Manu
was Śraddhā. Many sons were born to the couple.
Prominent among them were, Yama, Yamī, Aśvinī-
kumāras, Revanta, Sudyumna, Ikṣvāku, Nṛga, Śaryāti,
Diṣṭa, Dhṛṣṭa, Karūṣa, Nariṣyanta, Nābhāga, Pṛṣadhra
and Kavi.
