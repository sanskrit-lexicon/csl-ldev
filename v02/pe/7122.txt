ŚUNAKA III¦. A Rājarṣi. He was born from a portion
of the asura named Candrahantā. This Rājarṣi attained
Samādhi (passed away) at Candra tīrtha. During his
life he received a sword from King Hariṇāśva and he
presented it to another King Uśīnara. (M.B. Ādi
Parva, Chapter 67; Vana Parva, Chapter 123; Śānti
Parva, Chapter 166).
