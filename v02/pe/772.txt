ĀRYĀVARTA¦. A king of the dynasty of Viśvakarmā.
<div n="lb"/>{%Genealogy.%} Descending in order from Viṣṇu are Brahmā,
<div n="lb"/>Dharma, Prabhāsa, Viśvakarmā, Priyavrata, Agnīdhra,
<div n="lb"/>Nābhi, Ṛṣabha and Āryāvarta.
<div n="NI"/>Of these Ṛṣabha got of his wife Jayantī twenty sons:
<div n="lb"/>Bharata, Kuśāvarta, Ilāvarta, Brahmāvarta, Aryāvarta
<div n="lb"/>Malaya, Ketu, Bhadrasena, Indraspṛk, Vidarbha,
[Page092-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>Kīkaṭa, Kavi, Hari, Antarīkṣa, Prabuddha, Pippalāyana,
<div n="lb"/>Āvirhotra, Dramiḍa, Camasa and Karabhājana. Of these
<div n="lb"/>Bharata ruled over this land and gave it the name
<div n="lb"/>Bhārata. Brothers of Bharata ruled different parts of this
<div n="lb"/>land. Dramiḍa's land became known later as Drāviḍa
<div n="lb"/>(Dakṣiṇabhārata).
