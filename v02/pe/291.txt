ANENAS I¦. A king of the Lunar dynasty (Candra
Vaṃśa).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in the following
order: Brahmā-Atri-Candra-Budha-Purūravas-
Āyus-Anenas.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Purūravas had by his wife Urvaśī six sons
named Āyus, Śrutāyus, Satyāyus, Raya, Vijaya and
Jaya. Of them, Āyus, the eldest, had five sons named
Nahuṣa, Kṣatravṛddha, Rāji, Rambha and Anenas.
Nahuṣa had a son named Yayāti to whom were born
the sons Pūru, Yadu and others. The two dynasties of
Yadu and Pūru (Yaduvaṃśa and Pūruvaṃśa) origi-
nate from them. To Anenas, brother of Nahuṣa, a son
named Śuddha was born. Śuddha begot Śuci who be-
got Trikakup and a son named Śāntarayas was born to
Trikakup.
