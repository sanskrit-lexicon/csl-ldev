PARĀVAHA¦. One of the seven forms of Vāyu. Prāṇa-
<div n="lb"/>vāyu has got seven forms:—
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Pravaha.%} This vāyu makes lightning in the sky.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Āvaha.%} It is by this vāyu that the stars shine and
<div n="lb"/>waters of the ocean go up as water-vapour and down as
<div n="lb"/>rains.
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Udvaha.%} This vāyu makes movements among the
<div n="lb"/>clouds and produces thunder.
<div n="NI"/>4) {%Saṃvaha.%} This vāyu beats the mountains. Saṃvaha
<div n="lb"/>is also engaged in shaping the clouds and producing
<div n="lb"/>thunder.
<div n="NI"/>5) {%Vyavaha.%} Preparing holy water in the sky and
<div n="lb"/>making Ākāśagaṅgā stay there static is the work of this
<div n="lb"/>vāyu.
<div n="NI"/>6) {%Parivaha.%} This vāyu gives strength to one who sits
<div n="lb"/>in meditation.
<div n="NI"/>7) {%Parāvaha.%} It is this vāyu that fans the devas to
<div n="lb"/>make them cool. (Chapter 329, Bhīṣma Parva).
