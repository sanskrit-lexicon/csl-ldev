ROCAMĀNA I¦. A Kṣatriya King. The following
<div n="lb"/>information is available from Mahābhārata about
<div n="lb"/>him.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Rocamāna was born from a portion of an asura
<div n="lb"/>named Aśvagrīva. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 67,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 18).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Rocamāna also was present at the Svayaṃvara
<div n="lb"/>(marriage) of Draupadī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>185, Stanza 10).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Rocamāna was the King of the country Aśva-
<div n="lb"/>medha. Bhīmasena, at the time of his regional con-
<div n="lb"/>quest, defeated this King. (Mahābhārata, Sabhā
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 29, Stanza 8).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) At the beginning of the battle of Kurukṣetra, the
<div n="lb"/>Pāṇḍavas sent an invitation to this King to join the
<div n="lb"/>battle. (M.B. Udyoga Parva, Chapter 4, Stanza 12).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Rocamāna was a mighty warrior on the side of the
<div n="lb"/>Pāṇḍavas in the battle of Kurukṣetra. (M.B. Droṇa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 70, Stanza 47).
[Page650-b+ 63]
<div n="NI"/>(vi) Rocamāna was killed by Karṇa in a combat.
<div n="lb"/>(M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 56, Stanza 49).
