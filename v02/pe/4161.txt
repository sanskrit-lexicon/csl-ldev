MAHĀŚRAMA¦. A holy place. Mahābhārata, Vana
Parva, Chapter 84, says that a person who spends a
[Page464-b+ 64]
night here observing a fast, would obtain “Śubhaloka”.
One who spends three months like that will become a
“Paramasiddha”. (M.B. Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 25,
Verse 17).
