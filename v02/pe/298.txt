AṄGAKARMA¦. Actions on the stage are of two kinds.
<div n="lb"/>One is to express by the major organs of the body. The
<div n="lb"/>other is to express by the minor organs of the body. Head
<div n="lb"/>etc. are major organs and eyebrow etc. are minor
<div n="lb"/>organs. Actions expressed by major organs are called
<div n="lb"/>Aṅgakarma and those by minor organs are called Prat-
<div n="lb"/>yaṅga Karma. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 341).
