ŚRUTARVA¦ (ŚRUTARVĀ) II. One of the hundred
sons of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. In the course of the Bhārata battle
he attacked Bhīmasena with twelve of his brothers and
in the battle which followed Śrutarva was killed, by
Bhīmasena. Among the Kauravas, Śrutarvā was one
of the mighty archers. The terrible battle fought
against Bhīmasena under his leadership alarmed the
other warriors. (M.B. Śalya Parva, Chapter 26).
