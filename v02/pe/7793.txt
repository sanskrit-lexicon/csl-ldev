UŚAṄKU¦ (RUŚAṄKU). A hermit. Seeing that old age
<div n="lb"/>was coming on him this hermit forsook his body in the
<div n="lb"/>Pṛthūdaka tīrtha and went to the realm of Viṣṇu.
<div n="lb"/>Ārṣṭiṣeṇa, Viśvāmitra, Sindhudvīpa, Devāpi and such
<div n="lb"/>others got Brāhmaṇya (the state of being Brahmin) by
<div n="lb"/>doing penance in the hermitage of this hermit. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Śānti Parva, Chapter 38, Stanzas 33-39).
