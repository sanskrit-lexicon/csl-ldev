ŚAMBHU II¦. Grandson of Tvaṣṭā, son of Kaśyapa by
Surabhi. Tvaṣṭā had a son called Viśvarūpa who begot
fourteen sons of whom Hara, Bahurūpa, Tryambaka,
Aparājita, Vṛṣākapi, Śambhu, Kapardī, Raivata,
Mṛgavyādha, Sarpa and Kapālī these eleven sons of
Viśvarūpa form the Ekādaśarudras. (Agni Purāṇa,
Chapter 18).
