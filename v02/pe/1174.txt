BHOJAKAṬA¦. The capital of Vidarbha. Once Saha-
<div n="lb"/>deva, one of the Pāṇḍavas conquered this city. It was
<div n="lb"/>at this place that Śrī Kṛṣṇa defeated Rukmī, the brother
<div n="lb"/>of Rukmiṇī at the time of Rukmiṇī's Svayaṃvara. The
<div n="lb"/>original name of Bhojakaṭa was “Kuṇḍinapura”.
<div n="lb"/>(M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 31 and Udyoga Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 158).
