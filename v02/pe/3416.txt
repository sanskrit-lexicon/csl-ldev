<div n="lb"/>hermitage in Hastimatīsabhramatīsaṅgama and lived
<div n="lb"/>there. Once due to excess of rain the river flooded and
<div n="lb"/>his hermitage was washed away. So the hermit cursed
<div n="lb"/>the river. “Let the river be dried up”. Then he went
