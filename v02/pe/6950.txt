SUDHANVĀ I¦. A guard of the ends of the quarters.
<div n="lb"/>In the beginning of creation Brahmā made four guards
<div n="lb"/>of the four ends. They were Sudhanvā in the East,
<div n="lb"/>Śaṅkhapāda in the South, Ketumān in the West and
<div n="lb"/>Hiraṇyaromā in the North. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 19).
