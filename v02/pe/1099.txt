BHAṬṬI¦. A sanskrit poet who lived in the 7th century
A.D. His most important composition is the Mahā-
kāvya, Rāvaṇavadha, which has become very popular
and famous by the name Bhaṭṭikāvya. This mahākāvya
deals with the story of Rāmāyaṇa in its twentytwo
Kāṇḍas (cantos). The kāvya is believed to have been
composed at Valabhī in obedience to the request of
King Śrīdharasena.
