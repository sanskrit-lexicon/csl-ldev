SNĀNA¦. Bathing. The Purāṇas have ordered six kinds
of bathing. They are Nitya snāna (daily bath), Naimi-
ttika snāna (incidental bath), Kāmya snāna (Desir-
able), Kriyā snāna (ceremonial), Kriyāṅga snāna
(bathing only the limbs used for rites) and Malakar-
ṣaṇa snāna (Bathing to drag out excrements). (Agni
Purāṇa, Chapter 155).
