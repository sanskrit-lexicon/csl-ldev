KṚPĪ¦. (See Para 2 under Kṛpa II).
{%Later history.%} Kṛpī was brought up in the palace of
King Śantanu. She was duly married by Droṇācārya
and a son called Aśvatthāmā was born to them. (Ādi
Parva, Chapter 130). Kṛpī lamented very much over
the death of Droṇa in the great war. (Strī Parva,
Chapter 23, Verse 33).
<div n="P"/>The terms Śāradvatī and Gautamī are used as
synonyms of Kṛpī in the Mahābhārata.
