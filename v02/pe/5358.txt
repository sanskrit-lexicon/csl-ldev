PRATARDANA¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A King of the line of Pūru.
<div n="lb"/>Pratardana who was the son of the daughter of King
<div n="lb"/>Yayāti ruled the country after making Kāśī his capital.
<div n="lb"/>Pratardana's mother was Mādhavī, Yayāti's daughter,
<div n="lb"/>and father, Divodāsa. Pratardana once found
<div n="lb"/>on his way his grandfather, King Yayāti, who had
<div n="lb"/>fallen from Svarga. (See under Yayāti).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Emperor Śibi gave Pratardana a sword. (Śloka 80,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 166, Śānti Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He once gave a gift of a netra (eye) to the
<div n="lb"/>brahmins. (Śloka 20, Chapter 224, Śānti Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He slew the son of Vītahavya. (See under Vīta-
<div n="lb"/>havya).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Pratardana courted death after appointing his
<div n="lb"/>sons for the service of brahmins. (Śloka 5, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>137, Anuśāsana Parva).
