VĀSUKITĪRTHA¦. A holy place situated on the banks
of the Gaṅgā in Prayāga. Bhogavatītīrtha is another
name of this holy bath. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata
Vana Parva, Chapter 85, Stanza 86, that by taking
bath in this holy tīrtha one could attain the fruits of
performing the horse-sacrifice.
