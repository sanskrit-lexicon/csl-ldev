CEṂB¦(U). (COPPER). The sperm of Lord Śiva
which was dropped into the river Gaṅgā developed into
form and gave birth to Subrahmaṇya. Along with
Subrahmaṇya came out from Gaṅgā gold and silver
and from its heat steel and copper. From the dirt came
out tin. (Sarga 37, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
