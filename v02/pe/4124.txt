MAHĀMĀLĪ¦. A Rākṣasa hero of Rāvaṇa's army. He
was killed in the battle at Pañcavaṭī between Rāma and
Lakṣmaṇa on one side and Khara, Dūṣaṇa and Triśiras
on the other, over the affair of Śūrpaṇakhā. Mahāmālī
appeared on the scene as a commander of Khara's
army. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa. Araṇya Kāṇḍa, 26th
Sarga).
