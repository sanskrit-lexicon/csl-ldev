VIDYĀDHARA¦(S). A group of semi-gods. Vidyādharas,
<div n="lb"/>Apsarases (celestial maids), Yakṣas, Rākṣasas, Gandhar-
<div n="lb"/>vas, Kinnaras and so on are semigods. Of these Kinnar-
<div n="lb"/>as are lute-players, and Vidyādharas wear garlands,
<div n="lb"/>and all these groups live in the sky. (Agni Purāṇa,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 51). It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Ādi
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 56, Stanza 8, that when attracted by
<div n="lb"/>spells and incantations, Indra went to the Sarpasatra
<div n="lb"/>(serpent sacrifice) of Janamejaya, the Vidyādharas
<div n="lb"/>walked behind him (Indra).
