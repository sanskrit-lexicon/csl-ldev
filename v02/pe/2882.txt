JAPA¦(S). A group of Gods (devas) of the third
Manvantara (age of Manu). In that Manvantara the
Manu was Uttama, Indra was Suśānti, and the
Devagaṇas (groups of gods) were Sudharmas, Satyas,
Japas, Pratardanas, and Vaśavartins, the five groups,
each consisting of twelve members. (See under
Manvantara).
