MUÑJAKEŚA¦. A Kṣatriya King of ancient Bhārata.
He was the re-birth of an asura called Nicandra.
(Śloka 21, Chapter 67, Ādi Parva). The Pāṇḍavas had
sent an invitation to this King to participate in the
great battle. (Śloka 14, Chapter 4, Udyoga Parva).
