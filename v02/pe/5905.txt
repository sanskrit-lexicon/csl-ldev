SAHAJANYĀ¦. An apsarā woman. The following in-
<div n="lb"/>formation about her occurs in the Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(1) She is one of the six noble celestial girls, the other
<div n="lb"/>five being Urvaśī, Pūrvacitti, Menakā, Ghṛtācī and
<div n="lb"/>Viśvācī. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 74, Verse 69).
<div n="NI"/>(2) She was present at the Birthday Celebrations
<div n="lb"/>of Arjuna. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 122, Verse 64).
<div n="NI"/>(3) She is an actress in Kubera's court. (Sabhā Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 10, Verse 11).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Sahajanyā was among the celestial women who
<div n="lb"/>danced when Arjuna came to Devaloka. (Vana Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 43, Verse 30).
