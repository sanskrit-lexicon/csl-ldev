PRĀCĪNĀVĪTA¦. Wearing of an upper apparel or sacred
thread by brahmins on the left shoulder and through
the right arm-pit is called Upavīta and vice versa is
called Prācīnāvīta. If it is worn as a necklace it is called
Nivīta. (Chapter 2, Śloka 63, Manusmṛti).
