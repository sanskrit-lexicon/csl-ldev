PĀRIYĀTRA¦. A mountain of Purāṇic fame. The deity
of this mountain Pāriyātra was a member of the
court of Kubera. The āśrama of the celebrated sage
Gautama, was on this mountain. The sage Mārkaṇḍeya
once saw this mountain in the belly of Bālamukunda.
This mountain is situated on the western side of Mahā-
meru. (Śloka 115, Chapter 188, Vana Parva).
