SIDDHA¦ II (THE SIDDHAS). A group of Devas. The
group of devas called Siddhas lived on the Himālayas
near the hermitage of Kaṇva. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chap-
ter 70, Verse 15). It is mentioned in Mahābhārata,
Sabhā Parva, Chapter 8, Verse 29, that this group
of Gods stay in the palace of Yama serving him.
