GAYA IV¦. A King born in the dynasty of the famous
emperor Pṛthu. Pṛthu had two sons called Antardhāna
and Vādī. A son called Havirdhāna was born to Antar-
dhāna by Śikhaṇḍī; he (Havirdhāna) married Dhiṣaṇā
born in the family of Agni and they had six sons called
Prācīnabarhis, Śukra, Gaya, Kṛṣṇa, Vraja and Ajina.
(Harivaṃśa, Chapter 2, Verse 31).
