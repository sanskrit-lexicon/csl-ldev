SURĀṢṬRAM I¦. A kingdom of Purāṇic fame on the
south-western part of ancient India. Ākṛti, king of
this country, was once defeated by Sahadeva, one of
the Pāṇḍavas. (Sabhā Parva, Chapter 61). Holy place
like Camasodbheda, Prabhāsakṣetra, Piṇḍāraka etc.
are situated in this area. (Vana Parva, Chapter 81,
Verse 19).
