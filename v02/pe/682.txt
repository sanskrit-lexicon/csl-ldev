ĀDIKŪRMA¦. The mountain of Mandara which was
used as a churn-stick to churn the great milky ocean,
Kṣīrābdhi went down the ocean. Then Mahāviṣṇu
took the shape of a tortoise to lift it up and this first
tortoise was called Ādikūrma. (Śloka 12, Chapter 18,
Ādi Parva, M.B.).
