<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} Another Kṣatriya King of
<div n="lb"/>ancient India. He was the son of the King Vidaṇḍa.
<div n="lb"/>In Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, it is mentioned
<div n="lb"/>that Vidaṇḍa and Daṇḍa had attended Draupadī
<div n="lb"/>Svayaṃvara (the marriage of Draupadī).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%} (1) Bhīmasena defeated the
<div n="lb"/>King Daṇḍa. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 307, Stanza
<div n="lb"/>177).
<div n="NI"/>(2) This King Daṇḍa was the brother of Daṇḍadhara,
<div n="lb"/>the king of Magadha. Daṇḍadhara and Daṇḍa were
<div n="lb"/>killed by Arjuna in battle. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>18, Stanza 16).
