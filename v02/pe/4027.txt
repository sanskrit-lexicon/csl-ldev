LUSA¦. A Ṛgvedic hermit. It is mentioned in Ṛgveda
that Luśa and Kutsa were rivals to win the mercy of
Indra.
<div n="NI"/>Once Luśa and Kutsa invited Indra at the same time.
But Indra refused Luśa and put him in prison. Even
from the prison Luśa prayed to Indra to visit him.
(Ṛgveda, Maṇḍala 10, Anuvāka 88, Sūkta 5; Jai-
minīya Brāhmaṇa 1. 128).
