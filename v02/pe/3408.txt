KĀTYĀYANĪ¦. One of the two wives of Yājñavalkya.
After withdrawing completely from worldly activities,
Yājñavalkya asked his wives to divide his worldly assets
equally between them. But Maitreyī the other wife, being
of a deeply spiritual nature, Kātyāyanī herself had to
undertake the burden of all the material affairs. (Bṛha-
dāraṇyakopaniṣad).
