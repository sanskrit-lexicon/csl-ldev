SUMUKHA I¦. A nāga, son of Kaśyapa Prajāpati by
his wife Kadrū. Sumukha was the grandson of the nāga
called Āryaka of the Airāvata dynasty and his mother
was the daughter of Vāma. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter
103, Verse 24). For the story about the marriage of
Sumukha with Guṇakeśī, daughter of Mātali see under
Guṇakeśī.
