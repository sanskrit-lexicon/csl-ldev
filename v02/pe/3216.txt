KĀṆABHŪTI¦. A devil. His name in the previous life
was “Supratīka”. Once he made friends with the
devil Sthūlaśiras. Kubera who became angry at this,
cursed Supratīka and turned him into a devil. Supra-
tīka settled down in the Vindhya mountain, assuming
the new name “Kāṇabhūti”. After narrating Bṛhat-
kathā to Guṇāḍhya, Kāṇabhūti assumed his former
form. (For further details, see under the word GUṆĀ-
ḌHYA).
