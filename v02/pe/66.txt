AGNIMUKHA¦. An Asura.
<div n="NI"/>1. {%Genealogy.%} He was descended from Viṣṇu in this
<div n="lb"/>order: Viṣṇu-Brahmā-Marīci-Kaśyapa-Śūrapadma-
<div n="lb"/>Agnimukha.
<div n="NI"/>2. {%Birth.%} Śūrapadma married Maya's daughter and
<div n="lb"/>Agnimukha was born as their son. In the battle bet-
<div n="lb"/>ween the devas and asuras, the latter were defeated and
<div n="lb"/>one of them sought shelter in Pātāla (the lower world).
<div n="lb"/>Kaśyapa married his daughter, Surasā. They had six
<div n="lb"/>children; they were: Śūrapadma, Siṃhika, Siṃha-
<div n="lb"/>vaktra, Tārakāsura, Gomukha, and Ajāmukhī. Sūra-
<div n="lb"/>padma married Maya's daughter. Agnimukha was one
<div n="lb"/>of their four sons, the other three being Bhānugopa,
<div n="lb"/>Vajrabāhu and Hiraṇya. (Skanda Purāṇa, Āsura-
<div n="lb"/>kāṇḍa). In the Skanda Purāṇa there is a vivid descrip-
<div n="lb"/>tion of the valiant way in which Agnimukha fought
<div n="lb"/>in the battle between the devas and asuras.
