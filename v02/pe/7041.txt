ŚUKRA III¦. A king who belonged to the dynasty of
Emperor Pṛthu. Two sons, Antardhāna and Vādī were
born to Pṛthu. Antardhāna had a son named Havir-
dhāna by wife Śikhaṇḍinī. Havirdhāna married Dhiṣaṇā
who was born in Agnikula. Six sons were born to them.
They were, Prācīnabarhis, Śukra, Gaya, Kṛṣṇa, Vraja
and Ajina. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part 1, Chapter 14).
