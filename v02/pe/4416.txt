MĀRUTA¦ (M). An ancient place of habitation of
south Bhārata. Those who stood on the right side of
the Krauñcāruṇa Vyūha (a battle array) constructed
by Dhṛṣṭadyumna in the Kurukṣetra battle were the
people of Māruta from south Bhārata. (Śloka 57,
Chapter 50, Bhīṣma Parva).
