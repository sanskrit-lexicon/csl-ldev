YUDHĀJIT II¦. A King of Avantī. Līlāvatī, the daugh-
ter of Yudhājit was married by Sudarśana of the
[Page899-b+ 65]
Ikṣvāku dynasty. In course of time their relations be-
came hostile and they separated. At last Yudhājit exiled
Sudarśana from his country and crowned his own
brother, Śatrujit as King of Ayodhyā. (See under
Dhruvasandhi).
