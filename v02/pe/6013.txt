SĀLVA III¦. A king of the low castes. The details given
below about this king are from Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(1) This king attacked the vast army of the Pāṇḍavas
in the battle-field of Kurukṣetra. (M.B. Śalya Parva,
Chapter 20, Verse 1).
<div n="NI"/>(2) The elephant of this Sālva was as big as a mountain
and belonged to the family of elephants called Mahā-
bhadra. (Śalya Parva, Chapter 20, Verse 2).
<div n="NI"/>(3) Duryodhana had honoured this low caste King
Sālva. (M.B. Śalya Parva, Chapter 2, Verse 2).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Sātyaki killed this king Sālva; and Dhṛṣṭadyumna
killed his elephant in the battle of Bhārata. (M.B. Śalya
Parva, Chapter 20, Verse 4).
