SUDEVA III¦. The son of Haryaśva, the King of Kāśī.
He was quite valiant and radiant. After the death of
his father he was anointed as King of Kāśī. As soon as
he became King, the sons of King Vītahavya attacked
Sudeva and captured him. After this Divodāsa became
the King of Kāśī. (M.B. Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter
30, Verse 13).
