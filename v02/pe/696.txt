ĀGNEYĀSTRA¦. A powerful weapon or missile. One
<div n="lb"/>night Arjuna fought against a gandharva, Aṅgāraparṇa,
<div n="lb"/>on the banks of the river Ganges. Arjuna then described
<div n="lb"/>to him how he came into possession of this missile. This
<div n="lb"/>powerful missile was given first to Bhāradvāja by Bṛhas-
<div n="lb"/>pati and Bharadvāja gave it to Agniveśya who in turn
<div n="lb"/>gave it to Droṇa and the latter gave it to Arjuna, his
<div n="lb"/>most favourite disciple. (Ślokas 29-30, Chapter 170, Ādi
<div n="lb"/>Parva, M.B.).
