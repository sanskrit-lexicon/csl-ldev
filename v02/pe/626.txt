AUDAKA¦. This is the place where Narakāsura with his
ten thousand girls was once imprisoned. It is a plateau
on the top of Maṇiparvata. Because water was in
plenty there the place was called ‘Audaka’. An asura
named Nūrū was the keeper of this plateau. (Sabhā
Parva, M.B.).
