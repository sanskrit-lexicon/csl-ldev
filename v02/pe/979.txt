BHADRAMATĀ¦ (BHADRAMANĀ). Daughter born to
Kaśyapa prajāpati of his wife, Krodhavaśā. The ten
daughters of Krodhavaśā are: Mṛgī, Mṛgamandā,
Harī, Bhadramatā, Mātaṅgī, Śārddūlī, Śvetā, Surabhi,
Surasā and Kadru. (Sarga 14, Āraṇya Kāṇḍa,
Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
