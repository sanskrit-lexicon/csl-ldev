<div n="lb"/>Matsya. He was called Sūryadatta too. Further he
<div n="lb"/>was the commander-in-chief of the army of Virāṭa.
<div n="lb"/>When Virāṭa's cows were lifted by the Kauravas,

<div n="lb"/>Trigartas, who wore golden helmets on their heads.
<div n="lb"/>(Virāta Parva, Chapter 31). He was an ally of the
<div n="lb"/>Pāṇḍavas in the great war. He was wounded in the
<div n="lb"/>war by Bhīṣma and killed by Śalya. (Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 167, Verse 30; Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 118,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 27).
