NIKUMBHA I¦. A very mighty Rākṣasa. Son of Kum-
bhakarṇa (Rāvaṇa's brother) by his wife Vajramālā.
He had an elder brother called Kumbha.
<div n="NI"/>When Kumbha was killed in the Rāma-Rāvaṇa war
Nikumbha rushed to the front line and fought fiercely,
and he was killed by Hanūmān. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
Yuddha Kāṇḍa, Canto 77).
