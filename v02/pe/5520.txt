PURUKUTSA¦. The son born to the celebrated king,
Māndhātā of his wife Bindumatī. (See under Māndhātā
for Genealogy). Purukutsa had a brother named Mucu-
kunda. Descending in order from Purukutsa were
Araṇya—Bṛhadaśva—Haryaśva—Tridhanvā—Aruṇa—
Satyavrata—Triśaṅku.
<div n="NI"/>One Purukutsa is praised in the Ṛgveda. It is not
known whether both are one and the same person.
Purukutsa with his wife Narmadādevī went to the for-
est of Kurukṣetra and doing penance there attained
mokṣa. (Chapter 20, Āśramavāsika Parva).
