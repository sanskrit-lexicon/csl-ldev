MAHIṢMĀN I¦. A King of the Hehaya royal family.
It was this King who constructed on the banks of the
river Narmadā, Māhiṣmatī, the capital city of
Kārtavīryārjuna. It got the name of Māhiṣmatī be-
cause it was constructed by Mahiṣmān (Chapter 2,
Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa).
