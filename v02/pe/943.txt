<L>943<pc>108-a<k1>बर्हणाश्व<k2>बर्हणाश्व
BARHAṆĀŚVA¦. A king born of the dynasty of emperor
Pṛthu. Descending in order: Pṛthu-Viśvarandhi-Candra-
Yuvanāśva-Śāvanta-Bṛhadaśva-Dhundhumāra (alias
Valayāśva)-Dṛḍhāśva-Haryaśva-Nikumbha and to
Nikuṃbha was born Barhaṇāśva and from him Kṛśāśva-
Prasenajit-Yuvanāśva-Māndhātā. (Saptama Skandha,
Devī Bhāgavata).
