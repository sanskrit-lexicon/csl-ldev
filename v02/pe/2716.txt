HŪHŪ¦. A Gandharva, son of Kaśyapaprajāpati by
Pradhā.
<div n="NI"/>(1) He was present at the birthday celebrations of
Arjuna. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 122, Verse 59).
<div n="NI"/>(2) Hūhū was among the Gandharvas who welcomed
Arjuna in Devaloka. (Vana Parva, Chapter 48, Verse 14).
<div n="NI"/>(3) Hūhū lives in Indrasabhā.
<div n="NI"/>(4) Once he was turned into an alligator on account of
the curse of Devala. (See under Indradyumna).
