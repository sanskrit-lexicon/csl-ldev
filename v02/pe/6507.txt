SAUGANDHIKA¦. A flower-garden of Kubera. It is assumed
that Vāyu (the wind-god) carried fragrance from this
garden and remained in the palace of Kubera, praising
him. This garden was full of sweet-scented lotus
(Saugandhika-flower). (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter,
10).
