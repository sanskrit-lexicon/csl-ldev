TĀRĀDATTĀ¦. Wife of Kaliṅgadatta, King of Takṣaśilā
situated on the shores of the river Vitastā. Once a
celestial maiden named Surabhidattā came to be born
as the daughter of Tārādattā due to a curse of Indra.
She was then called Kaliṅgasenā. (See under Dharma-
datta).
