SVARGATĪRTHA¦. A holy place. This place is in the
forest Naimiṣa. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Anuśā-
sana Parva, Chapter 25, Verse 33, that he who lives in
this place for a month and gives offerings to the manes,
will obtain the fruits of performing Puruṣamedha yajña
(A sacrifice).
