DURMUKHA VI¦. An asura on the side of Mahiṣāsura.
Tāmra, finance minister of Mahiṣāsura sent Durmukha
with Bāṣkala to fight against Devī, who killed him.
(Devībhāgavata, Skandhas 5, 13). In his previous
life he was one of the Paulastyas. (Ādi Parva, Chapter
61).
