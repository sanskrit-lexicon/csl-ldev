BHĀSĪ¦. Daughter of Tāmrā, Dakṣa's daughter. Kaśyapa
prajāpati married the following daughters of Dakṣa:
Aditi, Diti, Danu, Kālikā, Tāmrā, Krodhavaśā, Manu
and Analā, Tāmrā had five daughters, viz. Krauñcī,
Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī. And, the sons of
Bhāsī are the Deva sect called the Bhāsas. (Vālmīki
Rāmāyaṇa, Āraṇya Kāṇḍa, Canto 14).
