INDRADYUMNA¦(M). A lake. A stork called Nāḍī-
jaṃgha and the Ādikūrma called Akūpāra lived in this
[Page329-b+ 65]
lake. It came into existence when the cows given in gift
by King Indradyumna passed along that way. (M.B.
Araṇya Parva, Chapter 198). The pool lay near
Mount Gandhamādana, and the Pāṇḍavas once visit-
ed it. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 118, Verse 18).
