UTTARAPĀÑCĀLA¦. An ancient country in Bhārata.
Drupada became the King of this country on the death
of King Pṛṣata. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 129, Stanza
43). In course of time Uttarapāñcāla came under the
control of the Ācārya Droṇa. (See under Droṇa). In the
Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 137, Stanzas 70 to
76 it is mentioned that this country was on the north
bank of the Gaṅgā.
