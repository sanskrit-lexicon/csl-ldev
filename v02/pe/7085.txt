SUMANTU¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A maharṣi, disciple of Vyāsa. Asita, Devala,
Vaiśampāyana, Sumantu and Jaimini were the chief
disciples of Vyāsa. (See under Guruparamparā and
Bhārata).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Vyāsa taught him all the Vedas and the Mahā-
bhārata. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 63, Verse 89).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He was a member in the court of Yudhiṣṭhira.
(Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 11).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) He was one of the munis who visited Bhīṣma on
his bed of arrows. (Śānti Parva, Chapter 47, Verse 5).
