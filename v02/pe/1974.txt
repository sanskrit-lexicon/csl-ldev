<div n="lb"/>with the name Dhaumya. In Mahābhārata, Anuśāsana
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 14, Stanza 112, it is mentioned that
<div n="lb"/>this hermit was the brother and teacher of hermit
<div n="lb"/>Upamanyu. He had been keeping contact with

<div n="lb"/>Ayodha Dhaumya, Āyodha Dhaumya, Apodhadhaumya,

<div n="lb"/>details see under Ayodhadhaumya).
