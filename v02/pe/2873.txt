JANĀRDANA I¦. A synonym of Mahāvīṣṇu (Śrī Kṛṣṇa).
Because he made the Dasyus (Asuras—demons) tremble,
Śrī Kṛṣṇa was called Janārdana.
<div n="NI"/>“He, who has lotus as his seat, who is eternal, imperi-
shable and unchangeable, he who has all these attribu-
tes is Puṇḍarīkākṣa (lotus-eyed) and he who makes the
Asuras tremble is Janārdana.” (M.B. Udyoga Parva,
Chapter 70, Stanza 6).
