KUKURA I¦. (KUKŪRA). A King of the Vṛṣṇi
dynasty. From Vṛṣṇi the descendants are in the
following order:—Yudhājit—Śini—Satyaka—Sātyaki
(Yuyudhāna)—Jaya—Kuṇi—Anamitra—Pṛśni—
Citraratha—Kukura. As Kukura was a very reputed
King his successors were also called Kukuras. The
Kṣatriyas of this dynasty were subject to the orders of
Śrī Kṛṣṇa. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter 28). Members of
the Kukura and Andhaka dynasties became drunkards,
and at last quarrelled with one another and died.
(Mausala Parva, Chapter 3).
