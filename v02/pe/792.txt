<div n="lb"/>Kapila Sāṃkhyadarśana and the guru of the maharṣi
<div n="lb"/>Pañcaśikha. Once Āsuri had a full vision of God. He

<div n="lb"/>Maharṣis. The Bhāgavata says that Āsuri received his
