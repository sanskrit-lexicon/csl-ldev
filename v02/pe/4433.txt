MĀSAVRATOPAVĀSA¦. Purāṇas mention about the
<div n="lb"/>months in which Vratas are to be observed with efficacy
<div n="lb"/>and the details are given below:
<div n="NI"/>(1) He who fasts for half a day in the month of Tulām will
<div n="lb"/>get children and vehicles in plenty and would become
<div n="lb"/>pure. (Śloka 29, Chapter 106, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(2) He who fasts the whole day of the Dvādaśī in Tulām
<div n="lb"/>and worships Viṣṇu will get the benefit of making a
<div n="lb"/>Sahasragodāna (giving away thousand cows as gift).
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 3, Chapter 106, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(3) He who fasts for one time of the day in Vṛścikam
<div n="lb"/>will become a very brave and valorous man and
<div n="lb"/>acquire many wives and fame. (Śloka 30, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>106, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(4) All men and women who worship Viṣṇu on the
<div n="lb"/>dvādaśī day in Vṛścikam fasting for the whole day will
<div n="lb"/>get the benefit of a Godānayajña. (Śloka 14, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>109, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(5) He who fasts for one time a day during the month
<div n="lb"/>of Meṣa will get gold, diamonds and pearls in plenty
<div n="lb"/>and will be born in a high family in his next birth.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 23, Chapter 106, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(6) If one fasts the whole of the Dvādaśī day in the
<div n="lb"/>month of Meṣa one would get the benefit of conducting
<div n="lb"/>a Puṇḍarīka. (Śloka 7 Chapter 109, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(7) He who fasts for one time in a day in the month of
<div n="lb"/>Mithuna will become very prosperous. (Śloka 25, Chap-
<div n="lb"/>ter 106, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(8) If one fasts the whole day and night of the Dvādaśī
<div n="lb"/>in Mithuna and does Trivikrama pūjā one would not
<div n="lb"/>only get the benefit of conducting a Gomedayajña but
<div n="lb"/>will be able to enjoy sexual pleasures with celestial
<div n="lb"/>maidens. (Śloka 9, Chapter 109, Anuśāsana Parva).
