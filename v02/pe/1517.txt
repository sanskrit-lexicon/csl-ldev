CEKITĀNA¦. (SĀTVATA, VĀRṢṆEYA).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} An archer of the Vṛṣṇi dynasty.
[Page181-b+ 65]
He lived for the Pāṇḍavas and attained Vīra Svarga
(Heaven for the brave) in the great battle.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Important events.%} (1) Cekitāna was present at the
svayaṃvara of Draupadī. (Chapter 171, Śloka 18,
Udyoga Parva).
<div n="NI"/>2) When the Pāṇḍavas entered the hall constructed by
Maya for the first time Cekitāna was with them.
(Śloka 27, Chapter 4, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>3) At the time of the Rājasūya he approached Dharma-
putra and presented him with an arrow-holder. (Śloka 9,
Chapter 53, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>4) On the first day of the great battle this great archer
wrestled with Suśarmā. (Śloka 60, Chapter 45, Bhīṣma
Parva).
<div n="NI"/>5) At the Kurukṣetra in the combat with Kṛpācārya
both of them fainted. (Śloka 31, Chapter 84, Bhīṣma
Parva).
<div n="NI"/>6) He wrestled with Citrasena. (Śloka 8, Chapter 110,
Bhīṣma Parva).
<div n="NI"/>7) Cekitāna fought with Anuvinda in the Kurukṣetra
battle. (Śloka 48, Chapter 14, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>8) Droṇācārya defeated Cekitāna in the great battle.
(Śloka 68, Chapter 125, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>9) Duryodhana killed Cekitāna in the great battle.
(Śloka 31, Chapter 12, Śalya Parva).
<div n="NI"/>10) When Vyāsa by his yogic powers invited the spirits
of all the dead warriors on the banks of the Gaṅgā the
spirit of Cekitāna was also there. (Śloka 12, Chapter 32,
Āśramavāsika Parva).
