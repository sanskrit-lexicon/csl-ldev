PIṄGALĀ II¦. A prostitute of the country of Avantī.
<div n="lb"/>Piṅgalā was born as the daughter of King Candrāṅgada
<div n="lb"/>in her next birth because of the virtuous act she did
<div n="lb"/>in doing service to the sage Ṛṣabha. The King named
<div n="lb"/>the girl Kīrtimālinī and Bhadrāyu married her. (See
<div n="lb"/>under Bhadrāyu).
