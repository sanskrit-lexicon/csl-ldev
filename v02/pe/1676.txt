DAKṢIṆASINDHU¦. A holy place. This place is on the
<div n="lb"/>shore of the southern sea. If one visits this place and
<div n="lb"/>takes bath there he will get the fruits of performing the
<div n="lb"/>sacrifice of Agniṣṭoma, and the opportunity of travelling
<div n="lb"/>by the aeroplane of the gods. (M.B. Vana Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 82, Stanza 53).
