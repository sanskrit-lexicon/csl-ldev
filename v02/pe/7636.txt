TURVASU¦. A son born to Yayāti of his wife Devayānī.
<div n="lb"/>Yayāti had two wives Śarmiṣṭhā and Devayānī. Of
<div n="lb"/>Śarmiṣṭhā, Yayātī had three sons named Druhyu,
<div n="lb"/>Anudruhyu and Pūru and of Devayānī, two sons named
<div n="lb"/>Yadu and Turvasu.
<div n="NI"/>Once Yayāti called all his five sons to his side and ask-
<div n="lb"/>ed them if any one of them would be willing to ex-
<div n="lb"/>change his youth with his father's old age. All the four
<div n="lb"/>refused but Pūru agreed to accede to his father's request.
<div n="lb"/>Yayāti then cursed Turvasu and all the other three.
