SAMUDRASENA¦. A Kṣatriya king. He was a wise
man, well-versed in Economics. He was the rebirth of
Kāleya, an asura. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Verse
54). Bhīmasena defeated this Samudrasena, during
his regional conquest of the east. Samudrasena was
killed by the Pāṇḍavas in the Bhārata battle. (M.B.
Sabhā Parva, Chapter 30; Karṇa Parva, Chapter 67).
