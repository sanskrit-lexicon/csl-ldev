JAYA V¦. The pseudo name taken by Yudhiṣṭhira at the
time of the pseudonymity of the Pāṇḍavas in the coun-
try of Virāṭa. Bhīma was known as Jayanta, Arjuna by
the name Vijaya, Nakula by the name Jayatsena and
Sahadeva by the name Jayadbala at that time. (Virāṭa
Parva, Chapter 5, Stanza 35). At that time Pāñcālī
addressed all her five husbands by the name Jaya.
(M.B. Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 23, Stanza 12).
