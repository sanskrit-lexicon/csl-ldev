DAṂBHA¦. A dānava or asura. Daṃbha was the son of
<div n="lb"/>Vipracitti an asura born to Kaśyapa by his wife Danu.
<div n="lb"/>This Daṃbha is the father of Śaṅkhacūḍa, an asura.
<div n="lb"/>Daṃbha received from their teacher Śukra, the spell
<div n="lb"/>known as the famous Viṣṇumantra and went to Puṣkara-
<div n="lb"/>tīrtha and did penance there for one lac of years. It was
<div n="lb"/>due to this penance that the son Śaṅkhacūda was born
<div n="lb"/>to him. (Devī Bhāgavata, Skandha 9).
