DAṂBHA¦. A dānava or asura. Daṃbha was the son of
Vipracitti an asura born to Kaśyapa by his wife Danu.
This Daṃbha is the father of Śaṅkhacūḍa, an asura.
Daṃbha received from their teacher Śukra, the spell
known as the famous Viṣṇumantra and went to Puṣkara-
tīrtha and did penance there for one lac of years. It was
due to this penance that the son Śaṅkhacūda was born
to him. (Devī Bhāgavata, Skandha 9).
