BRĀHMAHUTAM¦. A sacrifice. There are five kinds of
sacrifice, known as Āhuta, Huta, Prahuta, Brahma-
huta and Prāśita. Āhuta is sacrifice to Brahmā, Huta
is sacrifice to devas or Gods, Prahuta is sacrifice to
Ghosts, Brahmahuta is sacrifice to man and Prāśita is
the sacrifice to the manes. (Manusmṛti, Chapter 3).
