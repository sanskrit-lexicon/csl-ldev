SAPTANĀGA¦ (S). (Seven serpents). The Saptanāgas
are Ananta, Takṣaka, Karka, Padma, Mahāpadma,
Śaṅkhaka and Gulika. All of them should be consecrat-
ed in temples as wearing Brahma-string and having the
face with the expanded hood of a serpent. (Agni
Purāṇa, Chapter 51).
