DEVĀNĪKA¦. A king born in Śrī Rāma's dynasty.
<div n="lb"/>(Solar). Kuśa was Śrī Rāma's son, Aditi the son of
<div n="lb"/>Kusa, Niṣadha the son of Aditi, Nabhas Niṣadha's son,
<div n="lb"/>Puṇḍarīka the son of Nabhas, Kṣemadhanvā the son
[Page211-a+ 60]
<div n="lb"/>of Puṇḍarika and Devānīka the son of Kṣemadhanvā.
<div n="lb"/>In the navama skandha of Bhāgavata it is said that
<div n="lb"/>Devānīka was the grandfather of Pāriyātra and father
<div n="lb"/>of Ṛkṣa.
