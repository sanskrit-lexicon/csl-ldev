SAHYA¦. A mountain on the plain of Lavaṇasamudra
(salt sea). Monkeys, in the course of their search for
Sītā crossed this mountain, which is one of the sapta-
kulaparvatas (seven great mountains) in India.
Nahuṣa once picnicked on this mountain along with
apsarā women. (Udyoga Parva, Chapters 11 and 12;
Vana Parva, Chapter 282; Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 9).
