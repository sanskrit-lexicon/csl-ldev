<div n="NI"/>(i) He was of the family of Aṅgiras and he lived in a
<div n="lb"/>hermitage in the east. (Chapter 208, Śānti Parva, and

<div n="NI"/>(ii) He praised Viṣṇu by reciting the Ṛks with deep

<div n="NI"/>(iv) Kakṣīvān was famous among the Yajvans. (Sūkta
<div n="lb"/>18, Anuvāka 5, Maṇḍala 1, Ṛgveda).
