UTTĀNAPĀDA¦. A King. He was the son of Svāyam-
bhuva Manu, the son of Brahmā. Svāyambhuva Manu
had two sons Priyavrata and Uttānapāda. (Devī Bhāga-
vata, Skandha 8). A son named Uttama was born to
Uttānapāda by his wife Suruci. Uttānapāda had an-
other wife called Sunīti. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1,
Chapter 11). See under Dhruva.
