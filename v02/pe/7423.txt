ŚVETA III¦. A son of the Virāṭa King. Śveta was the
son of King Virāṭa by his chief wife Surathā, who was
a princess of Kosala. Śveta had attended Yudhiṣṭhira's
Rājasūya. This distinguished warrior was killed by
Bhīṣma in Bhārata Yuddha. (M.B. Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭha,
Virāṭa Parva, Chapter 16; Sabhā Parva, Chapter 44,
Verse 20; Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 48).
