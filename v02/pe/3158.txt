KALIṄGA¦(M) (KĀLIṄGA). An ancient place in the
<div n="lb"/>south of Bhārata.
[Page376-a+ 64]
<div n="NI"/>{%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(1) Arjuna visited this place while he was on a pilgrim-
<div n="lb"/>age. (Śloka 9, Chapter 214, Ādi Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(2) Kaliṅga was also included in the countries conquer-
<div n="lb"/>ed by Sahadeva while he was on his victory march.
<div n="lb"/>(Chapter 31, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(3) People from Kaliṅga presented Yudhiṣṭhira with
<div n="lb"/>gifts for the Rājasūya. (Śloka 18, Chapter 52. Sabhā
<div n="lb"/>Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(4) Yudhiṣṭhira visited Kaliṅga while he was on a
<div n="lb"/>pilgrimage. (Śloka 4, Chapter 114, Vana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(5) Karṇa conquered Kaliṅga while he was on a
<div n="lb"/>victory march. (Śloka 8, Chapter 254, Vana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(6) Sahadeva defeated the King of Kaliṅga. (Śloka
<div n="lb"/>24, Chapter 23, Udyoga Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(7) Śrī Kṛṣṇa slaughtered the people of Kaliṅga.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 76, Chapter 48, Udyoga Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(8) The people of Kaliṅga took part in the war bet-
<div n="lb"/>ween the Pāṇḍavas and Kauravas. (Śloka 6, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>20, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(9) Paraśurāma conquered this place. (Śloka 12,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 70, Dṛoṇa Parva).
