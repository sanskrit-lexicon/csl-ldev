BHOJA I¦. A king of the ancient country named Mārtti-
kāvata. In Mahābhārata Ādi Parva, Chapter 185,
Verse 6, we see that this king had attended the Svayaṃ-
vara of Draupadī. He was slain by Abhimanyu at the
battle of Kurukṣetra. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter
48, Verse 8).
