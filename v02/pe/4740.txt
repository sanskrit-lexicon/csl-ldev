<div n="lb"/>Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa, Chapter 48, that the semen intro-
<div n="lb"/>duced into the womb of the woman by the man at
<div n="lb"/>the time of coition, will get mixed with the blood in
<div n="lb"/>the womb, and that the issue will be male, female or
<div n="lb"/>eunuch according to the proportion of the mixture. In
<div n="lb"/>the mixture of semen and blood, if blood exceeds semen
<div n="lb"/>the issue will be female and if semen exceeds, it will be
<div n="lb"/>male child and if both are equal the child will be a
<div n="lb"/>eunuch.
