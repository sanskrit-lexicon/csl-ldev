SAHASRAPĀDA¦ (SAHASRAPĀT).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A maharṣi, who was transformed into a
serpent by the curse of Khagama, another maharṣi.
But a talk with King Ruru restored him to his former
self. (For details see under Ruru, para 4).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%} Ruru desired to get some
information about serpent yajña from Sahasrapāt, who
answered the former that he would hear the story of
Āstīka from brahmins and then disappeared. (Ādi
Parva, Chapter 12, Verse 3).
