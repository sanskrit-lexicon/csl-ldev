SAHASRAPĀDA¦ (SAHASRAPĀT).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A maharṣi, who was transformed into a
<div n="lb"/>serpent by the curse of Khagama, another maharṣi.
<div n="lb"/>But a talk with King Ruru restored him to his former
<div n="lb"/>self. (For details see under Ruru, para 4).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%} Ruru desired to get some
<div n="lb"/>information about serpent yajña from Sahasrapāt, who
<div n="lb"/>answered the former that he would hear the story of
<div n="lb"/>Āstīka from brahmins and then disappeared. (Ādi
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 12, Verse 3).
