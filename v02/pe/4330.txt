MAṆIMĀN I¦. A king. Mahābhārata gives the following
details about him.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Maṇimān was born of an aspect of Vṛtra, son of the
asura Danāyu. (Śloka 44, Chapter 67, Ādi Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) This king was present at the marriage of Draupadī.
(Śloka 22, Chapter 185, Ādi Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Bhīmasena while on his early victory march defeat-
ed this king. (Śloka 11, Chapter 30, Sabhā Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) This king fought on the side of the Pāṇḍavas dur-
ing the great Kurukṣetra battle. (Śloka 20, Chapter 4,
Udyoga Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Maṇimān was killed in the fight between Maṇimān
[Page479-b+ 64]
and Bhūriśravas. (Śloka 53, Chapter 23, Droṇa
Parva).
