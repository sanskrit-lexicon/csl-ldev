PAILA¦. A disciple of Vyāsa. It was the five disciples of
Vyāsa named Sumantu, Jaimini, Paila, Śuka and
Vaiśampāyana who gave publicity to the original
Mahābhārata. (See under Guruparamparā).
[Page545-b+ 64]
He was the son of a person named Vasu and attended
the Rājasūya of Yudhiṣṭhira. (Śloka 35, Chapter 33,
Sabhā Parva). Paila was also one among those who
visited Bhīṣma while he was lying on his bed of arrows.
(Śloka 6, Chapter 17, Śānti Parva).
