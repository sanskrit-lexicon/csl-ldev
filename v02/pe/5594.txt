RĀHU¦. An Asura.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Birth.%} Son of Kaśyapaprajāpati by his wife Siṃhikā.
<div n="lb"/>(Ādi Parva. Chapter 65, Verse 31).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Rāhu and the Solar eclipse.%} The oldest story about
<div n="lb"/>Rāhu is that about the solar eclipse. (See under Candra
<div n="lb"/>IV, Para 4).
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Other information.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Rāhu is a member of Brahmā's court. (Sabhā
<div n="lb"/>Parva Chapter 11, Verse 29).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Sañjaya once spoke to Dhṛtarāṣṭra about Rāhu.
<div n="lb"/>(Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 12, Verse 40).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Rāhu exists 10,000 yojanas below the Sun. (Bhāga-
<div n="lb"/>vata, 5th Skandha).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Rāhu is installed in temples as wearing a half-moon
<div n="lb"/>on his head. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 51).
