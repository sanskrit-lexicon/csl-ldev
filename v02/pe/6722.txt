ŚREṆIMĀN¦. A Rājarṣi. Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva,
Chapter 67, Verse 51 says that he was the rebirth of
the fourth Kālakeya. The following pieces of inform-
ation about this Rājarṣi are gathered from Mahā-
bhārata:-
<div n="NI"/>(i) Śreṇimān was present at the Svayaṃvara of
Pāñcālī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Verse 11).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He ruled over Kumāradeśa. Bhīmasena, in the
triumphal march of the eastern lands, defeated Śreṇimān.
(M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 30, Verse 1).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Sahadeva, in his conquest of Southern lands
defeated him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 31,
Verse 5).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) In the course of the Bhārata Yuddha, Śreṇimān
was killed by Droṇa. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 6,
Verse 35).
