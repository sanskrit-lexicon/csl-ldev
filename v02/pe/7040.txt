ŚUKRA II¦. A son of Vasiṣṭha. Seven sons were born to
Vasiṣṭha by his wife Ūrjjā; they were, Rajas, Gotra,
Ūrddhvabāhu, Savana, Anagha, Sutapas and Śukra. All
these seven persons were the Saptarṣis of the third
Manvantaram (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part 1, Chapter 10).
<div n="NI"/>In Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 20 the names of the seven
sons of Vasiṣṭha and Ūrjjā are given as Rajas. Gātra,
Ūrddhvabāhu, Savana, Alaghu, Śukra and Sutapas.
