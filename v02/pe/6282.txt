<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A Kṣatriya of the Yadu clan.
<div n="lb"/>It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 218,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 17, that he was the son of Vasudeva by Devakī
<div n="lb"/>and the brother of Śrī Kṛṣṇa and Subhadrā.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) This Sāraṇa was one of those who took the dowry
<div n="lb"/>to Hastināpura at the marriage of Arjuna and
<div n="lb"/>Subhadrā. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 220, Verse 32).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Sāraṇa shone in the court of Yudhiṣṭhira: (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Verse 30).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Sāraṇa participated in the Rājasūya sacrifice of
<div n="lb"/>Yudhiṣṭhira. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 34, Verse 15).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) When Śrī Krṣṇa went to Hastināpura from
<div n="lb"/>Dvārakā to take part in the horse-sacrifice of Yudhi-
[Page694-b+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>ṣṭhira, Sāraṇa also accompanied him. (M.B. Aśva-
<div n="lb"/>medha Parva, Chapter 66, Verse 4).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Sāraṇa was the foremost of those who disguised
<div n="lb"/>Samba the son of Jāmbavatī as a woman, and abused
<div n="lb"/>the hermits. (M.B. Mausala Parva, Chapter 1, Verse 15).
