KRĀTHA I¦. A famous King in Ancient India. The
following details about this King are found found
in the Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(1) He was the rebirth of an Asura called Rāhu,
the son of Siṃhikā. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 67,
Stanza 40).
<div n="NI"/>(2) Krātha attended the Svayaṃvara (marriage) of
Draupadī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 186, Stanza 15).
<div n="NI"/>(3) Śrī Kṛṣṇa defeated Krātha at the city of Jāruthi.
(M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 12, Stanza 30).
<div n="NI"/>(4) In the battle of Bhārata this King attacked Abhi-
manyu. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 46, Stanza 26).
<div n="NI"/>(5) In the battle of Bhārata Krātha killed the prince of
Kaliṅga, and a King from the mountain killed
Krātha. (M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 85, Stanza 15).
