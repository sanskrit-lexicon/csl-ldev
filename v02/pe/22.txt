ACYUTĀYU¦. He was a heroic warrior on the Kaurava
side. Śrutāyu was the brother of Acyutāyu. Both of
them were killed by Arjuna in the Kaurava-Pāṇḍava
battle. These heroes attacked Arjuna and Śrī Kṛṣṇa
furiously with their arrows and Arjuna had some
difficulty in killing them. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter
93, Verses 7-42).
