CAIDYA¦. Śiśupāla, king of Cedi. Dhṛṣṭaketu, King of
Cedi, is also known by this name. It was this Caidya
who led the Krauñcavyūha created by Dhṛṣṭadyumna
in the great battle. (Krauñcavyūha = A battle array
in the shape of a stork). (Śloka 47, Chapter 50, Bhīṣma
Parva).
