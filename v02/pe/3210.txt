KĀMPILYA¦. An ancient town in South Pāñcāla. It was
the capital city of King Drupada. Śikhaṇḍī had come
[Page382-a+ 65]
to this city after his marriage. Once the King Daśārṇa
came near this place and sent a Brāhmaṇa messenger
to Kāmpilya. In olden days, a King named Brahma-
datta used to rule over this city. (M.B. Udyoga Parva).
