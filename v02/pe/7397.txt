SVARṆĀ¦. A celestial woman. By the blessings of Krauñca
a daughter named Vṛndā was born to Svarṇā. There
were none who were not fascinated by her beauty. Once
Śukra asked Svarṇā, and obtained Vṛndā, for the asura
[Page779-a+ 65]
named Jalandhara. (Padma Purāṇa, Uttara Khaṇḍa,
Chapter 4).
