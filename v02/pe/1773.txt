DAŚĀRHA¦. A king of the Yadu family. He was so famous
that his descendants were called the Daśārhas. As Śrī
Kṛṣṇa was born as a descendant in the line of Daśārha,
Śrī Kṛṣṇa is sometimes called Dāśārha, in certain places.
For genealogy see under Yaduvaṃśa.
