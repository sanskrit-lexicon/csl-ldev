<div n="lb"/>Uttānapāda. Svāyambhuvamanu had two famous sons
<div n="lb"/>called Priyavrata and Uttānapāda. Priyavrata, the elder
<div n="lb"/>son, married two daughters of Viśvakarmaprajāpati
<div n="lb"/>called Surūpā and Barhiṣmatī, and of Surūpā were born
<div n="lb"/>ten sons called Agnīdhra, Idhmajihva, Yajñabāhu,
<div n="lb"/>Mahāvīra, Rukmaśukra, Ghṛtapṛṣṭha, Savana, Medhā-

<div n="lb"/>Ūrjasvatī. (Devībhāgavata, 8th Skandha).
