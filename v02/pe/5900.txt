SAHADEVA IV¦. A son of Jarāsandha about whom the
following facts are collected from the Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(1) Asti and Prāpti, two wives, of Kaṃsa were the
sisters of this Sahadeva. (Sabhā Parva, Chapter 14,
Verse 31).
<div n="NI"/>(2) He was present at the wedding of Draupadī. (Ādi
Parva, Chapter 185, Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(3) After the death of Jarāsandha he took refuge
with Śrī Kṛṣṇa, who crowned him King of Mathurā-
purī. (M.B. Southern text, Sabhā Parva, Chapter
24).
<div n="NI"/>(4) In the great war he came to the help of Yudhi-
ṣṭhira with one akṣauhiṇī (division of army). (Udyoga
Parva, Chapter 19, Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(5) He was one of the seven Mahārathins of the
Pāṇḍava army. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter 187, Verse
11).
<div n="NI"/>(6) He was killed in the great war by Droṇa. (Droṇa
Parva, Chapter 225, Verse 45).
