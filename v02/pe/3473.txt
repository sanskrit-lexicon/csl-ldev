KEŚĪ III¦. In Bhāgavata there is another Keśī who was
the son of Vasudeva.
<Poem>“Pauravī Rohiṇī Bhadrā
Madirā Rocanā Ilā /
Devakīpramukhā āsan
Patnya Ānakadundubheḥ.”</Poem>
<div n="NI"/>Pauravī, Rohiṇī, Bhadrā, Madirā, Rocanā, Ilā and
Devakī were the wives of Vasudeva.
<div n="NI"/>From this statement made in Bhāgavata, Skandha 9,
Chapter 24, it is clear that Vasudeva had a number of
wives. Kausalyā, who was one of them, was the mother
of Keśī. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 9, Chapter 24, Stanza
48).
