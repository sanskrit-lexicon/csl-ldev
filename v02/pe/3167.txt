KALMĀṢĪ¦. A river. It was while travelling by the side
<div n="lb"/>of this river, that King Drupada reached a Brāhmaṇa
<div n="lb"/>village where he happened to meet Upayāja. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Ādi Parva, Chapter 166). Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 78, Verse 16 says that it was on the bank of
<div n="lb"/>this river that the sage Bhṛgu blessed Yudhiṣṭhira.
<div n="lb"/>(Modern scholars believe that Kanmāṣī. is the same as
<div n="lb"/>the present river Yamunā).
