NANDIGRĀMA¦. It is stated in Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
Ayodhyākāṇḍa that while Śrī Rāma had been leading
forest life, Bharata lived in Nandigrāma for twelve
years worshipping the sandals of Śrī Rāma. This
Nandigrāma is situated nearly fourteen miles away
from Ayodhyā. (Faizabad).
