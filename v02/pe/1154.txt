BHĪṢAṆA¦. Son of Baka. From the day his father was
killed by Bhīma Bhīṣana was impatiently waiting for
revenge. When the Pāṇḍavas began the Aśvamedha
yajña he obstructed it at a place near Ekacakra. Arjuna
fought and killed him. (Jaimini Aśvamedha Parva,
Chapter 22).
