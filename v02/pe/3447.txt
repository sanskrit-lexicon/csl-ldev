son of Kaśyapa and Aditi. Vaivasvata Manu was the
son of Vivasvān. Manu had sixteen sons who were—
Manu, Yama, Yamī, Aśvinīkumāras, Revanta, Sudyu-
mna, Ikṣvāku, Nṛga, Śaryāti, Diṣṭa, Dhṛṣta, Karūṣa,
Nariṣyanta, Nābhāga, Pṛṣadhra and Kavi.
