PRAMATI I¦. (PRAMITI).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} Great Sage Bhṛgu, got of his wife
<div n="lb"/>Pulomā Cyavana and Cyavana got of his wife Sukanyā,
<div n="lb"/>Pramati. This sage Pramati married the beautiful
<div n="lb"/>Pratāpī and their son was Ruru.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Pramiti visited Bhīṣma while he was lying on his
<div n="lb"/>bed of arrows. (Śloka 5, Chapter 26, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Pramiti is described as the son of Vāgindra born
<div n="lb"/>in the family of Gṛtsamada son of Vītahavya. (Śloka
<div n="lb"/>58, Chapter 30, Anuśāsana Parva). For details see
<div n="lb"/>under Ruru).
