KĀLAÑJARAGIRI¦. A famous mountain at Medhāvika
tīrtha. He who bathes in Vedīhrada on this mountain
will get the benefits of gifting away 1000 cows. (Anu-
śāsana Parva, Chapter 25). Spread on the four sides of
mount Mahāmeru are twenty mountains. They are:—
Kuraṅga, Karaga, Kuśumbha, Vikaṅkata, Trikūṭa,
Śiśira, Pataṅga, Rucaka, Nīla, Niṣadha, Śītivāsa,
Kapila, Śaṅkha, Vaiḍūrya, Cārudhi, Haṃsa, Ṛṣabha,
Nāga, Kālañjara and Nārada. (Devī Bhāgavata, 8th
Skandha).
