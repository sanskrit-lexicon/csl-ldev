SUŚĪLA II¦. A brahmin who got rich due to the observance
of Navarātri-penance. He led a very hard life with
many sons and was naturally thinking of means of mak-
ing money and a noble brahmin taught him about the
greatness Navarātri. Accordingly Suśīla observed for
nine years the Navarātri-penance and at last Devī
appeared before him and made him rich. (Devī Bhāga-
vata, Skandha 3).
