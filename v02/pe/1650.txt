DADHĪCA¦(M). A holy place in Kurukṣetra. Aṅgiras
<div n="lb"/>the son of Sarasvatī was born in this place. From that
<div n="lb"/>day onwards this place became a holy place. It is men-
<div n="lb"/>tioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 83,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 186 that he who bathes in this holy place will
[Page192-a+ 66]
<div n="lb"/>obtain the fruits of performing the sacrifice Aśvamedha,
<div n="lb"/>and enter the world of Sarasvatī.
