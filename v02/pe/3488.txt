KETUMĀLA III¦. (See under Ketumāla I). Ketumāla
<div n="lb"/>is the ninth division of Jambūdvīpa. The people of this
<div n="lb"/>part of the earth are equal to gods (Devas). The women
<div n="lb"/>are very beautiful. In Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva it is
<div n="lb"/>stated that Arjuna conquered this land. Ketumāla is
<div n="lb"/>situated on the east of Mount Meru. In Mahābhārata,
<div n="lb"/>Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 6, there is the description of
<div n="lb"/>Ketumāla.
