DVĀDAŚĀDITYA¦(S). The twelve sons born to Kaśyapa
by his wife Aditi are called Dvādaśādityas (Twelve
Ādityas) The word Āditya means ‘born of Aditi’. As it
occurs in Agni Purāṇa (Ch. 51) a table, showing the names
of the twelve Ādityas and the sign of the Zodiac over
which each Āditya predominates and the colour of it,
is given below.
