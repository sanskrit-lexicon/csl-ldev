DEVAVĪTI¦. Daughter-in-law of king Agnīdhra. Pūrva-
citti, the apsarā woman was the king's wife and they had
nine sons called Nābhi, Kiṃpuruṣa, Hari, Ilāvṛta,
Ramuka, Hirañjaya, Kuru, Bhadrāśva and Ketumāla,
and these sons married the following daughters of Meru,
viz. Merudevī, Pratirūpā, Ugradaṃṣṭrī, Latā, Ramyā,
Syāmā, Nārī, Bhadrā and Devavīti. Thus Devavīti be-
came the wife of Ketumāla. (Bhāgavata, Pañcama
Skandha).
