CAKRAVĀN¦. A mountain very much extolled in the
Purāṇas. When deputing monkeys in quest of Sītā,
Sugrīva instructed them to make a special search in
Mount Cakravān. It was on this mountain that Viśva-
karmā made the Sudarśana Cakra. (Vālmīki Rāmā-
yaṇa, Kiṣkindhā Kāṇḍa, Chapter 42, Verse 16).
