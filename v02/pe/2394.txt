GAYĀ¦. A particular locality in North India (Nepal).
<div n="lb"/>Here there is Gaya mountain and a holy centre also.
<div n="lb"/>There is legend to the effect that Buddha did tapas here.
<div n="lb"/>People of this locality gave many presents to
<div n="lb"/>Yudhiṣṭhira. (Sabhā Parva, Chapter 52, Verse 16).
