SIDDHĀŚRAMA¦. A hermitage. The place at which
<div n="lb"/>Vāmana thrust Mahābali down to Pātāla. This place
<div n="lb"/>became a hermitage later. When Viśvāmitra took Śrī
<div n="lb"/>Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa to the forest for the protection of
<div n="lb"/>his sacrifice, he showed them this hermitage. (Vālmīki
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa, Bālākāṇḍa, Sarga 29, Verse 22).
