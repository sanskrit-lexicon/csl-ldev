SIDDHĀŚRAMA¦. A hermitage. The place at which
Vāmana thrust Mahābali down to Pātāla. This place
became a hermitage later. When Viśvāmitra took Śrī
Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa to the forest for the protection of
his sacrifice, he showed them this hermitage. (Vālmīki
Rāmāyaṇa, Bālākāṇḍa, Sarga 29, Verse 22).
