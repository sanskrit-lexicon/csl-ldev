HARYAŚVA II¦. A King of the solar dynasty. The follo-
wing information about him is culled from the Mahā-
bhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(1) He was king of Ayodhyā. A Powerful ruler, he
had a fully equipped army. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter
115. Verse 18).
<div n="NI"/>(2) He took Mādhavī, daughter of Yayāti as his wife
and thus solved the problem of gurudaksiṇā for Gālava.
(See under Gālava).
[Page311-a+ 63]
<div n="NI"/>(3) Never in life did he eat flesh. (Anuśāsana Parva,
Chapter 11, Verse 67).
