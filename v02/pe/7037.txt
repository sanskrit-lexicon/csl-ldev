ŚUKĪ¦. A daughter of Kaśyapa Prajāpati. Five daughters
named Krauñcī, Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī
were born to Kaśyapa by his wife Tāmrā. Krauñcī
gave birth to owls. Bhāsas were born to Bhāsī and
eagles and kites were born to Śyenī. Dhṛtarāṣṭrī gave
birth to swans and Cakravākas. Śukī gave birth to
Natā and Vinatā was the daughter of Natā. (Vālmīki
Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇyakāṇḍa, Sarga 14).
