RAGHU¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A famous king of the Solar dynasty. The
<div n="lb"/>great king Dilīpa was his father. (For Genealogy, birth
<div n="lb"/>etc., see under Daśaratha and Dilīpa).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Raghu also occupies a place in the list of famous
<div n="lb"/>kings of ancient days. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 1, Verse
<div n="lb"/>232).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) In the fight between the king of Virāṭa and the
<div n="lb"/>Kauravas (over the lifting of cows) Raghu was present
<div n="lb"/>along with Indra to see Arjuna fight. (Virāṭa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 56, Verse 10).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Raghu got from King Yuvanāśva a sword, which
<div n="lb"/>he presented to Hariṇāśva. (Śānti Parva Chapter 166,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 78).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) He did not eat flesh. (Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>150, Verse 81).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Kṣatriyas who praise Raghu will not be defeated
<div n="lb"/>in war. (Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 165, Verse 51).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) In the list of kings to be remembered at dawn and
<div n="lb"/>at dusk Raghu too is included. (Anuśāsana Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 165, Verse 51).
