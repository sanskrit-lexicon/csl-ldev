RAGHU¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A famous king of the Solar dynasty. The
great king Dilīpa was his father. (For Genealogy, birth
etc., see under Daśaratha and Dilīpa).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other information.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Raghu also occupies a place in the list of famous
kings of ancient days. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 1, Verse
232).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) In the fight between the king of Virāṭa and the
Kauravas (over the lifting of cows) Raghu was present
along with Indra to see Arjuna fight. (Virāṭa Parva,
Chapter 56, Verse 10).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Raghu got from King Yuvanāśva a sword, which
he presented to Hariṇāśva. (Śānti Parva Chapter 166,
Verse 78).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) He did not eat flesh. (Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter
150, Verse 81).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Kṣatriyas who praise Raghu will not be defeated
in war. (Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 165, Verse 51).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) In the list of kings to be remembered at dawn and
at dusk Raghu too is included. (Anuśāsana Parva,
Chapter 165, Verse 51).
