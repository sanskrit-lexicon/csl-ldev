STHĀṆUSTHĀNA¦. A holy place. This place is situated
<div n="lb"/>on the eastern bank of the river Sarasvatī. Vasiṣṭha
<div n="lb"/>had once built his hermitage here. This place became
<div n="lb"/>a holy tīrtha since god Sthāṇu had performed penance
<div n="lb"/>here. It is said that the devas anointed Subrahmaṇya
<div n="lb"/>as the commander of the army, at this place. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Śalya Parva, Chapter 45, Verse 48).
