VṚṢASENA I¦. A son of Karṇa. The following inform-
ation is taken from Mahābhārata about him.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Vṛṣasena was a famous warrior in the army of the
Kauravas. (M.B. Udyoga Parva, Chapter 167, Stanza
23).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) In the Bhārata-battle he confronted the prominent
archers such as Śatānīka, Pāṇḍya, Abhimanyu, Arjuna,
Drupada, Sātyaki, Nakula and others. (M.B. Droṇa
Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) In the fight with Arjuna, he was killed. (M.B.
Karṇa Parva, Chapter 85, Stanza 35).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Among the brave souls of the Kurus who were
invoked to the surface of the river Gaṅgā by Vyāsa,
Vṛṣasena also was present. (M.B. Āśramavāsika Parva,
Chapter 32, Stanza 10).
