ANUHLĀDA¦. Brother of Prahlāda.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu thus: Viṣṇu,
<div n="lb"/>Brahmā, Kaśyapa Hiraṇyakaśipu, Anuhlāda.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Kaśyapa married Diti, daughter of Daṣka. Diti
<div n="lb"/>was a younger sister of Aditi. The sons of Aditi became
<div n="lb"/>devas and the sons of Diti became asuras. Diti gave
<div n="lb"/>birth to two sons named Hiraṇyākṣa and Hiraṇya-
<div n="lb"/>kaṣipu and a daughter Siṃhikā. Siṃhikā was given in
<div n="lb"/>marriage to Vipracitti. They had two sons, Rāhu and
<div n="lb"/>Ketu. Five sons were born to Hiraṇyākṣa and four sons
<div n="lb"/>were born to Hiraṇyakaśipu. These four sons were:
<div n="lb"/>Anuhlāda, Hlāda, Prahlāda and Saṃhlāda. (Viṣṇu
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 15 and Agni Purāṇa, Chap-
<div n="lb"/>ter 19).
