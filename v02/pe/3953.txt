LAKṢMAṆA II¦. A very brave son of Duryodhana. He
<div n="lb"/>was a great archer. The Mahābhārata gives the
<div n="lb"/>following information about him.
<div n="NI"/>(i) In the great battle Abhimanyu defeated this
<div n="lb"/>Lakṣmaṇa. (Śloka 32, Chapter 73, Bhīṣma Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Lakṣmaṇa fought against Kṣatradeva. (Śloka 49,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 14, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Abhimanyu killed him in the battle-field. (Śloka
<div n="lb"/>17, Chapter 46, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) After the great war when once, at the request of
<div n="lb"/>Kuntī and Gāndhārī, Vyāsa by his yogic powers
<div n="lb"/>arrayed the dead warriors for Kuntī and Gāndhārī to
<div n="lb"/>see, this Lakṣmaṇa was also among them. (Śloka 11,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 32, Āśramavāsika Parva).
