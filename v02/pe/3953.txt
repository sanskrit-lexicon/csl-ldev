LAKṢMAṆA II¦. A very brave son of Duryodhana. He
was a great archer. The Mahābhārata gives the
following information about him.
<div n="NI"/>(i) In the great battle Abhimanyu defeated this
Lakṣmaṇa. (Śloka 32, Chapter 73, Bhīṣma Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Lakṣmaṇa fought against Kṣatradeva. (Śloka 49,
Chapter 14, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Abhimanyu killed him in the battle-field. (Śloka
17, Chapter 46, Droṇa Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) After the great war when once, at the request of
Kuntī and Gāndhārī, Vyāsa by his yogic powers
arrayed the dead warriors for Kuntī and Gāndhārī to
see, this Lakṣmaṇa was also among them. (Śloka 11,
Chapter 32, Āśramavāsika Parva).
