LAVAṆA I¦. A demon. He was the son of a demon
called Madhu. Madhuvana on the banks of the river
Kālindī was the abode of this demon. This demon was
a great oppressor of the Devas and Śatrughna had to kill
him Śatrughna then constructed a beautiful city there
and lived there. In recognition of the increasing pros-
perity of the city it was named Madhurā. After the
death of Śatrughna his two sons lived there. (4th
Skandha, Devī Bhāgavata).
