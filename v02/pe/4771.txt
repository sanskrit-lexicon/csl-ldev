NĀRĪTĪRTHA¦. Common name for the five tīrthas, i.e.
<div n="lb"/>Agastya tīrtha, Saubhadratīrtha, Paulomatīrtha, Kāran-
<div n="lb"/>dhamatīrtha and Bharadvājatīrtha. Once Arjuna bathed
<div n="lb"/>in Bharadvājatīrtha. The Apsarā women called Vargās
<div n="lb"/>were living in the five tīrthas in the form of crocodiles as
<div n="lb"/>the result of a curse. With the arrival of Arjuna there, the
<div n="lb"/>crocodiles resumed their previous forms as Apsarā wo-
<div n="lb"/>men and returned to Devaloka. (For the curse etc. see
<div n="lb"/>under Vargā).
