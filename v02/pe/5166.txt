PHĀLGUNA¦. A month (March). It is so called
because the phalgunīnakṣatra yoga occurs in that
month. He who gives away as gift his meal for a
time of the day will become more loved by his wife.
[Page588-b+ 64]
Not only that, the Purāṇas declare that such a man
would attain Candraloka. (Chapter 109, Anuśāsana
Parva).
