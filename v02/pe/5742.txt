RIPU¦. Grandson of Dhruva. Two sons called Śiṣṭi and
Bhavya were born to Dhruva by his wife Śambhū.
Śiṣṭi's wife Succhāyā delivered five sons, i.e. Ripu,
Ripuñjaya, Vipra, Vṛkala and Vṛkatejas. Cākṣuṣa Manu
was born as the son of Ripu by his wife Bṛhatī.
(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part 1, Chapter 13).
