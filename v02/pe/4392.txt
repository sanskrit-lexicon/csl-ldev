<div n="NI"/>MĀRGAŚĪRṢAMĀSA. The month of Dhanu (Decem-
ber). The month has got great Purāṇic importance.
If one lives taking food only once during the whole of
this month one will be free from all sins and diseases.
(Śloka 17, Chapter 106, Anuśāsana Parva). If on the
Dvādaśī day in this month one fasts the whole day
and night and worships Keśava one will get the
benefit of conducting an Aśvamedha yajña. (Śloka 3,
Chapter 109, Anuśāsana Parva).
