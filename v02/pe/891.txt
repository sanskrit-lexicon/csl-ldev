<L>891<pc>101-a<k1>बलन्धरा<k2>बलन्धरा
BALANDHARĀ¦. A daughter of the king of Kāśī. Let
the mightiest of men marry her, said her father. Defeating
all the kings who tried to take her Bhīmasena married
her and Sarvaśa was the son born to Bhīmasena of
Balandharā. (Śloka 70, Chapter 95, Aśvamedha Parva,
M.B.).
