VANEYU¦. A King who was the grandson of emperor
Pūru and the son of Rudrāśva. Vaneyu was born to
Rudrāśva by a celestial woman named Miśrakeśī. Ten
sons named Vaneyu, Ṛceyu, Kakṣeyu, Kṛpaṇeyu,
Sthaṇḍileyu, Jaleyu, Tejeyu, Satyeyu, Dharmeyu
and Santateyu, were born to this celestial woman.
(M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 94, Stanza 8).
