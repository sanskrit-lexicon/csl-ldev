<div n="lb"/>It was a resort of thieves and robbers. Dead bodies
<div n="lb"/>were often thrown into the river which was flowing
<div n="lb"/>through this village. Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chap-
<div n="lb"/>ter 129, verse 9 says that it was against Śāstras to bathe
<div n="lb"/>in this river.
