SUNĪTI¦. Mother of Dhruva. Svāyambhuva Manu had
two sons called Priyavrata and Uttānapāda; the latter
wedded two wives named Suruci and Sunīti. Suruci's
son is called Uttama and Sunīti's son Dhruva. The
father had not much love for Sunīti and Dhruva. (See
under Uttānapāda).
