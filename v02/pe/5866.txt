ŚABALĀŚVA II¦. A King born in the dynasty of King
Kuru. His father, Avikṣit or Aśvavān was the grand-
son of King Kuru. Avikṣit had, besides Śabalāśva,
seven sons called Parīkṣit, Ādirāja, Virāja, Śālmali,
Uccaiśśravas, Bhaṃgakāra and Jitāri. (Ādi Parva,
Chapter 94, Verse 52).
