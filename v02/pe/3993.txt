LĪLĀTILAKAM¦. The first book of Literary criticism in
Malayālam. The author of the book is unknown. The
book is divided into eight parts. Maṇipravāla, Malayā-
laśabdas, Alaṅkāra, Kāvyaguṇas, Kāvyadoṣas and
Rasa are critically examined in this book.
