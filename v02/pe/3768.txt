KUKUDMIN¦. A son, Ānarta, was born to King
Śaryāti, the son of Vaivasvata Manu. Revata, the
famous Rājarṣi, who ruled from his capital at Kuśas-
thalī island, was the son of Ānarta. Ānarta had hundred
sons, the eldest being Kukudmin, and also a daughter
called Revatī. (Devī Bhāgavata, 7th Skandha).
