KṢATRADEVA¦. The son of Śikhaṇḍī. He was a
famous archer. Bhagadatta wounded the right hand of
Kṣatradeva in the Bhārata-battle. He fought with
Lakṣmaṇa and Droṇa. Lakṣmaṇa killed him.
(M. B. Udyoga Parva, Chapters 57 and 171; Bhīṣma
Parva, Chapter 95; Droṇa Parva, Chapters 14, 21 and
23; Karṇa Parva, Chapter 6).
