VĀDĪ¦. A son of emperor Pṛthu. Pṛthu had two righteous
sons called Antardhāna and Vādī. A son named Havir-
dhāna was born to Antardhāna by Śikhandinī. Dhiṣaṇā
[Page818-a+ 64]
born in the dynasty of Agni became the wife of Havir-
dhāna. Six sons named Prācīnabarhis, Śukra, Gaya,
Kṛṣṇa, Vraja and Ajina were born to the couple.
(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 14).
