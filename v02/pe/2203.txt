DURYODHANA II¦. The Mahābhārata refers to another
Duryodhana, grandson of Suvīra, and son of Durjaya.
(Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 2, Verse 18). A daughter
called Sudarśanā was born to him by his wife Narmadā.
Duryodhana married Sudarśanā to Agnideva.
