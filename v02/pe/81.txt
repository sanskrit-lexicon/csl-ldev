AGNIṢṬU¦. (AGNIṢṬOMA).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in this order:—
<div n="lb"/>Viṣṇu-Svāyambhuvamanu-Uttānapāda-Dhruva-Sṛṣṭi-
<div n="lb"/>Ripu-Cākṣuṣamanu-Agniṣṭu.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Ten good sons were born to Manu by his wife,
<div n="lb"/>Nadvalā and Agniṣṭu was one of them. The other nine
<div n="lb"/>sons were: Ūru, Pūru, Śatadyumna, Tapasvī, Satyavāk,
<div n="lb"/>Kavi, Atirātra, Sudyumna and Atimanyu. (Agni
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa, Chapter 18).
