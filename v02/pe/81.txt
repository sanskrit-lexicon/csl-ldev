AGNIṢṬU¦. (AGNIṢṬOMA).
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descended from Viṣṇu in this order:—
Viṣṇu-Svāyambhuvamanu-Uttānapāda-Dhruva-Sṛṣṭi-
Ripu-Cākṣuṣamanu-Agniṣṭu.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth.%} Ten good sons were born to Manu by his wife,
Nadvalā and Agniṣṭu was one of them. The other nine
sons were: Ūru, Pūru, Śatadyumna, Tapasvī, Satyavāk,
Kavi, Atirātra, Sudyumna and Atimanyu. (Agni
Purāṇa, Chapter 18).
