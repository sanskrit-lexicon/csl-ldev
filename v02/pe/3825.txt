KUMUDA VIII¦. There are four mountains supporting
Mahāmeru on its four sides, and Kumuda is one of those
four mountains, Mandara, Merumandara and Supārśva
being the other three. According to the 8th Skandha
of the Devī Bhāgavata there are other twenty mountains
on the four sides of Mahāmeru, viz. Kuraṅga, Kuraga,
Kuśumbha, Vikaṅkata, Trikūṭa, Śiśira, Pataṅga,
Rucaka, Nīla, Niṣadha, Śitivāsa, Kapila, Śaṅkha,
Vaidūrya, Cārudhi, Haṃsa, Ṛṣabha, Nāga, Kālañjara
and Nārada.
