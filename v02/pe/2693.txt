HIRAṆYANĀBHA¦. II. A son of Sṛñjaya. A child
named Suvarṇaṣṭhīvī was born to Sṛñjaya, but it died
before long, and then it was Nārada who brought it
[Page315-a+ 65]
back to life again. After its rebirth the child came to
be called Hiraṇyanābha also. Hiraṇyanābha lived for
1000 years. (Śānti Parva, Chapter 149).
