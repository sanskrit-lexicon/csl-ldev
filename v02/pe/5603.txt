RAIVATAKA I¦. A mountain in Gujarat. It stands near
<div n="lb"/>the present Junagaḍh. The present name of Raivataka
<div n="lb"/>mountain is Girnar. In Mahābhārata it is spoken of as
<div n="lb"/>Ujjayantagiri. It is stated in Mahābhārata that while
<div n="lb"/>Śrī Kṛṣṇa and Arjuna were travelling through Prabhāsa-
<div n="lb"/>kṣetra once, they got to the top of this mountain.
<div n="lb"/>(Ādi Parva, Chapter 217, Verse 8).

<div n="NI"/>The Yādavas once celebrated a great festival on the
<div n="lb"/>Raivataka mountain. It was during this festival that
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna carried away Subhadrā, the sister of Śrī Kṛṣṇa.
<div n="lb"/>(M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 219; Bhāgavata, Skandha
