BHAYA¦(M). Hiṃsā is the wife of Adharma. They got
<div n="lb"/>a son named Anṛta and a daughter named Nikṛti. From
<div n="lb"/>them were born Bhaya, Naraka, Māyā and Vedanā.
<div n="lb"/>Of these Māyā produced Mṛtyu, destroyer of matter.
<div n="lb"/>Vedanā got of her husband Raurava son named Duḥkha.
<div n="lb"/>From Mṛtyu were born Vyādhi, Jarā, Śoka, Tṛṣṇā
<div n="lb"/>and Krodha. (Chapter 20, Agni Purāṇa). Another
<div n="lb"/>version about the birth of Bhaya is found in Śloka 54,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 66 of Ādi Parva, M.B. Adharma married Nirṛtī
<div n="lb"/>and to them were born Bhaya, Mahābhaya and Mṛtyu,
<div n="lb"/>three sons of demoniac disposition. These three sons
<div n="lb"/>led a sinful life.
