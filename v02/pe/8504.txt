VIṢṆUPADATĪRTHA¦. A holy place. It is stated in
Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 83, that by bath-
ing in this place and worshipping Vāmana, one could
attain the world of Viṣṇu. This place is on the bank
of the river Vipāśā. It is mentioned in Droṇa Parva,
Chapter 8, that Arjuna, in his dream, had travelled with
Śrī Kṛṣṇa to the world of Śiva and had, on the way,
visited this holy place.
