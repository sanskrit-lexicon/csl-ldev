VIṢṆUPADATĪRTHA¦. A holy place. It is stated in
<div n="lb"/>Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 83, that by bath-
<div n="lb"/>ing in this place and worshipping Vāmana, one could
<div n="lb"/>attain the world of Viṣṇu. This place is on the bank
<div n="lb"/>of the river Vipāśā. It is mentioned in Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 8, that Arjuna, in his dream, had travelled with
<div n="lb"/>Śrī Kṛṣṇa to the world of Śiva and had, on the way,
<div n="lb"/>visited this holy place.
