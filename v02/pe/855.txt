BAHULĀ III¦. Wife of Vidura a Brahmin, who frequented
the houses of harlots. Bahulā was in the habit of going
to the temple of Gokarṇa and hear Purāṇas, after the
death of her husband. By this good deed Vidura got
deliverance from sin. (Skanda Purāṇa 3.3.22).
