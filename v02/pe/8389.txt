VIPṚTHU¦. A Kṣatriya of Vṛṣṇi dynasty. The following
information is available about him in Mahābhārata.
<div n="NI"/>(i) Vipṛthu was present at the Svayaṃvara marriage
of Draupadī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 185, Stanza
18).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He participated in the festival conducted on the
mountain Raivata. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 218,
Stanza 10).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Vipṛthu was a member of the group of Yādavas
who took the dowry to Hastināpura in the marriage
between Arjuna and Subhadrā. (M.B. Ādi Parva,
Chapter 220, Stanza 32).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Vipṛthu stayed in the Palace of Yudhiṣṭhira.
(M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 4, Stanza 30).
