NAGNAJIT I¦. A Kṣatriya King. It is mentioned in
Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 37, Stanza 21 that
this King was born from a portion of an asura called
Iṣupāda. This Nagnajit who was the King of Gān-
dhāra was killed by Karṇa and his sons were defeated
by Śrī Kṛṣṇa. (Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter
254 and Udyoga Parva, Chapter 43).
