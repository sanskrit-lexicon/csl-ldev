<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A Yakṣa. He was a deity of
<div n="lb"/>merchants and travellers. (Śloka 130, Chapter 64, Vana
<div n="lb"/>Parva).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}

<div n="NI"/>(ii) Once this Yakṣa on the request of a cloud named
<div n="lb"/>Kuṇḍadhāra gave a boon to a brahmin. (Śloka 21,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 171, Śanti Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) The sage Aṣṭāvakra once welcomed this Yakṣa.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 33, Chapter 19, Anuśāsana Parva).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) When Arjuna went to Marutta to bring his wealth
<div n="lb"/>Arjuna worshipped this Yakṣa and the Yakṣa blessed
<div n="lb"/>him. (Śloka 7, Chapter 65, Aśvamedha Parva).
