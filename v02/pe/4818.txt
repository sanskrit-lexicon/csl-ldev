NĪLAGIRI¦. Name of a mountain in the region called
Ilāvṛta of Jambū island. In Ilāvṛta there are three
chief mountains called Nīlagiri, Śvetagiri and Śṛṅgavān,
and in their middle three Varṣas (regions) called
Ramyaka, Hiraṇmaya and Kuru. (Devī Bhāgavata,
8th Skandha).
