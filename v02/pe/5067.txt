PARṆAYA¦. A demon of Ṛgvedic times. He had two
friends named Karañjaya and Vaṅgṛda. They attacked
the two Kings belonging to the group of Āryas named
Atithiśvā and Ṛjiśvā. The asuras were defeated
because of the help sent to the kings by Indra. (Sūkta 54,
Anuvāka 10, Maṇḍala 1, Ṛgveda).
