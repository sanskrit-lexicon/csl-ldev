DURMARṢAṆA I¦. One of the 100 sons of Dhṛtarāṣṭra.
In the great war Arjuna destroyed his Gajasenā and
Sātyaki wounded him. Finally he was killed by Bhīma.
After the war was over Nakula occupied his palace.
(M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 113, Droṇa Parva,
88, 89; 116 and 135).
