VṚṢAṄKU¦. An ancient hermit. When Śrī Rāma return-
ed from his forest life, the hermits such as Vṛṣaṅku,
Kaviṣa, Dhaumya, Raudreya, Nārada, Vāmadeva,
Saurabhi, Aṣṭāvakra, Śrīśuka, Bhṛgu, Lomaśa and
Maudgala, from the west came and bowed before him.
(Uttara Rāmāyaṇa).
