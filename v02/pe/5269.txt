PRAHASTA¦. A minister of Rāvaṇa.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descending in order from Viṣṇu—
<div n="lb"/>Brahmā—Heti—Vidyutkeśa—Sukeśa—Sumālī—
<div n="lb"/>Prahasta.
<div n="NI"/>The demon Sukesa got of his wife Devavatī three sons
<div n="lb"/>named Mālyavān, Sumālī and Mālī. Of these Sumālī
<div n="lb"/>got of his wife Ketumatī ten sons named Prahasta,
<div n="lb"/>Akampana, Vikaṭa, Kālakāmukha, Dhūmrākṣa,
<div n="lb"/>Daṇḍa, Supārśva, Saṃhrāda, Prakvāta and Bhāsakarṇa
<div n="lb"/>and four daughters named Vekā, Puṣpotkaṭā, Kaikasi
<div n="lb"/>and Kumbhīnasī. Most of the sons were the ministers
<div n="lb"/>of Rāvaṇa.
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Prahasta was the chief minister of Rāvaṇa. (Uttara
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) In the Rāma-Rāvaṇa battle Prahasta fought
<div n="lb"/>against Vibhīṣaṇa and was killed by the latter. (Śloka
<div n="lb"/>4, Chapter 286, Vana Parva).
