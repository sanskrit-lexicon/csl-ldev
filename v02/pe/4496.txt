MEGHAHĀSA¦. A son of Rāhu. When he heard that
<div n="lb"/>Mahāviṣṇu had cut off the head of his father he went
<div n="lb"/>to the banks of the river Gautamī and practised severe
<div n="lb"/>penance. As a result of that Rāhu, his father, got a
<div n="lb"/>place among the other planets in the sky. (Chapter 142,
<div n="lb"/>Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa).
