SAṂHLĀDA¦ (SAṂHRĀDA) I. A son of Hiraṇyakaśipu.
<div n="lb"/>Two sons called Hiraṇyākṣa and Hiraṇyakaśipu and a
<div n="lb"/>daughter called Siṃhikā were born to Kaśyapaprajāpati
<div n="lb"/>by his wife called Diti, and Hiraṇyakaśipu had four sons
<div n="lb"/>called Anuhrāda, Hrāda, Prahlāda and Saṃhrāda.
<div n="lb"/>Saṃhrāda had three sons named Āyuṣmān. Śibi, and
<div n="lb"/>Bāṣkala. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 19). According to
<div n="lb"/>verse 17, Chapter 65 of Ādi Parva, Prahlāda, Saṃhlāda,
<div n="lb"/>Anuhlāda, Śibi and Bāṣkala were the sons of Hiraṇya-
<div n="lb"/>kaśipu. This Saṃhlāda, in his next life, was born as
<div n="lb"/>Śalya, the Bālhīka king.
