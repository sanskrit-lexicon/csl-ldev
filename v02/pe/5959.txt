ŚĀKAṬĀYANA¦. A famous grammarian. He lived be-
fore Yāska and Pāṇini. He is considered to be the
author of the well-known text on grammar called
‘Uṇādisūtrapāṭha’. He is referred to as the foremost
among the grammarians in the aṣṭādhyāyī of Pāṇini.
(Pāṇinisūtra, 1, 4, 86 and 87).
