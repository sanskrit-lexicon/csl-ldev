MANDĀKINĪ II¦. A river which flows near the moun-
tain of Citrakūṭa. If one bathes in this river one will
have to one's credit the benefit of performing one Aśva-
medha yajña. If one lives there bathing in that river
daily, one will become possessed of Rājalakṣmī (wealth
and majesty of a King). (Śloka 29, Chapter 25,
Anuśāsana Parva).
