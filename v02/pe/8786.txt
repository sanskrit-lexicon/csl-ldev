YUGANDHARA¦ I (YUGANDHARAS). In the Purā-
ṇas there are references to a mountain called Yugan-
dhara. The inhabitants of that place were known as
Yugandharas. In Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter
129, Verse 9, there is a statement that these people
used to drink the milk of camels and donkeys.
