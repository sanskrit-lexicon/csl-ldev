DALA¦. The son of King Parīkṣit of the dynasty of Ikṣvāku.
<div n="lb"/>The mother of Dala was Suśobhanā, the daughter of the
<div n="lb"/>King of Maṇḍūka. Dala had an elder brother called
<div n="lb"/>Śala. Dala became king when Śala was killed. Hermit
<div n="lb"/>Vāmadeva was the priest of this King. (M.B. Vana
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 192). See Parīkṣit II.
