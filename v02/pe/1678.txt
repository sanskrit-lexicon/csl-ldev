DALA¦. The son of King Parīkṣit of the dynasty of Ikṣvāku.
The mother of Dala was Suśobhanā, the daughter of the
King of Maṇḍūka. Dala had an elder brother called
Śala. Dala became king when Śala was killed. Hermit
Vāmadeva was the priest of this King. (M.B. Vana
Parva, Chapter 192). See Parīkṣit II.
