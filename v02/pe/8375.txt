VINDA II¦. A prince of Avantī. It is stated that this
Vinda had a brother called Anuvinda. The inform-
ation obtained about Vinda from Mahābhārata is
given below:
<div n="NI"/>(i) Sahadeva defeated this Vinda at the time of his
southern regional conquest. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chap-
ter 31, Stanza 10).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Vinda helped Duryodhana by fighting on his side
with an akṣauhiṇī of army. (M.B. Udyoga Parva,
Chapter 19, Stanza 24).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Bhīṣma once said that Vinda was a noble warrior.
(M.B. Udyoga Parva, Chapter 166, Stanza 6).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Vinda was one of the ten commanders of Duryo-
dhana in the battle of Bhārata. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva,
Chapter 16, Stanza 15).
<div n="NI"/>(v) On the first day of the battle of Bhārata, Vinda
fought with Kuntibhoja. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter
45, Stanza 72).
<div n="NI"/>(vi) When Śveta, the prince of Virāṭa, surrounded
Śalya, the King of Madra, Vinda helped Śalya. (M.B.
Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 47, Stanza 48).
<div n="NI"/>(vii) Once Vinda and his brother Anuvinda together
attacked Irāvān. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 81,
Stanza 27).
<div n="NI"/>(viii) In the Bhārata-battle, Vinda fought with Bhīma-
sena, Arjuna and Virāṭa. Vinda was killed in the fight
with Arjuna. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 99, Stanza
17).
