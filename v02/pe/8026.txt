VARASTRĪ¦. A sister of Bṛhaspati. The Vasu named
Prabhāsa married Varastrī. This woman who was an
expounder of the Vedas, pervaded all the creations by
the power of harmony with the Supreme Spirit. (M.B.
Ādi Parva, Chapter 60, Stanza 96: Vāyu Purāṇa, 84,
15).
