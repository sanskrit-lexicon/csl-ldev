IKṢUMATĪ¦. A river. It flows near Kurukṣetra. The
<div n="lb"/>nāgas, Takṣaka and Aśvasena lived in this river. (M.B.
<div n="lb"/>Ādi Parva, Chapter 3, Verse 138).
<div n="NI"/>Kuśadhvaja, brother of the King of Mithilā used to
<div n="lb"/>live in the Ikṣumatī river valley, (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
<div n="lb"/>Bāla Kāṇḍa. 7th Sarga, Verse 2).
<div n="NI"/>There is a reference to this river in Kathāsaritsāgara,
<div n="lb"/>Madanamañcukālaṃbaka, 2nd taraṅga also.
