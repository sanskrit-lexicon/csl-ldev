NĀRĀYAṆAVARMAMANTRA¦. Imparted by Viśva-
rūpa, son of Tvaṣṭā, to Indra, this mantra is competent
to destroy enemies. He who desires to chant this mantra
should do so silently after having first washed his hands
and feet and holding Kuśa grass in his hands with face
[Page533-b+ 64]
turned towards the north. The mantra is to be chanted
when some danger is imminent.
