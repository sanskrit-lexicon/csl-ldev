VĀYUCAKRA¦. A hermit. It is mentioned in Mahā-
bhārata, Śalya Parva, Chapter 38, Stanza 32, that this
Vāyucakra was born from the semen kept in a pot by
the hermit called Maṅkaṇaka. From this same pot some
other hermits such as Vāyubala, Vāyujvāla and others
were born. (See under Maṅkaṇaka, Para 3).
