TĀMRĀ¦. Daughter of Dakṣa. Tamrā was married to
<div n="lb"/>Kaśyapa and he begot of her five daughters named
<div n="lb"/>Krauñcī, Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī. Of these
<div n="lb"/>Krauñcī delivered owls, Bhāsī delivered Bhāsas, Śyenī,
<div n="lb"/>Kites and Vultures, Dhrtarāṣṭrī, swans, geese and
<div n="lb"/>cuckoos, and Śukī delivered Natā and Natā in turn
<div n="lb"/>Vinatā. (Sarga 14, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
