TĀMRĀ¦. Daughter of Dakṣa. Tamrā was married to
Kaśyapa and he begot of her five daughters named
Krauñcī, Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī. Of these
Krauñcī delivered owls, Bhāsī delivered Bhāsas, Śyenī,
Kites and Vultures, Dhrtarāṣṭrī, swans, geese and
cuckoos, and Śukī delivered Natā and Natā in turn
Vinatā. (Sarga 14, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa).
