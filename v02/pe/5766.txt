ROHIṆĪ I¦. The mother of all the cows. The following
<div n="lb"/>is a story about the origin of Rohiṇī, given in Vālmīki
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Sarga 14.
<div n="NI"/>Prajāpati Kaśyapa married Surabhi the seventh daugh-
<div n="lb"/>ter of Dakṣa. Two daughters Rohiṇī and Gandharvī,
<div n="lb"/>were born to Surabhi. In later years cows and oxen
<div n="lb"/>were born in the world from Rohiṇī and horses from
<div n="lb"/>Gandharvī. In the Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 66, it is mentioned that two daughters Vimalā
<div n="lb"/>and Analā were born to Rohiṇī and that from these
<div n="lb"/>two, in later years cattle were born.
