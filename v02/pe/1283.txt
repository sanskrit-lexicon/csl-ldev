BRAHMĀVARTTA¦. A holy place situated in Kuruk-
ṣetra. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva,
Chapter 83, Stanza 53, that he who takes a bath in
this holy place will go to the world of Brahmā. Manu-
smṛti, Chapter 2, Stanza 17, refers to this Brahmāvartta.
Manu says as follows:
<Poem>“Sarasvatīdṛṣadvatyor-
devanadyoryadantaram /
Taddevanirmitaṃ deśaṃ
Brahmāvarttaṃ pracakṣate. //</Poem>
<div n="NI"/>“The place between the two rivers Sarasvatī and Dṛṣad-
vatī is called Brahmāvartta, which was made by the
devas.”
