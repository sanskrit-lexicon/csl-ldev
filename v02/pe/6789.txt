ŚRUTĀVATĪ¦. Daughter of Bharadvāja Muni. Once
Bharadvāja happened to see the Apsarā woman Ghṛtācī
and he had an involuntary emission of semen. Śrutāvatī
was born from that semen. She observed an austere
tapas with the object of getting Indra as her husband.
(For more information see Para 30 under Indra).
