BALI III¦. An incarnation of Śiva. Śiva incarnated in
the hermitage of the Bālakhilyas in the mount of
Gandhamādana during the period of Varāha Kalpa
(Kalpa—one day of Brahmā or the period of 14
manus). It is seen in Śiva Purāṇa, Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa
that Bali had four sons, called Sudhāmā, Kaśyapa,
Vasiṣṭha and Virajas.
