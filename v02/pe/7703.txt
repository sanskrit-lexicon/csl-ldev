UJJĀNAKA¦. The Āśrama of Ārṣṭiṣeṇamaharṣi was situated
near Mount Gandhamādana in front of Mānasa lake.
Ujjānaka was a lake near the āśrama. A dip in the
waters of the lake, it was believed, would wash off all
sins. (M.B. Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 25, Verse 35).
