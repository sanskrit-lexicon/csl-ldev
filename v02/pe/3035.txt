JYOTIṢMĀN¦. An ancient King of Kuśadvīpa. This
King had seven sons, called Udbhida, Veṇumān,
Vairatha, Lambana, Dhṛti, Prabhākara and Kapila.
Each division of that country is given the name of each
of these sons. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 2, Chapter 4).
