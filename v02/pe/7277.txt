SŪRYAVARMĀ¦. A King of the country of Trigarta.
This King had a brother called Ketuvarmā. Sūrya-
varmā and Ketuvarmā fought with Arjuna when he
was leading the sacrificial horse of Yudhiṣṭhira. Both
the Trigartas were killed in that fight. (M.B. Aśva-
medha Parva, Chapter 74).
