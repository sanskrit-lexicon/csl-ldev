NAVAKANYAKĀ¦(S). An important item of Navarātri-
pūjā (A festival of 9 days, as observed by Śakti wor-
shippers) is the worship of virgins. The nine kinds of
virgins (from two to ten years in age) thus wor-
shipped are called Navakanyakās (nine virgins). (See
undeṛ Kumārīpūjā).
