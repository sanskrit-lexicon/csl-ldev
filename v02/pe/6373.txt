ŚATADYUMNA¦. A King in ancient India, son of
Cākṣuṣa Manu by his wife Naḍvalā. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Part 1, Chapter 13). Naḍvalā had ten brilliant sons
including Śatadyumna. Śatadyumna once gifted away
a house of gold to the brahmin called Mudgala and
thereby attained heaven. (Śānti Parva, Chapter 234,
Verse 32).
