DUṢYANTA II¦. A son born to King Ajamīḍha of the Pūru
dynasty by his wife called Nīlī. He had a brother called
Parameṣṭhī. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 94, Verse 33). Both
the Kings, Duṣyanta and Parameṣṭhī are referred to as
Pāñcālas also. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 94, Verse 33).
