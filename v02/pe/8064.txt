VĀRUṆĪ II¦. The daughter of Varuṇa. When the
Devas and the Asuras churned the sea of Milk, four
damsels were caused to arise by Varuṇa and holy pot
of Ambrosia, by Soma. The four damsels were Sulakṣmī,
Vāruṇī, Kāmodā and Śreṣṭhā, of whom Vāruṇī was
married by Devas. (Padma Purāṇa, Bhūmi Khaṇḍa,
Chapter 119).
