SATYA II¦. An Agni (fire). This Agni Satya was the son
of the Agni Niścyavana. He was a worker of Kāla-
dharma (The god of Death). He reduces the pain of
living beings who are suffering. So this Agni Satya
got the name Niṣkṛti also. This Agni brightens the
[Page704-a+ 65]
houses and gardens where people stay. It is stated in
Mahābhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 219, Verse 13,
that this Agni had a son named Svana.
