ŚOṆĀ¦ NADĪ. A river famous in the Purāṇas. Some
important facts about this river are given below:-
<div n="NI"/>(i) River Śoṇā remains in Varuṇa's assembly wor-
shipping him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 9, Verse
21).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) Lord Śrī Kṛṣṇa crossed this river on his way from
Indraprastha to Rājagṛha. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chap-
ter 20, Verse 27).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) There is a sacred place called “Vaṃśagulma
tīrtha” in river Śoṇā. By bathing in it one gets the
benefit of Aśvamedha Yāga. (M.B. Vana Parva, Chap-
ter 85, Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) River Śoṇā is regarded as the source of Agni.
(M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 222, Verse 25).
<div n="NI"/>(v) Viśvāmitra, Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa took rest for a
night on the banks of river Śoṇā. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
Bāla Kāṇḍa, 31st Sarga).
