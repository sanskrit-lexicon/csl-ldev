JHILLĪ I¦. ({%Jhillīpiṇḍāraka%}). A warrior of the Vṛṣṇis.
It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 185,
Stanza 20, that this warrior was present at the svayaṃ-
vara (marriage) of Draupadī. It is also seen that this
Yādava carried the dowry of Subhadrā to Khāṇḍava-
prastha from Dvārakā. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 120,
Stanza 32). This warrior took part in the Bhārata
battle. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva,
Chapter 11, Stanza 28 that this warrior played a laud-
able part in the battle of Kurukṣetra.
