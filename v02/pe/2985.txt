JHILLĪ I¦. ({%Jhillīpiṇḍāraka%}). A warrior of the Vṛṣṇis.
<div n="lb"/>It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Chapter 185,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 20, that this warrior was present at the svayaṃ-
<div n="lb"/>vara (marriage) of Draupadī. It is also seen that this
<div n="lb"/>Yādava carried the dowry of Subhadrā to Khāṇḍava-
<div n="lb"/>prastha from Dvārakā. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 120,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 32). This warrior took part in the Bhārata
<div n="lb"/>battle. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 11, Stanza 28 that this warrior played a laud-
<div n="lb"/>able part in the battle of Kurukṣetra.
