DHṚTARĀṢṬRA III¦. A deva gandharva,. (Semi-god).
{%Some information.%} (1) This deva gandharva was the son
of the hermit Kaśyapa by his wife Muni. (M.B. Ādi
Parva, Chapter 65, Stanza 15).
<div n="NI"/>(2) He took part in the birth-celebration of Arjuna.
(M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 122, Stanza 55).
<div n="NI"/>(3) He went to the presence of King Marutta as a
messenger of Indra. (M.B. Aśvamedha Parva, Chapter
107, Stanza 2).
<div n="NI"/>(4) It was this Gandharva who had taken birth as
Dhṛtarāṣṭra, the father of Duryodhana. (M.B. Svar-
gārohaṇa Parva, Chapter 4, Stanza 15).
