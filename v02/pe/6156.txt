ŚĀṆḌILYA IV¦. A maharṣi, who desired to worship
Viṣṇu not in the Vedic method for which purpose he
even wrote a book to propagate non-vedic principles.
For the above sinful action he had to live in hell
and at last he was born as Jamadagni of the Bhṛgu
dynasty. (Vṛddhahārītasmṛti, 180, 193).
