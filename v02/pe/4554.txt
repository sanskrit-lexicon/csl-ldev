MITRAVINDĀ¦. Daughter of Śrī Kṛṣṇa's father's sister.
<div n="lb"/>Mitravindā's mother's name was Rājādhidevī. Rājādhi-
<div n="lb"/>devī, queen of Avantī, got three children named Vinda,
<div n="lb"/>Anuvinda and Mitravindā. In the Svayaṃvara Mitra-
<div n="lb"/>vindā chose Kṛṣṇa as her husband. Vinda and
<div n="lb"/>Anuvinda did not like it and joining the Kaurava side
<div n="lb"/>fought against Kṛṣṇa. But Kṛṣṇa defeated them all and
<div n="lb"/>took Mitravindā to Dvārakā. (10th Skandha, Bhāga-
<div n="lb"/>vata).
