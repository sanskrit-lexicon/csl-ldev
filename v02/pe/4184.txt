MAHĪDĀSA¦. An ācārya (Preceptor). He is believed
<div n="lb"/>to be the author of “Aitareya Brāhmaṇa” and
<div n="lb"/>“Aitareya Āraṇyaka”.
<div n="NI"/>According to Chāndogyopaniṣad and Jaiminīya
<div n="lb"/>Upaniṣad Brāhmaṇa, Mahīdāsa lived for 117 years.
<div n="lb"/>Although this ācārya was a victim of diseases he was
<div n="lb"/>quite indifferent to the painful sufferings caused by
<div n="lb"/>them. (Chāndogyopaniṣad 3; 16; 7—Jaiminīya
<div n="lb"/>Upaniṣad Brāhmaṇa, 4; 2; 11).
