VIPRACITTI¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General information.%} A Dānava (asura) born to
<div n="lb"/>Prajāpati Kaśyapa, by his wife Danū. Danū had given
<div n="lb"/>birth to thirtythree notorious asuras (demons). Vipra-
<div n="lb"/>citti was the eldest of them. His younger brothers
<div n="lb"/>were Śambara etc. (M.B. Ādi Parva Chapter 65,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 25).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other details.%} (i) Siṃhikā was the wife of Vipraoitti.
<div n="lb"/>This Siṃhikā was the sister of Hiraṇyakaśipu and
[Page858-a+ 65]
<div n="lb"/>Hiraṇyākṣa and the daughter of Kaśyapa and Diti.
<div n="lb"/>(Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Aṃśa 1, Chapter 15).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) It was this Vipracitti who took rebirth later as
<div n="lb"/>Jarāsandha. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Stanza 4).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Vipracitti stays in the palace of Varuṇa praising
<div n="lb"/>and glorifying him. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 9,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 12).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) When Mahāviṣṇu took the form of Vāmana and
<div n="lb"/>began to measure the three worlds, Vipracitti with
<div n="lb"/>some asuras surrounded Vāmana. (M.B. Sabhā Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Dākṣiṇātya pāṭha, Chapter 38).
<div n="NI"/>(v) In Mahābhārata, Śalya Parva, Chapter 31, Stanza
<div n="lb"/>12, a statement occurs to the effect that in days of old,
<div n="lb"/>Mahāviṣṇu took the form of Indra and killed Vipracitti.
<div n="NI"/>(vi) In the war between Devas and asuras, Vipracitti
<div n="lb"/>was killed by Indra. (M.B. Śānti Parva, Chapter 47,
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 11).
