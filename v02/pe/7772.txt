ŪRJJASVATĪ¦. A daughter born to Priyavrata, the son
of Manu Svāyambhuva, by Surūpā the daughter of
Viśvakarmā. Ūrjjasvatī had ten brothers. (Devī Bhāga-
vata, Skandha 8). Priyavrata gave Ūrjjasvatī to the
teacher-priest Śukra. Devayānī, the wife of Yayāti,
was the daughter born to Śukra by Ūrjjasvatī. (Bhāga-
vata, Skandha 5).
