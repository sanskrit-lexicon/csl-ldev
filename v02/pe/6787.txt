ŚRUTAŚRAVAS V¦. Wife of Damaghoṣa King of Cedi.
<div n="lb"/>She was the sister of Śrī Kṛṣṇa's father and mother of
<div n="lb"/>Śiśupāla. She requested Śrī Kṛṣṇa (who came to her
<div n="lb"/>place after Śiśupāla's birth) not to kill Śiśupāla. Śrī
<div n="lb"/>Kṛṣṇa agreed to forgive Śiśupāla's wrongs a hundred
<div n="lb"/>times. (For further details, see under Śiśupāla).
