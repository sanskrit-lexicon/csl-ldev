DEVĀTITHI¦. A king born in the Pūru dynasty. Akrodha
was his father and Karambhā, daughter of the king of
Kaliṅga, his mother. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 95, Verse 22).
Devātithi had wedded Maryādā, daughter of the King
of Videha. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 95, Verse 23).
