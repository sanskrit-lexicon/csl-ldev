<div n="lb"/>devas such as Brahmā and others visit this place
<div n="lb"/>monthly. Those who bathe in this place, at the time of
<div n="lb"/>the solar eclipse will obtain the fruits of conducting six
<div n="lb"/>horse-sacrifices. It is believed that giving offerings to
<div n="lb"/>the manes at this place is of great importance. (M.B.
