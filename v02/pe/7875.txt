VAIḌŪRYAPARVATA¦. A mountain which stands near
Gokarṇatīrtha (holy place) in the country of Śūrpāraka
(Kerala). Agastya once built a hermitage on this
mountain. If one bathes in the river Narmadā, after
having visited this Vaiḍūrya mountain one could attain
the holy worlds. (M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 33,
Verse 13).
