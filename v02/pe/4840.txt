NIRṚTI I¦. A Deva. The Purāṇas contain the following
information about him.
<div n="NI"/>(i) He is one of the Aṣṭadikpālas (guards of the
eight quarters). He is in charge of the south-western
corner. (See under Aṣṭadikpālas).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He is one of the Ekādaśarudras. (Ādi Parva,
Chapter 66, Verse 2).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Brahmā was his grand-father and Sthāṇu his
father. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 66, Verse 2).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) He attended the birth-day celebrations of Arjuna.
(Ādi Parva, Chapter 129, Verse 63).
<div n="NI"/>(v) In temples his idols are installed with sword in
hand and seated on an ass. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 51).
