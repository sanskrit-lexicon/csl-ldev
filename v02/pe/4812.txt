NĪLA II¦.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%General.%} A King born in the Hehaya dynasty. His
was a rebirth of the asura called Krodhavaśa. Nīla
was called Duryodhana also. Māhiṣmatī was the capital
of his kingdom. He attended the Svayaṃvara of Drau-
padī. (Ādi Parva, Chapter 67, Verse 61).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Other Information%}
<div n="NI"/>(i) Once he fought a fierce battle with Sahadeva, but
ultimately yielded to the latter at the instance of
Agnideva. (See under Agni, Para 8).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) He gave his daughter Sudarśanā in marriage to
Agnideva. (See under Agni, Para 8).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) During his triumphal tour, Karṇa defeated Nīla.
(Vana Parva, Chapter 254, Verse 15).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) In the battle of Kurukṣetra he fought on the side
of the Kauravas. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter 19, Verse 23).
<div n="NI"/>(v) He was reckoned as one of the mahārathis on the
side of Duryodhana. (Udyoga Parva, Chapter 164,
Verse 4).
[Page538-b+ 65]
<div n="NI"/>(vi) Sudarśanā was a daughter born to him of his
wife Narmadā. (Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 2).
