GULMAKA¦. The uncle of Guṇāḍhya. Guṇāḍhya is the
author of the famous book ‘Bṛhatkathā’. In the city of
Supratiṣṭhita there was a brahmin named Somaśarmā
who had three children, namely, Vatsa, Gulmaka and
Śrutārtha. Guṇāḍhya was the son of Śrutārtha (Taraṅga
6, Kathāpiṭhalaṃbaka, Kathāsaritsāgara).
