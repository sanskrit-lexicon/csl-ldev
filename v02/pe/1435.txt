CANDRAGUPTA II¦. A minister of Kārtavīryārjuna.
Kārtavīryārjuna was once fed sumptuously by Jamad-
agni at his āśrama with the help of Śuśīlā, the deva-
surabhi (divine cow). Candragupta, at the instance of
Kārtavīryārjuna tried to capture Suśīlā by force, which
disappeared immediately in the sky. Kārtavīryārjuna's
servants then forcibly took away its calf. Candragupta
thrashed to death Jamadagni, who tried to obstruct
their action. (See Kārtavīrya, Para 9).
