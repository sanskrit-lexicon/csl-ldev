CANDRAGUPTA II¦. A minister of Kārtavīryārjuna.
<div n="lb"/>Kārtavīryārjuna was once fed sumptuously by Jamad-
<div n="lb"/>agni at his āśrama with the help of Śuśīlā, the deva-
<div n="lb"/>surabhi (divine cow). Candragupta, at the instance of
<div n="lb"/>Kārtavīryārjuna tried to capture Suśīlā by force, which
<div n="lb"/>disappeared immediately in the sky. Kārtavīryārjuna's
<div n="lb"/>servants then forcibly took away its calf. Candragupta
<div n="lb"/>thrashed to death Jamadagni, who tried to obstruct
<div n="lb"/>their action. (See Kārtavīrya, Para 9).
