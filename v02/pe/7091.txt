SUMATI V¦. A King, grandson of Ṛṣabha and son of
Bharata. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa, Part II, Chapter 1). He was
a righteous ruler. After ruling the country well for
long and performing many yajñas his father Bharata
crowned Sumati king and practising meditation in
Sālagrāma temple he gave up his life. (See under
Bharata III).
