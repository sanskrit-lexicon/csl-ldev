PRAMĀTHĪ II¦. Brother of Dūṣaṇa who was an
associate of Rāvaṇa. When Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa were
in exile in the forests they had to fight Khara-
dūṣaṇatriśirases. Then the captains of their army,
Mahākapāla, Sthūlākṣa and Pramāthī attacked Rāma-
[Page601-b+ 65]
Lakṣmaṇas. Pramāthī attacked Lakṣmaṇa and then
Nīla an army-chief of the monkeys killed Pramāthī.
(Sarga 26, Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa and Śloka 22, Chapter
287, Vana Parva).
