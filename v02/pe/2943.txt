JĀYĀ¦. Wife. The husband enters the wife in the form of
semen and takes birth as the foetus and then is born
from her as son and so the wife is called Jāyā.
<Poem>“Patirbhāryāṃ sampraviśya
Garbho bhūtveha jāyate /
Jāyāyāstaddhi jāyātvaṃ
Yadasyāṃ jāyate punaḥ” //
(Manusmṛti, Chapter 9, Stanza 8).</Poem>
