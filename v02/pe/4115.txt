MAHĀKĀLA II¦. A sacred place situated in the Śiprā
river valley in Ujjain. The Śivaliṅga installed in this
place is called “Mahākāla.” Mahābhārata, Vana Parva,
Chapter 82, Verse 49 says that those who take a bath
in the Koṭitīrtha at this place, get the same effect as
that of an Aśvamedha yāga.
