JALASANDHI II¦. A warrior who fought on the side of
the Kauravas against the Pāṇḍavas. He was present at
the svayaṃvara of Pāñcālī. (marriage of Pāñcālī). It is
mentioned in Mahābhārata, Droṇa Parva, Chapter
115, Stanza 52, that he was killed by Sātyaki in the
battle of Bhārata.
