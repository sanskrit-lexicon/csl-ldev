DĀŚĀRHĪ I¦. A woman belonging to Dāśārha's family.
Vijayā, the wife of Drumanyu, Sudevā, the wife of
Vikaṇṭhana, Śubhāṃgī, the wife of Kuru, Kuntī, the
wife of Pāṇḍu—all these Yādava women are entitled
to the name Dāśārhī. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter
38).
