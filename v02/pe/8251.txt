VIDEHA¦ II The kingdom of Mithilā. This country
which lies on the North East part of India was ruled
by Kṣatriya kings of the dynasty of Videha. It is stat-
ed in Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva, Chapter 29, that
Bhīmasena had during his eastern regional conquest,
conquered this country. About this kingdom which is
the native country of Sītā, the following information
is available in Mahābhārata.
[Page847-b+ 64]
<div n="NI"/>(i) The hermitage of Paraśurāma was on the North
of Videha. (M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 130, Stanza
13).
<div n="NI"/>(ii) The army of Videha attacked Arjuna in the
battle of Bhārata. (M.B. Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 117,
Stanza 32).
<div n="NI"/>(iii) Karṇa defeated the Kṣatriya princes of the country
of Janaka. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 4, Stanza 6).
<div n="NI"/>(iv) Paraśurāma cut the Kṣatriyas of this country
into pieces with his sharp arrows. (M.B. Droṇa Parva,
Chapter 70, Stanza 11).
<div n="NI"/>(v) This country had been giving tribute to Karṇa.
(M.B. Karṇa Parva, Chapter 9, Stanza 33).
