MITRAVĀN II¦. An ascetic who lived in the city of
Saupura. He acquired complete peace of mind by
reading the second Chapter of the Gītā sitting in a Śiva
temple. Once a brahmin of name Devaśarmā approach-
ed a sage named Muktakarmā seeking advice to obtain
peace of mind. The latter directed Devaśarmā to Mitra-
vān and Mitravān advised him to read the second Chap-
ter of the Gītā. (Padma Purāṇa, Uttara Khaṇḍa, 176).
