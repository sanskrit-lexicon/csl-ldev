DHARMĀRAṆYA¦(M). A forest which is a holy place.
It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva Chapter 82
Stanza 46, that the moment one enters this forest one
would become sinless. This place was once the capital
of the King named Asūtarajasa. (See under Kuśa
I).
