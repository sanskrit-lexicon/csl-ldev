MADANAMAÑCUKĀ¦. A heroine of ‘Vatsarājacarita’.
<div n="lb"/>Her mother was a nymph in her previous birth. By a
<div n="lb"/>curse of Devendra she was born on earth as Kaliṅgasenā
<div n="lb"/>and lived serving in the palace of Udayana, King of
<div n="lb"/>Vatsa. She desired to make Udayana her husband. In
<div n="lb"/>the meantime a Gandharva named Madanavega fell in
<div n="lb"/>love with Kaliṅgasenā. Knowing that Kaliṅgasenā was
<div n="lb"/>in love with Udayana the Gandharva followed her in
<div n="lb"/>the garb of the King Udayana and at last they were
<div n="lb"/>married. Kaliṅgasenā soon delivered Madanamañ-
<div n="lb"/>cukā, an incarnation of Ratidevi. When she grew up
<div n="lb"/>she married Naravāhanadatta, son of Udayana.
<div n="lb"/>(Madanamañcukālambaka, Kathāsaritsāgara, Taraṅga
