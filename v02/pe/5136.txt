PAUṢAMĀSA¦. The month of Pauṣa (January). During
this month, on the full moon day the constellation
Puṣya and the moon join in a zodiac. He who takes
food only once a day during this month will get beauty,
fame and prosperity. (Chapter 106, Anuśāsana Parva).
