ŚĀṆḌILYA II¦. A maharṣi born in the dynasty of
Kaśyapa, son of Marīci. As Agni was born in the
family of the maharṣi it came to be called ‘Śāṇḍilya-
gotrīya’ (born in the family of Śāṇḍilya). King
Sumanyu once gave him food and other edible things.
(Anuśāsana Parva, Chapter 137, Verse 22).
