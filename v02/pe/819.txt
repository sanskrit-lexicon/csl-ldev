ĀYUS I¦. Son of Purūravas and father of Nahuṣa.
<div n="NI"/>1) {%Genealogy.%} Descending in order from Viṣṇu Brahmā-
Candra-Budha-Purūravas-Āyus.
<div n="NI"/>Āyus was born to Purūravas of Urvaśī. To Āyus was
born of his wife Svarbhānavī Nahuṣa. (Śloka 24, Chapter
7, Ādi Parva, M.B.).
<div n="NI"/>2) {%Birth of the son.%} See under Nahuṣa.
<div n="NI"/>3) {%Other details.%} Āyus was a king who had acquired
great power by penance. (Śloka 15, Chapter 296, Śānti
Parva, M.B.).
