SATYEYU¦. A King born in the dynasty of King Pūru.
This Satyeyu was one of the ten sons born to Raudrā-
śva by the celestial maid Ghṛtācī. (Bhāgavata, Skandha
9).
<div n="NI"/>According to the statement that occurs in Mahābhārata,
Ādi Parva, Chapter 94, the father of Satyeyu was
[Page710-a+ 64]
Raudrāśva and mother, Miśrakeśī. The son Satyeyu
was born to Raudrāśva by the celestial maid Miśrakeśī.
