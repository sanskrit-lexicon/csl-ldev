LAKṢAṆĀ¦ (LAKṢMAṆĀ) III. One of the eight queens
of Śrī Kṛṣṇa. Lakṣaṇā was the daughter of Bṛhatsena,
King of Madra. (Sṛṣṭi Khaṇḍa, Padma Purāṇa). Śrī
Kṛṣṇa got ten sons of her some of whom are Praghoṣa,
Gātravān, Siṃha and Bala. (10th Skandha, Bhāga-
vata).
