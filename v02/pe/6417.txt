ŚATRUGHNA¦. A brother of Śrī Rāma. He and Lakṣ-
maṇa were the sons of Sumitrā, and Śrutakīrti was his
wife. As ordered by Rāma he killed Lavaṇāsura, who
lived in Madhu forest, and established there the city
called Madhurāpurī. After the death of Śatrughna two
sons of his lived in Madhurāpurī, and after the dis-
appearance of the solar dynasty the city went to the
Yadus. (For details see under Śrī Rāma and Hanūmān,
Para 10).
