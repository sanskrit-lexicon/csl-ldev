VṚKA IV¦. A king. It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi
Parva, Chapter 185, Stanza 10, that this king had been
present at the Svayaṃvara (marriage) of Draupadī.
Mention is made in Mahābhārata, Karṇa Parva, Chap-
ter 25, Stanza 16, that this king was killed by a moun-
tain King in the battle of Bhārata.
