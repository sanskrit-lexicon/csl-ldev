AVIMUKTA¦(M). The middle part of the city of Kāśī.
<div n="lb"/>There is a holy temple here. It is said that those who
<div n="lb"/>commit suicide in this temple would attain heaven.
<div n="lb"/>(Vana Parva, Chapter 64, Stanzas 78 and 79). For
<div n="lb"/>more information see the word Divodāsa.
