SAVARṆĀ¦. Wife of the muni called Prācīnabarhis.
<div n="lb"/>Savarṇā was the daughter of Samudra (ocean). (Viṣṇu
<div n="lb"/>Purāṇa, Part 1, Chapter 14). The couple had ten
<div n="lb"/>sons called the Pracetases, and they were very efficient
<div n="lb"/>in Dhanurveda. All of them engaged themselves in
<div n="lb"/>observing the same dharma (duty) and did tapas for
<div n="lb"/>10,000 years under the sea. (For details see under
<div n="lb"/>Prācīnabarhis).
