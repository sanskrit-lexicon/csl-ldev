BHARTṚSTHĀNA¦(M). A very holy place in ancient
India. It is said that even those who merely visited this
place would get the benefit of performing an Aśvamedha
yajña. The place got its importance because Lord
Subrahmaṇya lived there. (Śloka 76, Chapter 84, Vana
Parva, M.B.)
