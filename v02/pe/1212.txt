BHŪRIŚRAVĀ¦ (BHŪRIŚRAVAS).
<div n="NI"/>(1) {%General.%} He was the son of Somada, a king of
<div n="lb"/>Kuruvaṃśa (Kuru dynasty).
<div n="NI"/>(2) {%Other details.%} Bhūriśravas had two brothers named
<div n="lb"/>Bhūri and Śala. They were all present at the Svayaṃ-
<div n="lb"/>vara of Draupadī. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 185,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 14).
<div n="NI"/>(3) He once described to Duryodhana, the heroic quali-
<div n="lb"/>ties of the Pāṇḍavas and advised him to maintain peace-
<div n="lb"/>ful relations with them. (M.B. Ādi Parva, Chapter 199,
<div n="lb"/>Dākṣinātya Pāṭha).
<div n="NI"/>(4) He took part in Yudhiṣṭhira's Rājasūya yajña
<div n="lb"/>with his father and brothers. (M.B. Sabhā Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 34, Verse 8).
<div n="NI"/>(5) In the war between Kauravas and Pāṇḍavas, he
<div n="lb"/>arrived with an “Akṣauhiṇī” (A division of the army)
<div n="lb"/>in order to give help to Duryodhana. (M.B. Udyoga
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 19, Verse 16).
[Page144-a+ 65]
<div n="NI"/>(6) Bhīṣma once remarked that Bhūriśravas deserved
<div n="lb"/>to be counted among the Rathīs and Yūthapatis.
<div n="lb"/>(Udyoga Parva, Chapter 165, Verse 29).
<div n="NI"/>(7) On the first day of the battle at Kurukṣetra Bhūri-
<div n="lb"/>śravas fought a duel with Śaṃkha. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 45, Verse 35).
<div n="NI"/>(8) He fought with Sātyaki (Bhīṣma Parva, Chapter 63,
<div n="lb"/>Verse 33).
<div n="NI"/>(9) He killed the ten sons of Sātyaki. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 74, Verse 25).
<div n="NI"/>(10) Defeated Dhṛṣṭaketu in battle. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 84, Verse 35).
<div n="NI"/>(11) He fought a duel with Bhīmasena. (Bhīṣma Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 11, Verse 44).
<div n="NI"/>(12) A duel was fought with Śikhaṇḍī. (Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 43, Verse 45).
<div n="NI"/>(13) Bhūriśravas fell down under the stroke of Sātyaki.
<div n="lb"/>(Droṇa Parva, Chapter 142, Verse 59).
<div n="NI"/>(14) Arjuna cut off the left arm of Bhūriśravas. (Droṇa
<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 141, Verse 72).
<div n="NI"/>(15) Enraged by Arjuna's action, Bhūriśravas squatted
<div n="lb"/>on the ground to attain Svarga (Heaven). (Droṇa Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 143, Verse 33).
<div n="NI"/>(16) Sātyaki killed Bhūriśravas. (Droṇa Parva, Chapter
<div n="lb"/>143, Verse 54).
<div n="NI"/>(17) After death Bhūriśravas became a Viśvadeva.
<div n="lb"/>(Svargārohaṇa Parva, Chapter 5, Verse 16).
<div n="NI"/>(18) Some of the other names of Bhūriśravas given in
<div n="lb"/>Mahābhārata are:—Bhūridakṣiṇa, Śalāgraja, Kaurava,
<div n="lb"/>Kauraveya, Kauravya, Yūpaketana, Yūpaketu, Śārdūla,
<div n="lb"/>Kuruśreṣṭha, Kurūdvaha.
