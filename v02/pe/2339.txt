GAṄGĀHRADA¦(M). A sacred place on the limits of
Kurukṣetra. There is a terribly deep whirlpool of the
river Ganges here and that depth of the river is con-
sidered to be equivalent to three crores of sacred places.
(Vana Parva, Chapter 83, and Anuśāsana Parva,
Chapter 25).
