ŚRUTAKĪRTI I¦. Wife of Śatrughna. Rāma married
Sītā; Bharata married Māṇḍavī; Lakṣmaṇa married
Ūrmilā, and Śatrughna married Śrutakīrti. Of these
Sītā was the daughter of Janaka and the other three
were the daughters of Janaka's younger brother,
Kuśadhvaja. (Kamba Rāmāyaṇa, Bālakāṇḍa).
