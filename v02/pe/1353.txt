BṚHATĪ I¦. Mother of Cākṣuṣamanu. Two sons, Śiṣṭhi,
and Bhavya were born to Dhruva by his wife Śambhu.
Succhāyā the wife of Śiṣṭhi gave birth to five sons,
Ripu, Ripuñjaya, Vipra, Vṛkala and Vṛkatejas. These
sons were sinless. Bṛhatī was the wife of Ripu. Cākṣu-
ṣa Manu was born to Ripu and Bṛhatī. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Aṃśa 1, Chapter 13).
