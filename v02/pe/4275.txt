MĀNASADVĀRA¦. A mountain near Mānasasaras.
<div n="lb"/>Because the entrance to the lake of Mānasasaras was
<div n="lb"/>through this mountain it got the name of Mānasadvāra.
<div n="lb"/>In the centre of this was an āśrama of Paraśurāma.
<div n="lb"/>(Śloka 12, Chapter 130, Vana Parva).
