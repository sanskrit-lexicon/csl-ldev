VIŚĀLA¦. A son of Ikṣvāku. Mention is made in Vālmīki
Rāmāyaṇa, Bālakāṇḍa, Sarga 47, that a son named
Viśāla was born to Ikṣvāku, by the celestial maid
Alambuṣā and that Viśāla built a city named Viśālā.
This Viśāla had a son named Hemacandra. It was
near the city Viśālā, that Ahalyā, the wife of Gautama,
[Page863-b+ 64]
stood as stone, because of a curse. (See under Viśāla-
purī).
