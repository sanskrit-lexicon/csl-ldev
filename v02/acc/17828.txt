{#मीमांसा#}¦ consists of two kinds. The first called Pūrva-
mīmāṃsā, Karmamīmāṃsā, Dharmamīmāṃsā, Bhāṭṭa,
is based on the Jaiminisūtra. The second Uttara-
mīmāṃsā, Vedānta, rests on the authority of the
Brahmasūtra by Bādarāyaṇa. The following works
belong only to the Pūrvamīmāṃsā.
