{#पद्मनाभदत्त#}¦ the author of the Supadma Grammar,
composed the Pṛṣodarādivṛtti in 1367. In his
Paribhāṣāvṛtti (IO. No. 890) he mentions the follow-
ing works of his: 1) Supadma Grammar and Pañjikā.
2) Prayogadīpikā. 3) Uṇādivṛtti. 4) Dhātukaumudī.
5) Yaṅlugvṛtti. 6) Gopālacarita. 7) Ānandalaharī-
ṭīkā. 8) Śiśupālavadhaṭīkā. 9) Chandoratna. 10) Ācāra-
candrikā. 11) Bhūriprayoga.
