<L>36398<pc>2-121,2<k1>रामभद्रदीक्षित<k2>रामभद्र दीक्षित
{#रामभद्र दीक्षित#}¦ of the Kauṇḍinya family, born in
Kaṇḍaramāṇikkā in the Tañjore district, son of
Yajñarāma Dīkṣita, grandson of Nallādīkṣita, son-in-
law of Cokkanātha. He was guru of Raghunātha, Veṅka-
ṭeśvarakavi, Śrīnivāsadīkṣita. He lived in the last
decade of the 17^th century, under Śahaji, son of
Ekoji, kings of Tañjore:
