{#बिल्हण#}¦ son of Jyeṣṭhakalaśa, son of Rājakalaśa, son
of Muktikalaśa, brother of Iṣṭarāma and Ānanda,
lived in the middle of the eleventh century. He is
mentioned in the Rājataraṅgiṇī VII, 938:
<HI1>Karṇasundarī nāṭikā.
<HI1>Caurīsuratapañcāśikā.
<HI1>Bilhaṇacarita.
<HI1>Vikramāṅkacarita.
<HI1>Bilhaṇīya, kāvya. Oppert 6950. II, 1775.
<HI1>Verses of his are given Śp. p. 55. Sbhv. He
is mentioned by Maṅkha Report CXII.
