{#शङ्करानन्द#}¦ son of Vāñcheśa and Veṅkaṭāmbā, pupil of
Ānandātman, guru of Sāyaṇa (Oxf. 222^a):
<HI1>Ātmapurāṇa or Upaniṣadratna, the substance of
a number of Upaniṣads, in verse.
<HI1>He wrote dīpikās on the following Upaniṣads:
Atharvaśikhā, Atharvaśiras, Atharvaśīrṣa, Amṛ-
tanāda, Amṛtabindu, Āruṇī, Īśāvāsya, Aitareya, 
[Page1-629-b+ 46]
Kāṭhaka, Keneṣita, Kaivalya, Kauṣītaka, Ga-
rbha, Chāndogya, Jābāla, Taittirīya, Nārāyaṇa,
Nṛsiṃhatāpanīya, Paramahaṃsa, Praśna, Brahman,
Brahmavallī, Mahopaniṣad, Māṇḍūkya, Muṇḍaka,
Śvetāśvatara, Haṃsa.
<HI1>Bhagavadgītātātparyabodhinī.
<HI1>Yatyanuṣṭhānapaddhati.
<HI1>Śivasahasranāmaṭīkā.
<HI1>Sarvapurāṇasāra.
