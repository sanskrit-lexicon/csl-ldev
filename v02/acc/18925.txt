{#रघुनाथ तार्किकचूडामणि भट्टाचार्य#}¦ with the epithet Tā-
rkikaśiromaṇi, or usually Śiromaṇi. He was a pupil
of Vāsudeva Sārvabhauma, and father of Rāmakṛ-
ṣṇa (Guṇaśiromaṇiprakāśa). Tradition alleges that
he was a contemporary of Raghunandana, the author
of the Smṛtitattva. He is quoted in Saṃkṣepa-
śārīraka 2, 182:
