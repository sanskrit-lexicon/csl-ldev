{#चण्डेश्वर ठक्कुर#}¦ son of the minister Vīreśvara Ṭhakkura,
was a minister of Harasiṃhadeva, son of Bhaveśa,
princes of Mithilā. He quotes the Kalpadruma, Kāma-
dhenu, Pārijāta, Prakāśa, Halāyudha. The collective
title of his works on law was Smṛtiratnākara. He
himself enumerates seven Ratnākara: Kṛtya, Dāna,
Vyavahāra, Śuddhi, Pūjā, Vivāda, Gṛhastha. He is
quoted by Raghunātha, Kamālākara, by Anantadeva
in Saṃskārakaustubha, by Keśava in Dvaitapariśiṣṭa,
by Nīlakaṇṭha, and others:
