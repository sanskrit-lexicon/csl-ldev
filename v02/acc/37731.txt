in eight sthāna. These are called Unnaya, Uttara,
Śārīraka, Cikitsita, Kiśoracikitsā or Śiśubhaiṣajya,

sthāna). Stein 191 (where the fourth sthāna is
wanting). 351. Burnell's Śālihotronnaya belongs
hither.
