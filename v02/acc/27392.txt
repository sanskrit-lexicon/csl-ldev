{#सर्वज्ञ#}¦ king of Karṇāṭa, had a son Aniruddhadeva, who
was father of Rūpeśvara and Harihara. Rūpeśvara's
son Padmanābha had five sons, Puruṣottama, Ja-
gannātha, Nārāyaṇa, Murāri, Mukunda. The last of
these had one son, Kumāra, whose three sons were
Sanātana, Rūpa and Vallabha.
