{#दिवाकर भट्ट#}¦ son of Gaṅgā and Mahādeva Bhaṭṭa, son
of Bālakṛṣṇa Bhaṭṭa, son of Mahādeva Bhaṭṭa, son
of Nārāyaṇa Bhaṭṭa, who, with Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa,
was son of Mādhava Bhaṭṭa, son of Rāmakṛṣṇa
Bhaṭṭa. Divākara's mother was daughter of Nīla-
kaṇṭha Bhaṭṭa, son of Śaṅkara Bhaṭṭa, son of Nārā-
yaṇa Bhaṭṭa, son of Rāmeśvara Bhaṭṭa. He was
father of Vaidyanātha. Hall p. 175:
