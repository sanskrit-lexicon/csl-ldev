{#हेमाद्रि#}¦ son of Kāmadeva, son of Vāsudeva, son of
Vāmana, lived under king Mahādeva (1260--71) of
Devagiri, son of Caitrapāla, and under his successor
Rāmacandra (1271--1309). See Pariśeṣakhaṇḍa 1,
p. 4. He is quoted for the first time by Vopadeva,
then in Kālamādhava and Madanapārijāta:

<HI1>Kaivalyadīpikā Muktāphalaṭīkā.
