{#वृत्तिकार#}¦ and {#वृत्तिकृत्#}¦ Sāyaṇa in the Dhatuvṛtti and
Kṣīrasvāmin in the Dhātutaraṅgiṇī mean by this
name the authors of the Kāśikāvṛtti. Anantadeva

allude by this term to some well-known commentator.
