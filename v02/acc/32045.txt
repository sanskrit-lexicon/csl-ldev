{#काकोजि#}¦ or Gaṅgādharādhvarin had three sons: Nara-
siṃhādhvarin, Tryambakarāya, Bhagavantarāya. Āna-
ndarāyamakhin was the son of Narasiṃhādhvarin.

from 1676--1684). Kuppūsvāmin Śāstrin in Preface
to Patañjalicarita.
