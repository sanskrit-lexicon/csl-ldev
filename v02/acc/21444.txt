{#वररुचि#}¦ an ancient writer, to whom, as in the case of
other celebrated authors, a number of most modern
and contemptible pieces are attributed. He is quoted
in the Tribhāṣyaratna 1, 18. 2, 14. 19. 4, 40 etc.
As a grammarian he is sometimes identified with
Kātyāyana, the author of the Vārttika to Pāṇini:
