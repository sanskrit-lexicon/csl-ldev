{#प्राकृतमिच#}¦ m. ({#-त्रः#}) A natural friend or ally; as applied to a
sovereign, it means one whose states are removed by an intervening 
[Page585]
kingdom from those to which it should be friendly, the {#प्राकृतारि#} or natural
enemy is the chief of the intervening state, and the {#प्राकृतोदासीनः#} or
natural neutral is the outer one of the four.
.E. {#प्राकृत#} natural, and {#मिच#} a friend.
