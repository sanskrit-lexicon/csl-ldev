.²2 The argument of an equation.

seventh, or tenth degree. It is variously applied in combination, as
{#पतनकेन्द्रं#} argument of the latitude; {#द्वितीयकेन्द्रं#} the distance of
the higher apsis form a planet in any point of its orbit; {#शीघ्रकेन्द्रं#}
commutation, the distance of

inferior planet from the sun; {#मन्दकेन्द्रं#} argument of anomaly.
