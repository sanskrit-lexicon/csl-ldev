{%Nakṣatra;%}¦ the order of the Nakṣatras is
differently given, but the following is the most
usual: 1. Aśvinī; 2. Bharaṇī; 3. Kṛttikā; 4. Ro-
hiṇī; 5. Mṛga-śiras; 6. Ārdrā; 7. Punar-vasū;
8. Puṣya; 9. Āśleṣā; 10. Maghā; 11. Pūrva-
Phalgunī; 12. Uttara-Phalgunī; 13. Hasta; 14.
Citrā; 15. Svātī; 16. Viśākhā; 17. Anurādhā;
18. Jyeṣṭhā; 19. Mūla; 20. Pūrvāṣāḍhā; 21.
Uttarāṣāḍhā; 22. Abhijit; 23. Śravaṇa; 24. Śra-
viṣṭhā or Dhaniṣṭhā; 25. Śata-bhiṣaj; 26. Pūrva-
Bhādrapadā; 27. Uttara-Bhādrapadā; 28. Revatī:
sometimes the twenty-second, Abhijit, is omitted, a
sidereal revolution of the moon being accomplished
in little more than twenty-seven days.
