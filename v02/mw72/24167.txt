modern mythology he is a friend of Kṛṣṇa and is
regarded as inventor of the Vīṇā or lute; in epic
poetry he is occasionally called a Deva-gandharva,
‘divine Gandharva,’ or a Gandharva-rājan, ‘king of
the Gandharvas,’ or simply Gandharva); N. of a
