consideration (equivalent to humph! umph! &c.);
of interrogation (hey? indeed? eh?); of assent

a magical or mystical monosyllable, (see {%hum.%})
{%—Hūṅ-kāra, as,%} m. the word or sound {%hūm%}

sound {%hūm;%} roaring, grunting. {%—Hūṅ-kṛti, is,%} f.
