{#तामिस्र#}¦ {%tāmisra%} (fr. {%tamisra%}), scil. {%pakṣa,%}
the dark half of the month, the time from full moon
to new moon; ({%as%}), m. a Rākṣasa (as going about
in the dark); indignation at being disappointed or
slighted, anger, one of the five forms of Avidyā in
the Sāṅkhya system; a division of hell, that of
deep gloom.
