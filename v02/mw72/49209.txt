{%yati%} (ep. also A. {%-te%}), {%sandraṣṭum,%} to see together
or at the same time; to look at steadfastly, see well,
gaze at, see, behold, view, perceive, become aware

at, allow to happen; to look about (A. if without
an object, Pāṇ. 1. 3, 29, Vārtt. 2): Pass. {%-dṛśyate,%}

observed, become visible, appear: Caus. {%-darśayati,%}
{%-yitum,%} to cause to see well, make manifest, show,
display; to represent; to show one's self to any one
