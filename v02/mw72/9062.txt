four Gānas or psalm-books of the Sāma-veda. {%—Ā-%}

Mahā-bhārata, more usually called {%vana-parvan.%}
{%—Āraṇya-paśu, us,%} m. a wild or forest animal (as a

m. (in the zodiac) the sign Leo; Aries and Taurus;
