{#कालञ्जर#}¦ {%kālañjara, as,%} m., N. of a mountain
in Bundalkhand considered as sacred, the modern
Kallinjer; N. of the adjacent country; an assembly
or meeting-place of religious mendicants; Kallinjer
is one of the places at which such assemblies meet,
being enumerated amongst the Tapasyasthānas or
spots adapted to practices of austere devotion; an
epithet of Śiva; ({%ā%} or {%ī%}), f. an epithet of Durgā.
