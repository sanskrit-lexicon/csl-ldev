{#कुडव#}¦ {%kuḍava, as,%} m. a measure of grain,
wood, iron, &c., the fourth part of a Prastha, de-
scribed by some as a vessel four fingers wide and as
many deep and containing twelve Prakṛtis or hand-
fuls; it is also said to contain 13(1/2) cubic Angulas;
or to be a finger and a half deep, and three fingers

each in length and breadth; in medicine it is equal
