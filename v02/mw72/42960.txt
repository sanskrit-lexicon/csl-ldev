{#विना#}¦ {%vinā,%} ind. (probably a kind of inst.
of 1. {%vi%}), without, except (with acc., inst., or occa-
sionally abl., e. g. {%bhūṣaṇam%} or {%bhūṣaṇena%} or
{%bhūṣaṇād vinā,%} without ornament); short of, ex-
clusive of. {%—Vinā-kṛta, as, ā, am,%} ‘made-without,’
deprived of, bereaved, abandoned, left, relinquished.
{%—Vinānyonyam (°nā-an°),%} ind. without each
other, one without the other. {%—Vinokti (°nā-uk°),%}
{%is,%} f. ‘the utterance of {%vinā,%}’ i. e. the use of the
word {%vinā%} in composition (e. g. {%kā niśā śaśinā%}
{%vinā,%} what is the night without the moon?).
