{#नैषध#}¦ {%naiṣadha, as, ī, am,%} relating to
Niṣadha; ({%as%}), m. a prince of the Niṣadhas,
especially an epithet of Nala; ({%ās%}), m. pl., N. of the
people of Niṣadha; a species of plant used as food;
({%am%}), n. or {%naiṣadha-carita, am,%} n., N. of an
artificial epic poem by Śrī-harṣa, treating of Nala's
adventures. {%—Naiṣadhacarita-bhāva-dyotanikā,%}
f. ‘an explanation of the meaning of the Naiṣadha-
carita,’ N. of a commentary on the preceding poem.
{%—Naiṣadhānveṣaṇa (°dha-an°), am,%} n. the
seeking or searching for Naiṣadha, i. e. Nala.
