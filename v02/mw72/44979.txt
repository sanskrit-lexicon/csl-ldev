{#वैखरी#}¦ {%vaikharī,%} f. speech in the fourth
of its four stages from the first stirring of the air or
breath, articulate utterance, that utterance of sounds
or words which is complete as consisting of full and
intelligible sentences, (according to Malli-nātha =
{%śabda-niṣpattiḥ,%} Kumāra-sambhava II. 17); the
faculty of speech or the divinity presiding over it.
