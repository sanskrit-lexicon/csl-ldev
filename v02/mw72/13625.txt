{#कपाल#}¦ {%kapāla, am,%} n. (said to be fr. rt.
{%kamp%}), a cup, a jar, a dish, used especially for the

the first member of which is a numeral, e. g. {%tri-%}
{%kapāla,%} ‘consisting of three cups’); the shell of an
egg, shell of a tortoise; the cotyla of the leg of a
man or animal, any flat bone; a kind of leprosy;
({%as, am%}), m. n. the fragment of a vessel, a pot-
sherd; a cover or lid; the skull, the cranium, the
skull-bone; either half of a water-jar; multitude,
assemblage, collection; a treaty of peace on equal
terms, {%= kapāṭa?; (as),%} m., N. of an intermediate
caste; N. of a man; ({%am, ī%}), n. f. a beggar's bowl;
