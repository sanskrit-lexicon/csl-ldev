{#रु#} 1¦. {%ru,%} cl. 2. P. {%rauti, ravīti%} (Ved. also
{%ruvati,%} and in some forms also A.), Impf.
{%araut%} or {%aravīt%} (1st du. {%aruva%} or {%aruvīva,%} 3rd
pl. {%aruvan%}), {%rurāva%} (1st du. {%ruruviva,%} 3rd pl. A.
{%ruruvire%}), {%ravitā%} or (according to Vopa-deva IX.

particular sound or noise, bellow, roar, howl, yelp,
