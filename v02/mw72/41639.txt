{#वस्#} 6¦. {%vas,%} cl. 1. P. {%vasati%} (ep. also A.
{%vasate%}), {%uvāsa, vatsyati%} (ep. {%vasiṣyati%}),
avātsīt (ep. {%avosīt%}), {%vastum,%} to dwell, live, to
inhabit, dwell in, abide in (with loc. or even acc.,
e. g. {%vaneṣu vas,%} to dwell in woods; {%nadīr vas,%}
to live in rivers); to dwell with, live near (with loc.
or with {%saha%} governing an inst.); to spend (time),
pass (a night); cl. 10. P. {%vasayati,%} &c., to dwell:

be dwelt: Caus. {%vāsayati, -yitum,%} Aor. {%avīvasat,%}
to cause to dwell, to lodge; to people; to receive
hospitably or as a guest; to let abide; to dwell, in-
habit: Desid. {%vivatsati,%} to wish to dwell: Intens.
{%vāvasyate, vāvasti,%} to remain, be in, be engaged
