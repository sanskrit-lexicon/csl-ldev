of Muñja grass or its fibres; resembling Muñja grass;
({%as%}), m. a blade of Muñja grass; ({%ī%}), f., scil. {%me-%}
{%khalā,%} a Brāhman's girdle or cord made of a triple
string of Muñja grass; any girdle. {%—Mauñjī-tṛṇā-%}
{%khya (°ṇa-ākh°), as,%} m. Muñja grass. {%—Mauñjī-%}
{%dhara, as, ā, am,%} wearing or carrying Muñja grass.
{%—Mauñjī-nibandhana, am,%} n. the binding on of
the girdle of Muñja grass, ligation of the sacrificial
cord (Manu II. 27). {%—Mauñjī-pattrā,%} f. Eleusine
Indica. {%—Mauñjī-bandhana, am,%} n. the ligation or
binding on of the Muñja grass girdle, investiture
with the sacred thread, (in Manu 11. 169. shortened
on account of the metre to {%mauñji-bandhana.%})
{%—Mauñjībandhana-cihnita, as, ā, am,%} distin-
guished by the ligation of the Muñja zone (Manu
II. 170).
