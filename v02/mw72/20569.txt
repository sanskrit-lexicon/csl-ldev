{#तर्कु#}¦ {%tarku, us, us,%} m. f. (said to be fr. rt. {%kṛt,%}
with transposition of the consonants), a spindle, an
iron pin upon which the cotton is first drawn out and
which serves as the distaff, the cotton being next
transferred from it to the wheel; [cf. Gr. <lang n="greek">ἄ-τρακτος.</lang>]
{%—Tarku-piṇḍa, as,%} m. a ball of clay &c. at the
lower end of a spindle to assist in giving it a rotatory
motion. {%—Tarku-pīṭha, as, ī,%} m. f. or {%tarku-pāṭhī,%}
f. a ball at the lower end of a spindle. {%—Tarku-%}
{%lāsaka, as,%} m. a concave shell or saucer which serves
to hold the lower end of the spindle when whirled
round. {%—Tarku-śāṇa, as,%} m. a small whetstone for
sharpening spindles, &c.
