{#परिक्षित्#}¦ {%pari-kṣit%} or {%parī-kṣit%} (see rt. 2.
{%kṣi%}), Ved. spreading round or about, extending;
(‘living about or among mankind’), an epithet of
Agni; ({%au%}), du. an epithet of heaven and earth; ({%t%}),
m., N. of an ancient king, a son of Abhi-manyu and
father of Janam-ejaya; of a son of Kuru and father
of another Janam-ejaya; of a son of A-vikṣit and
brother of Janam-ejaya; of a king of A-yodhyā.
